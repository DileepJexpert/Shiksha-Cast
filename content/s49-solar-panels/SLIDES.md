# S49 — How Do Solar Panels Make Electricity? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_015.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A glowing blue solar panel on a rooftop at sunrise, golden sun rays streaming down, tiny animated light particles drifting from the sun toward the panel. Silhouette of an Indian house below. Cinematic dark sky with neon orange-blue gradient.
- **Text overlay:** "Solar Panel बिजली कैसे बनाता है?"
- **Style:** Huge bold title, neon cyan glow on the panel, Katixo Shiksha logo top-left. Spark/lightning icon as accent.

---
## Slide 2 — Sunlight = Photons
- **Visual:** A close-up beam of sunlight breaking apart into thousands of tiny glowing dots (photons), like rain droplets falling toward the bottom of the screen. Comparison inset showing rain droplets vs photons.
- **Text overlay:** "धूप = energy के packets (PHOTONS)"
- **Style:** Dark background, neon yellow photon dots with motion trails. Small labels pointing to a single photon: "1 photon = energy packet".

---
## Slide 3 — Silicon the Semiconductor
- **Visual:** A shiny silicon wafer disc center stage, with a handful of sand beside it and an arrow showing sand turning into silicon. A scale bar showing "conductor — SEMICONDUCTOR — insulator" with silicon highlighted in the middle.
- **Text overlay:** "Silicon — ना पूरा conductor, ना insulator"
- **Style:** Metallic grey wafer with neon blue rim glow. Three-zone slider graphic with the middle zone pulsing.

---
## Slide 4 — Doping: P-type and N-type
- **Visual:** Split screen. Left half: silicon block labeled N-type with extra glowing blue electron dots and a phosphorus atom icon. Right half: silicon block labeled P-type with empty circles (holes) and a boron atom icon.
- **Text overlay:** "Doping → N-type (extra electrons) + P-type (holes)"
- **Style:** Left side cool blue, right side warm orange. Bold labels N-type and P-type. Small "+" and "−" markers.

---
## Slide 5 — The P-N Junction
- **Visual:** The N-type and P-type blocks pushed together; at the seam a glowing boundary line with curved arrows showing electrons drifting and a faint field-line pattern. A subtle ramp/slope icon overlaid to suggest the electric field.
- **Text overlay:** "P-N Junction = छुपा हुआ Electric Field"
- **Style:** Junction line glowing neon green, field arrows in white. Caption "solar cell का दिल" with a small heart-pulse icon.

---
## Slide 6 — Photon Knocks Electron Free
- **Visual:** A single photon streaking in and striking the silicon lattice, an electron popping out of its spot with a burst of light. Carrom-board inset: striker hitting a coin, drawn as an analogy.
- **Text overlay:** "Photon ने Electron को आज़ाद कर दिया!"
- **Style:** Dynamic impact burst, neon yellow photon trail, electron as a bright blue spark. Motion lines for energy.

---
## Slide 7 — Electrons Flow = Current
- **Visual:** Many freed electrons all flowing in one direction along the junction, forming a stream. Side-by-side pipe-with-water analogy showing water flowing one way.
- **Text overlay:** "Electrons एक दिशा में बहे = ELECTRIC CURRENT"
- **Style:** River of blue electron dots with directional arrows. Pipe analogy in a small corner inset. Neon arrow indicating direction.

---
## Slide 8 — DC Output Through Wires
- **Visual:** A solar cell with thin metal contact lines on top and bottom, wires leading out to a glowing bulb and a charging phone, then looping back. A small "DC" badge with a straight-arrow symbol.
- **Text overlay:** "Solar Cell बनाता है DC (Direct Current)"
- **Style:** Circuit-loop diagram, neon green wire path, glowing bulb. "DC →" straight-line icon highlighted.

---
## Slide 9 — Inverter: DC to AC
- **Visual:** A sleek inverter box in the center; left input arrow labeled DC (straight line wave), right output arrow labeled AC (sine wave). Behind it, house appliances: fan, fridge, TV lighting up.
- **Text overlay:** "Inverter: DC → AC (घर के लिए)"
- **Style:** Inverter as a glowing translator box. DC straight waveform vs AC sine waveform shown clearly in neon. Appliance icons in a row.

---
## Slide 10 — Cell to Panel to Array
- **Visual:** Three-step zoom-out: a single small cell → a grid panel of ~60 cells → a large field array of many panels. Army analogy icons: soldier, squad, full army subtly behind each stage.
- **Text overlay:** "Cell → Panel (60–72 cells) → Array"
- **Style:** Progressive scale graphic left to right with size arrows. Neon blue grid pattern. Bold step labels under each stage.

---
## Slide 11 — Efficiency ~15–22%
- **Visual:** A big sun on the left sending energy; a panel converting only a slice of it into electricity, shown as a pie/bar where ~15–22% glows as "electricity" and the rest fades as "heat". 
- **Text overlay:** "Efficiency ≈ 15–22% (बाकी heat)"
- **Style:** Bar or donut chart with a glowing electricity segment and a dim heat segment. Caption "मुफ्त और clean!" with a leaf icon.

---
## Slide 12 — India's Solar Push
- **Visual:** Map of India with bright sun icons over Rajasthan and Gujarat, large solar park imagery, and small rooftop panels spreading across cities. Indian flag accent.
- **Text overlay:** "भारत: धूप की ताकत, Solar की उड़ान"
- **Style:** Map glowing with neon solar markers, photo-style solar park strip at bottom. Warm sunlit palette over the dark theme.

---

- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! — 3 सवाल — PAUSE करो!"
- **Style:** Quiz-show vibe, neon purple accents, three numbered bullets (1 photons? 2 material? 3 DC→AC device?). Pause icon bottom-right.

# S27 — How Do QR Codes Store Information? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** Phone camera scanning a glowing QR code, data beams shooting out into a website + UPI icon
- **Text overlay:** "How Do QR Codes WORK?" in bold neon-green
- **Subtext:** "Black & white squares hiding secrets"
- **Bottom bar:** Katixo Shiksha logo + "S27" badge
- **Style:** Dark background, matrix-green data rain, glowing QR

## Slide 2 — Quick Response History
- **Visual:** Old 1D barcode vs modern 2D QR code side by side; Japan flag + 1994 + Denso Wave label
- **Text overlay:** "QR = Quick Response (1994, Japan)"
- **Key note:** "1D barcode → 2D = way more data"
- **Style:** Timeline split, retro vs modern contrast, dark theme

## Slide 3 — It's All 0s and 1s
- **Visual:** Zoom into QR modules, each labeled: black = 1, white = 0, becoming a binary string
- **Text overlay:** "Every Square = 0 or 1"
- **Key note:** "Black = 1, White = 0 → binary grid"
- **Style:** Dark theme, glowing binary overlay, pixel zoom callout

## Slide 4 — Versions & Capacity
- **Visual:** Small 21×21 grid vs huge 177×177 grid; capacity meter filling up
- **Text overlay:** "Version 1 → 40 (21×21 to 177×177)"
- **Key stats:** "Up to ~7089 numbers / ~4296 letters / ~2953 bytes"
- **Style:** Size-comparison infographic, neon capacity bar, dark background

## Slide 5 — Finding Patterns
- **Visual:** QR code with the 3 corner squares glowing and rotation arrows showing any-angle scan
- **Text overlay:** "3 Corner Squares = Position Markers"
- **Key note:** "Tell phone WHERE & WHICH WAY"
- **Style:** Dark theme, neon-orange corner highlights, rotation motion arcs

## Slide 6 — Alignment & Timing
- **Visual:** QR on a curved bottle (alignment squares) + black-white ruler line between markers (timing)
- **Text overlay:** "Alignment + Timing Patterns"
- **Key note:** "Fix distortion + measure module size"
- **Style:** Dark theme, curved-surface demo, ruler graphic in cyan

## Slide 7 — Error Correction Magic
- **Visual:** Torn/dirty QR code with a logo on top, still scanning successfully with green tick
- **Text overlay:** "Reed-Solomon Error Correction"
- **Key note:** "Damaged or logo-covered → still works"
- **Style:** Dark theme, repair-glow effect, "same tech as CDs & satellites" tag

## Slide 8 — 4 Levels (L M Q H)
- **Visual:** Four bars showing 7%, 15%, 25%, 30% recovery with logo-in-center example for H
- **Text overlay:** "Levels: L 7% · M 15% · Q 25% · H 30%"
- **Key note:** "More backup = less room for data"
- **Style:** Stepped bar chart, neon gradient, dark background

## Slide 9 — Masking
- **Visual:** Before/after — clumpy black blobs vs evenly mixed pattern; 8 mask thumbnail options
- **Text overlay:** "Masking: Mix Black & White Evenly"
- **Key note:** "8 masks — generator picks the best"
- **Style:** Dark theme, checker-mix animation, magenta highlight

## Slide 10 — Full Process Flow
- **Visual:** Pipeline: text → binary → +error bits → zig-zag fill → patterns → mask → final QR
- **Text overlay:** "How a QR Is Built"
- **Steps:** "Encode → Backup bits → Fill grid → Patterns → Mask"
- **Style:** Horizontal flowchart, glowing node chain, dark theme

## Slide 11 — Safety / Quishing

- **Text overlay:** "Beware: QR Phishing (Quishing)"
- **Key note:** "Check the link & receiver name before paying"
- **Style:** Dark theme, red alert glow, scam-sticker illustration

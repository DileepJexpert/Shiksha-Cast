# S21 — How Does Bluetooth Connect Without Wires? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A teen wearing glowing wireless earbuds, with animated neon waves arcing from a smartphone to the earbuds; no cables visible.
- **Text overlay:** "Bluetooth: No Wires, Still Connected!"
- **Style:** Dark navy background, electric blue and cyan neon waves, large bold white title, Katixo Shiksha logo top-left.

---
## Slide 2 — Radio Waves Basics
- **Visual:** A phone and earbuds emitting concentric radio-wave rings; a frequency dial pointing to 2.4 GHz.
- **Text overlay:** "2.4 GHz Invisible Radio Waves"
- **Style:** Glowing wave rings in cyan, frequency label in neon yellow, dark gradient backdrop.

---
## Slide 3 — Pairing & Secret Address
- **Visual:** Two devices exchanging a glowing key icon and a unique address tag; a padlock symbol between them.
- **Text overlay:** "Pairing = Secret Handshake"
- **Style:** Two device silhouettes, neon-green key and padlock icons, dark background.

---
## Slide 4 — Frequency Hopping
- **Visual:** A bouncing signal dot jumping across a row of 79 numbered channel bars, motion trails showing speed.
- **Text overlay:** "1600 Channel Hops Per Second!"
- **Style:** Channel bars in gradient blue-to-purple, glowing orange jumping dot, dark grid background.

---
## Slide 5 — The King Behind the Name
- **Visual:** Stylized illustration of King Harald Bluetooth beside the Bluetooth logo, with the runes H and B highlighted.
- **Text overlay:** "Named After a 10th-Century King"
- **Style:** Old-parchment accent panel inside dark theme, gold rune highlights, neon blue logo.

---
## Slide 6 — Piconet: Master & Slaves
- **Visual:** One central master phone connected by glowing lines to earbuds, smartwatch, and car speaker (up to 7 nodes).
- **Text overlay:** "1 Master, Up To 7 Devices"
- **Style:** Hub-and-spoke network diagram, central node in bright cyan, spokes pulsing, dark backdrop.

---
## Slide 7 — Bluetooth Low Energy
- **Visual:** A tiny coin-cell battery powering a fitness band, with a long-life clock and small data packets flowing.
- **Text overlay:** "BLE: Months on a Coin Battery"
- **Style:** Battery icon glowing green, small packet squares in neon, dark minimal layout.

---
## Slide 8 — Is It Safe?
- **Visual:** A shield icon wrapping the connection, with encrypted scrambled code turning into readable text only for paired devices.
- **Text overlay:** "Encrypted = Only You Can Listen"
- **Style:** Neon-green shield, scrambled-to-clear text animation feel, dark secure-vibe background.

---
## Slide 9 — Beyond Earbuds

- **Text overlay:** "Everywhere: Bluetooth 5, Up to 240m"

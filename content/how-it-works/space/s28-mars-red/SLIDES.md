# S28 — Why Is Mars Red? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** Mars glowing rusty-red against a star field, with a faint comparison to a reddish "star" in night sky
- **Text overlay:** "Why Is MARS Red?" in bold rust-orange with glow
- **Subtext:** "The Red Planet's biggest secret"
- **Bottom bar:** Katixo Shiksha logo + "S28" badge
- **Style:** Deep-space dark background, neon-orange accents, dramatic planet render

## Slide 2 — It's Literally Rust
- **Visual:** Rusty bicycle/nail morphing into the Mars globe; iron oxide molecule callout
- **Text overlay:** "Mars Is Covered in RUST"
- **Key note:** "Iron oxide = same rust on old metal"
- **Style:** Dark theme, rust-texture overlay, magenta highlight on "iron oxide"

## Slide 3 — How Rust Forms
- **Visual:** Equation-style diagram: Iron + Oxygen + Water → Iron Oxide (oxidation)
- **Text overlay:** "Oxidation: Iron + Oxygen = Rust"
- **Key note:** "Same chemistry as Earth, planet-scale"
- **Style:** Clean reaction diagram, neon arrows, dark background

## Slide 4 — Mars's Wet Past
- **Visual:** Ancient Mars with blue rivers/lakes vs dry Mars today; rover finding dried riverbed
- **Text overlay:** "Billions of Years Ago: Water!"
- **Key note:** "Curiosity & Perseverance found dry riverbeds"
- **Style:** Before/after split, dark theme, cyan water vs rust dry

## Slide 5 — Dust Storms Spread It
- **Visual:** Global dust storm swirling rust-red dust across Mars; pinkish butterscotch sky
- **Text overlay:** "Dust Storms Spread the Rust"
- **Key note:** "Fine iron-oxide dust → pink Martian sky"
- **Style:** Dark theme, swirling dust effect, salmon-pink horizon

## Slide 6 — Mars Basic Facts
- **Visual:** Earth vs Mars size comparison; stat cards for day, year, temp, gravity
- **Text overlay:** "Meet Mars: The 4th Planet"
- **Key stats:** "~6779 km wide · day ~24h37m · year 687 days · -60°C · gravity 38%"
- **Style:** Infographic stat cards, neon labels, dark background

## Slide 7 — Record-Breakers
- **Visual:** Olympus Mons towering over Everest; Valles Marineris stretched over India outline; two potato-moons
- **Text overlay:** "Biggest Volcano & Canyon in the Solar System"
- **Key stats:** "Olympus Mons ~22 km · Valles Marineris ~4000 km · moons Phobos & Deimos"
- **Style:** Dramatic scale comparison, dark theme, neon-orange callouts

## Slide 8 — Thin Atmosphere
- **Visual:** Cross-section comparing thick Earth air vs thin Mars air; gas pie chart 95% CO2
- **Text overlay:** "Air: 95% CO₂, almost no Oxygen"
- **Key note:** "100× thinner than Earth → no breathing, freezing cold"
- **Style:** Dark theme, gas pie chart, cyan/red comparison

## Slide 9 — Life on Mars?
- **Visual:** Polar ice caps, possible underground water, microbe question mark; Perseverance collecting samples
- **Text overlay:** "Is There Life on Mars?"
- **Key note:** "Ice at poles + samples being collected for Earth"
- **Style:** Dark theme, glowing question mark, hopeful green accents

## Slide 10 — Humans to Mars
- **Visual:** Astronaut + spacecraft journey path Earth→Mars; challenge icons (cold, radiation, no air)
- **Text overlay:** "Can Humans Live on Mars?"
- **Key note:** "~220 million km, 7–9 month trip, huge challenges"
- **Style:** Dark theme, mission-path arc, neon challenge badges

## Slide 11 — Not All One Red
- **Visual:** Real rover photo-style Mars surface — butterscotch desert with dark/brown patches
- **Text overlay:** "Really a Rusty DESERT, Not Bright Red"
- **Key note:** "Different minerals = different shades"
- **Style:** Dark theme, realistic dusty palette, comparison to old over-red images

## Slide 12 — Quiz
- **Visual:** "Quick Brain Check!" banner with 3 numbered cards + pause icon
- **Text overlay:** "3 Questions — PAUSE & Think!"
- **Questions:** "1. Which chemical makes Mars red?  2. Main atmosphere gas + oxygen %?  3. Tallest volcano name?"
- **Style:** Game-show style, neon quiz cards, dark background

## Slide 13 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three answer cards with green checkmarks
- **Text overlay:** "Answers!"
- **Answers:** "1. Iron oxide (rust)  2. ~95% CO₂, ~0.13% oxygen  3. Olympus Mons (~22 km)"
- **Style:** Dark theme, green tick glow, clean layout

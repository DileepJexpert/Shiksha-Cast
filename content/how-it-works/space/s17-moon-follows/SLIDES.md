# S17 — Why Does the Moon Seem to Follow You — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A kid on a cycle at night on an empty road, big glowing moon hovering above, motion streaks behind the cycle.
- **Text overlay:** "Why Does the Moon Follow You?"
- **Style:** Midnight blue sky, soft glowing moon, neon title with playful glow.

---
## Slide 2 — The Moon Is Very Far
- **Visual:** A scale diagram: Earth on one side, Moon far on the other, glowing distance label between them.
- **Text overlay:** "Moon is ~384,000 km away!"
- **Style:** Dark space backdrop, dotted distance line, big bold number.

---
## Slide 3 — Finger Parallax Experiment
- **Visual:** A face with a finger close to the eye (blurred, moving fast) vs finger far away (steady), side-by-side panels.
- **Text overlay:** "Try it: near = moves a lot, far = barely moves"
- **Style:** Clean two-panel layout, cyan motion arrows on near finger.

---
## Slide 4 — Trees Rush Past, Moon Stays
- **Visual:** View from a car window: trees and poles blurring backward, moon sitting calmly in the sky.
- **Text overlay:** "Near things zoom past. The Moon doesn't."
- **Style:** Speed blur on foreground, sharp steady moon, warm streetlight glow.

---
## Slide 5 — Your Brain's Conclusion
- **Visual:** A glowing brain with a thought bubble showing the moon and an arrow saying it's coming with me.
- **Text overlay:** "Brain thinks: It's coming with me!"
- **Style:** Magenta brain glow, playful thought bubble, illusion vibe.

---
## Slide 6 — Relative Motion / Reference Points
- **Visual:** Diagram comparing moon to nearby trees as reference points, moon marked as steady.
- **Text overlay:** "It's all about reference points"
- **Style:** Schematic style, labelled steady vs moving markers, teal accents.

---
## Slide 7 — Moon's Size Keeps Our Eyes on It
- **Visual:** The bright large moon in the sky compared to tiny faint stars around it.
- **Text overlay:** "Moon is HUGE & bright — easy to track"
- **Style:** Glowing large moon, small dim star dots, contrast lighting.

---
## Slide 8 — Not Just the Moon
- **Visual:** Train window view: near fields blurring, distant mountains and sun standing still.
- **Text overlay:** "Far mountains & the Sun do it too"
- **Style:** Dynamic train-window framing, blur on near fields, steady horizon.

---
## Slide 9 — The Moon Does Move (Slowly)
- **Visual:** Moon orbiting Earth with a 27-day label, a small clock showing the slow pace.
- **Text overlay:** "Moon orbits Earth in ~27 days"
- **Style:** Orbit path glow, dark space, subtle motion arc.

---
## Slide 10 — Distance Is the Key
- **Visual:** Split image: an imaginary close moon zooming past like a tree vs the real far moon staying put.
- **Text overlay:** "Bigger distance = stronger illusion"
- **Style:** Imaginative split-screen, contrasting motion blur, bold caption.

---
## Slide 11 — Eyes vs Reality
- **Visual:** An eye with a small illusion graphic overlaid, and a science magnifier revealing the truth behind it.
- **Text overlay:** "Our eyes take shortcuts — science finds the truth"
- **Style:** Reflective eye close-up, magnifier glow, thoughtful tone.

---
## Slide 12 — Quick Brain Check
- **Visual:** Glowing brain with three question marks and a big pause icon.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! ⏸ Pause & Think — 3 Questions"
- **Style:** Bright quiz colours, pause symbol, energetic layout.

---
## Slide 13 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three glowing tick marks with short answers appearing one by one.
- **Text overlay:** "1. Parallax  2. ~384,000 km  3. Illusion"
- **Style:** Green checks, clean numbered list, celebratory glow.

## Slide 14 — Recap & Next Episode

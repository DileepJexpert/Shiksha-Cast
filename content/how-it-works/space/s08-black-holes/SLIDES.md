# S08 — What Is Inside a Black Hole — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A glowing accretion disk swirling around a pitch-black sphere, light bending around its edge (gravitational lensing).
- **Text overlay:** "BLACK HOLE — Andar Kya Hota Hai?"
- **Style:** Cinematic space, orange accretion glow vs deep black center, bold neon title.

## Slide 2 — How Black Holes Form
- **Visual:** Sequence: giant star → supernova explosion → collapse into a tiny dark point.
- **Text overlay:** "Bada taara → fuel khatam → gravitational collapse"
- **Style:** Left-to-right progression, fiery to dark gradient.

## Slide 3 — Event Horizon
- **Visual:** A black sphere with a glowing ring boundary labeled "point of no return"; a rocket and a light ray crossing in, never returning.
- **Text overlay:** "Event Horizon = point of no return | Light bhi nahi bachti"
- **Style:** Diagram with neon red boundary ring on black.

## Slide 4 — Schwarzschild Radius
- **Visual:** Earth shrunk to a marble (~9 mm) and Sun shrunk to a ~3 km ball, beside their normal sizes for scale.
- **Text overlay:** "Earth → marble (9mm) | Sun → 3km ball"
- **Style:** Scale comparison, glowing size labels.

## Slide 5 — Singularity
- **Visual:** Deep funnel of curved space-time grid plunging to an infinitely small bright point at the center.
- **Text overlay:** "Singularity = infinite density point"
- **Style:** Warped grid funnel, intense central glow, purple/blue.

## Slide 6 — Physics Breaks Down
- **Visual:** Two puzzle pieces labeled General Relativity and Quantum Mechanics that don't fit together at the singularity.
- **Text overlay:** "Yahan physics fail | Quantum gravity ki talaash"
- **Style:** Clashing puzzle motif, glitch effect, red warning glow.

## Slide 7 — Spaghettification
- **Visual:** A cartoon astronaut stretched long and thin like a noodle being pulled toward the black hole.
- **Text overlay:** "Spaghettification — tidal forces se noodle ban jaoge!"
- **Style:** Playful but eerie, stretched figure on dark.

## Slide 8 — Hawking Radiation
- **Visual:** A black hole slowly emitting faint particle sparkles at its edge, gradually shrinking.
- **Text overlay:** "Hawking Radiation (1974) | Black holes dheere-dheere evaporate"
- **Style:** Subtle quantum sparkle, soft teal glow.

## Slide 9 — Time Dilation
- **Visual:** Two clocks — one far away ticking normally, one near the horizon frozen/slowed; a figure appearing to freeze at the edge.
- **Text overlay:** "Zyaada gravity = dhima time (Einstein)"
- **Style:** Dual-clock comparison, warped time waves.

## Slide 10 — Real Black Holes
- **Visual:** The 2019 M87 black hole image (orange ring) and Milky Way center with Sagittarius A* labeled.
- **Text overlay:** "Sagittarius A* | Pehli photo 2019 (M87)"
- **Style:** Photo-realistic ring imagery on starfield.

## Slide 11 — The Story So Far
- **Visual:** Layered diagram: formation → event horizon → singularity, with time/spaghetti/Hawking icons around.
- **Text overlay:** "Collapse → Event Horizon → Singularity"
- **Style:** Summary infographic, neon-labeled layers.

## Slide 12 — Quick Brain Check
- **Visual:** Big pause icon, three numbered question bubbles, a black hole in the center.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! Video PAUSE karo — 3 sawaal"
- **Style:** Quiz-show vibe, bold yellow numbers on dark.

## Slide 13 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three checkmark cards: event horizon, singularity/general relativity, spaghettification.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: Event Horizon | Singularity | Spaghettification"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, clean answer cards.

## Slide 14 — Recap & Next Episode
- **Visual:** Recap strip of black hole anatomy; teaser card with satellites and a phone map pin for next episode.
- **Text overlay:** "Next: How Does GPS Find You? | Subscribe karna mat bhoolna!"
- **Style:** Recap on left, glowing GPS teaser on right. Katixo KhojLab logo bottom-right.

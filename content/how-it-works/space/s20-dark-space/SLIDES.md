# S20 — Why Is Space Black If There Are Billions of Stars — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A breathtaking starry night sky, billions of stars, yet vast pitch-black gaps between them.
- **Text overlay:** "Why Is Space Black If There Are Billions of Stars?"
- **Style:** Deep black space, sharp white stars, glowing neon title.

---
## Slide 2 — Olbers' Paradox
- **Visual:** A forest analogy: looking into dense trees, every line of sight hits a trunk, mirrored with stars.
- **Text overlay:** "Olbers' Paradox: shouldn't the sky be all light?"
- **Style:** Split forest/space comparison, glowing sight-lines, intriguing tone.

---
## Slide 3 — Light Speed & Time
- **Visual:** A beam of light travelling across space with a clock ticking, distant stars labelled with travel years.
- **Text overlay:** "Light is fast — but not infinite"
- **Style:** Light beam streak, glowing clock, distance/time labels.

---
## Slide 4 — Observable Universe
- **Visual:** A bubble around Earth showing the limit of how far light has reached, stars beyond it greyed out.
- **Text overlay:** "We only see the OBSERVABLE universe"
- **Style:** Glowing horizon bubble, faded distant stars, age label ~13.8 billion yrs.

---
## Slide 5 — The Universe Is Expanding
- **Visual:** Galaxies moving apart on a stretching grid, a wave being stretched longer (redshift).
- **Text overlay:** "Expanding space stretches light"
- **Style:** Stretching grid animation-style, wave elongation, Hubble nod.

---
## Slide 6 — Redshift Makes Light Invisible
- **Visual:** Visible light bar shifting toward red, then into infrared/radio, fading from the eye's range.
- **Text overlay:** "Far light → redshifted → invisible to us"
- **Style:** Spectrum shift gradient, eye-range highlight, dark backdrop.

---
## Slide 7 — No Scattering in Space
- **Visual:** Earth's blue sky (air scattering sunlight) vs empty vacuum of space with straight light rays.
- **Text overlay:** "Space = vacuum, nothing to scatter light"
- **Style:** Side-by-side day-sky vs black-space, straight ray lines.

---
## Slide 8 — Light Only If It Hits Your Eye
- **Visual:** Astronaut in space: bright Sun visible, but surrounding sky pitch black, light rays missing the eye.
- **Text overlay:** "You see light only when it reaches your eye"
- **Style:** Astronaut POV, bright Sun, deep black surroundings.

---
## Slide 9 — The CMB Glow
- **Visual:** A faint uniform microwave glow filling the whole sky, revealed by a telescope, big bang remnant.
- **Text overlay:** "Space isn't truly empty — the CMB glows!"
- **Style:** Subtle speckled glow map, telescope reveal, awe-inspiring tone.

---
## Slide 10 — The Full Answer
- **Visual:** Three labelled panels: finite age, expansion/redshift, no scattering, combining into black sky.
- **Text overlay:** "3 reasons → black sky"
- **Style:** Triptych layout, glowing labels, clean summary design.

---
## Slide 11 — The Darkness Has Meaning
- **Visual:** A person gazing at the night sky, the blackness subtly labelled as evidence of an expanding, aging universe.
- **Text overlay:** "The dark sky is proof of an expanding universe"
- **Style:** Reflective silhouette, soft cosmic glow, philosophical tone.

---
## Slide 12 — Quick Brain Check
- **Visual:** Glowing brain with three question marks and a big pause icon.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! ⏸ Pause & Think — 3 Questions"
- **Style:** Bright quiz colours, pause symbol, energetic layout.

---
## Slide 13 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three glowing tick marks with short answers appearing one by one.
- **Text overlay:** "1. Olbers' Paradox  2. Redshift  3. ~13.8 billion yrs"
- **Style:** Green checks, clean numbered list, celebratory glow.

---
## Slide 14 — Recap & Series Wrap
- **Visual:** Collage of stars, redshift wave, CMB glow icons, with a warm thank-you and curiosity teaser.
- **Text overlay:** "Stay curious! Subscribe for more!"
- **Style:** Warm recap glow, Subscribe button, Katixo KhojLab logo corner.

# S45 — How Do Planes Stay in the Air? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A giant Boeing 747 cruising above clouds, with a weight tag "~400,000 kg ≈ 100 elephants" floating beside it.
- **Text overlay:** "How Do PLANES Stay in the Air?"
- **Style:** Big neon-cyan title, dramatic sky-at-dusk backdrop, scale comparison.

## Slide 2 — Four Forces
- **Visual:** A plane in the centre with four labelled arrows — Lift (up), Weight (down), Thrust (forward), Drag (back).
- **Text overlay:** "4 Forces: Lift · Weight · Thrust · Drag"
- **Style:** Clean force-diagram, neon directional arrows, dark theme.

## Slide 3 — Wings & Airfoil Shape
- **Visual:** Cross-section of a wing (airfoil) — curved top, flatter bottom; air streamlines splitting around it.
- **Text overlay:** "Wing shape = airfoil"
- **Style:** Glowing streamlines, neon wing cutaway, labelled top/bottom.

## Slide 4 — Bernoulli's Principle
- **Visual:** Fast air over the curved top (low pressure) vs slow air below (high pressure); pressure arrows pushing wing up.
- **Text overlay:** "Fast air = low pressure → LIFT"
- **Style:** Neon pressure-zone colours, speed labels, upward push glow.

## Slide 5 — Newton's Third Law
- **Visual:** Wing tilted at an angle of attack pushing air downward; reaction arrow pushing wing upward.
- **Text overlay:** "Push air down → wing pushed UP"
- **Style:** Action/reaction arrows, neon angle-of-attack marker, dark theme.

## Slide 6 — Thrust & Jet Engines
- **Visual:** Jet engine cutaway — air sucked in, compressed, burned with fuel, blasted out the back; plane moving forward.
- **Text overlay:** "Engines blast air back → plane goes forward"
- **Style:** Glowing engine flow stages, neon exhaust, motion lines.

## Slide 7 — Speed & Runways
- **Visual:** Plane accelerating down a long runway with a speedometer hitting ~250-290 km/h at takeoff.
- **Text overlay:** "Takeoff ~250-290 km/h | cruise ~900 km/h"
- **Style:** Neon speed gauge, long runway perspective, speed-streak effect.

## Slide 8 — Control Surfaces
- **Visual:** Plane top-view with ailerons (roll), elevators (pitch), rudder (yaw) highlighted with motion arrows.
- **Text overlay:** "Ailerons · Elevators · Rudder = steering"
- **Style:** Labelled moving flaps, neon roll/pitch/yaw arcs, dark theme.

## Slide 9 — Flaps for Slow Speed
- **Visual:** Wing with flaps extended during landing, making the wing bigger/curvier; lift arrow staying strong at low speed.
- **Text overlay:** "Flaps = more lift at slow speed"
- **Style:** Animated extended flaps, neon lift arrow, landing-approach scene.

## Slide 10 — Gliding Without Engines
- **Visual:** A plane with engines off gliding gently down over a long distance; calm "physics has it" vibe.
- **Text overlay:** "No engine? Planes can GLIDE"
- **Style:** Long glide path, neon descent line, reassuring sky scene.

## Slide 11 — QUIZ
- **Visual:** Glowing brain icon with three numbered plane-themed question cards. Pause symbol.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! | 1·2·3 → PAUSE"
- **Style:** Neon-yellow energetic accents, countdown vibe, dark backdrop.

## Slide 12 — ANSWERS
- **Visual:** Three answer cards with green ticks, mini-icons echoing Slides 2, 4, 7.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: 4 Forces · Pressure Diff · Need Speed"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, clean reveal layout, dark theme.

## Slide 13 — RECAP / Series Wrap
- **Visual:** Recap montage of wing + four-force icons; a "thank you for watching" plane flying into a sunset; glowing Subscribe button.
- **Text overlay:** "More science soon! | SUBSCRIBE"
- **Style:** Bold Katixo KhojLab logo, neon Subscribe CTA, upbeat dark finish.

# S33 — How Do Noise-Cancelling Headphones Work? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** Person on a noisy bus/plane wearing headphones; chaotic noise icons fading to a calm "mute" symbol
- **Text overlay:** "How Headphones KILL Noise" in bold white + neon cyan glow
- **Bottom bar:** "Katixo KhojLab" logo + "S33" badge
- **Style:** Split chaos-to-calm, cinematic

## Slide 2 — What Is Sound
- **Visual:** Air particles oscillating, a clean sine wave with labelled crests and troughs traveling to an ear/eardrum
- **Highlight:** "crest", "trough", "eardrum"
- **Text overlay:** "Sound = a wiggling air pattern"
- **Style:** Neon blue waveform on dark grid

## Slide 3 — Destructive Interference
- **Visual:** Two waves overlaid — one's crest meeting the other's trough — combining into a flat line
- **Highlight:** "anti-phase", arrow up vs arrow down cancelling to zero
- **Text overlay:** "Wave + opposite wave = silence"
- **Style:** Two-color waves merging into flat neon line

## Slide 4 — Microphones Listen
- **Visual:** Headphone cutaway showing tiny mics catching surrounding noise; signal flowing to a chip labelled "processor"
- **Highlight:** "microphone", "processor (the detective)"
- **Text overlay:** "It listens to the noise first"
- **Style:** Tech cutaway, glowing signal path

## Slide 5 — Anti-Noise = ANC
- **Visual:** Processor generating an inverted wave (anti-noise) played from speaker, cancelling incoming noise at the ear
- **Highlight:** Big label "Active Noise Cancellation (ANC)"
- **Text overlay:** "Fight noise with anti-noise"
- **Style:** Two waves cancelling near a glowing ear

## Slide 6 — Works Best On...
- **Visual:** Comparison — steady low rumble (plane engine, AC) easily cancelled vs sudden sharp sound (shout, clap) only partly cancelled
- **Highlight:** "low-frequency steady = great", "sudden random = hard"
- **Text overlay:** "Great for engine hum, not for shouts"
- **Style:** Side-by-side waveforms, green vs amber

## Slide 7 — Passive vs Active
- **Visual:** Left: thick ear cushions physically blocking sound (passive). Right: electronic anti-noise (active). Best = both combined
- **Highlight:** "Passive = barrier", "Active = ANC"
- **Text overlay:** "Two layers of quiet"
- **Style:** Split diagram, neon dividers

## Slide 8 — Who Invented It
- **Visual:** Vintage-style plane window, engineer sketching idea; portrait-style icon of an inventor
- **Highlight:** "Amar Bose, ~1978", "first made for pilots"
- **Text overlay:** "Born from an annoying flight"
- **Style:** Retro-tech, sepia accent on dark

## Slide 9 — Myth Buster
- **Visual:** "MYTH" stamp over "ANC absorbs sound"; reveal: it ADDS an opposite sound
- **Highlight:** "Doesn't block — neutralizes by adding anti-noise"
- **Text overlay:** "Add sound to remove sound?!"
- **Style:** Bold myth-vs-fact split

## Slide 10 — Feedback System & Battery
- **Visual:** Headphone with outer mics + inner mics; loop arrow showing constant self-correction; small battery icon
- **Highlight:** "outer + inner mics", "feedback loop", "needs charging"
- **Text overlay:** "It fixes itself every moment"
- **Style:** Tech cutaway, looping neon arrows

## Slide 11 — Transparency Mode
- **Visual:** Headphone letting outside voices/traffic IN through mics; person hearing a friend while wearing them
- **Highlight:** "Opposite of ANC", "stay aware & safe"
- **Text overlay:** "Same mics, opposite job"
- **Style:** Open, bright teal accents

## Slide 12 — Quiz
- **Visual:** Big neon "Quick Brain Check!" banner + pause icon
- **Text overlay:** Three questions (cancelling rule name? / best on which noise? / the noise-catching part?)
- **Style:** Game-show style, bright cyan, pause prompt corner

## Slide 13 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three checkmark cards revealing answers
- **Text overlay:** "1) Destructive interference (anti-noise)  2) Low-frequency steady  3) Microphone"
- **Style:** Green tick reveals on dark cards

# S25 — How Do Magnets Work? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A horseshoe magnet pulling glowing iron nails through the air without touching, invisible-force lines.
- **Text overlay:** "Magnets: The Invisible Force!"
- **Style:** Dark background, neon-blue field lines, bold white title, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.

---
## Slide 2 — Atoms & Electrons
- **Visual:** Zoom into an atom showing spinning electrons drawn as tiny arrow-magnets, most pointing randomly.
- **Text overlay:** "Tiny Magnets Inside Atoms"
- **Style:** Glowing atom model, neon electron arrows, dark backdrop.

---
## Slide 3 — Magnetic Domains
- **Visual:** Two blocks: one with randomly pointing domain arrows (not magnetic), one with all arrows aligned (magnet).
- **Text overlay:** "Domains Line Up = Magnet!"
- **Style:** Side-by-side arrow grids, aligned side glowing, neon green arrows, dark theme.

---
## Slide 4 — Two Poles
- **Visual:** A bar magnet labeled N and S, with attract and repel arrows between pairs of magnets.
- **Text overlay:** "Like Repel, Unlike Attract"
- **Style:** Red N pole, blue S pole, push/pull arrows, dark background.

---
## Slide 5 — Break a Magnet
- **Visual:** A magnet snapping in two, each half instantly showing its own N and S poles.
- **Text overlay:** "Break It? Still Two Poles!"
- **Style:** Cracked magnet sequence, neon pole labels, dark dramatic backdrop.

---
## Slide 6 — Magnetic Field
- **Visual:** Iron filings on paper over a magnet forming curved field lines from N to S.
- **Text overlay:** "Invisible Field, Made Visible"
- **Style:** Realistic filing pattern in neon, curved glowing lines, dark theme.

---
## Slide 7 — Earth Is a Magnet
- **Visual:** Earth cutaway showing molten iron core, field lines wrapping the planet, a compass aligning, aurora at poles.
- **Text overlay:** "Earth = One Giant Magnet"
- **Style:** Glowing planetary field, green aurora glow, compass inset, dark space background.

---
## Slide 8 — Electromagnets
- **Visual:** A nail wrapped in wire connected to a battery becoming magnetic; crane and maglev train insets.
- **Text overlay:** "Electricity + Coil = Switchable Magnet"
- **Style:** Coil-and-battery diagram, neon current lines, crane/train insets, dark theme.

---
## Slide 9 — Magnets Everywhere
- **Visual:** Icon collage: fridge, earphones, motor/fan, hard disk, MRI machine, all linked by field lines.
- **Text overlay:** "Fridge, Speakers, Motors, MRI!"
- **Style:** Icon grid with neon connecting lines, dark gradient backdrop.

---
## Slide 10 — Recap
- **Visual:** Four mini-icons: atom, aligned domains, N-S poles, field lines.
- **Text overlay:** "Recap: Electrons → Domains → Poles → Field"
- **Style:** Four glowing badge icons, bold caption strip, dark background.

---
## Slide 11 — Quiz
- **Visual:** A glowing brain with three magnet-shaped question marks and a pause-button prompt.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! Pause & Answer (3 Qs)"
- **Style:** Neon brain, magnet question marks, large pause icon, dark quiz-show vibe.

---
## Slide 12 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three answer cards revealing: Magnetic Domains, Repel, Earth is a magnet.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers Revealed!"

# S13 — Why Does Ice Float on Water? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A glass of water with a single ice cube bobbing at the surface, half submerged, glowing neon-cyan rim light. Faint silhouettes of a sinking stone and coin in the background contrasting with the floating ice. Bubbles rising.
- **Text overlay:** "Why Does ICE FLOAT?"
- **Style:** Huge bold title in neon cyan, dark navy gradient background, small "Katixo KhojLab" logo top-left, subtle question marks scattered.

---
## Slide 2 — What is Density?
- **Visual:** A balance scale comparing a small heavy metal box vs a big light foam box. A clean formula card glowing on screen. A measuring cube showing volume.
- **Text overlay:** "DENSITY = MASS ÷ VOLUME"
- **Style:** Formula in bright neon green, water density "≈ 1.0 g/cm³" highlighted in a callout box.

---
## Slide 3 — Water is Weird
- **Visual:** Split screen. Left: molten wax/iron solidifying and SINKING (red down-arrow). Right: water freezing and RISING (cyan up-arrow). A big "vs" in the middle.
- **Text overlay:** "Most solids SINK... water FLOATS!"
- **Style:** "Anomalous Expansion of Water" banner across bottom in neon orange, dramatic contrast lighting.

---
## Slide 4 — The Water Molecule
- **Visual:** A large 3D water molecule: one red oxygen atom, two white hydrogen atoms in a bent V-shape. Charge labels: slight "+" near hydrogens, slight "−" near oxygen.
- **Text overlay:** "H₂O — Bent & POLAR"
- **Style:** Glowing atom spheres, dotted axis lines showing the bend angle, dark background with grid.

---
## Slide 5 — Hydrogen Bonds
- **Visual:** Several water molecules with dotted lines connecting the positive hydrogen of one to the negative oxygen of another. Tiny magnet icons hint at attraction.
- **Text overlay:** "Hydrogen Bonds = molecule magnets"
- **Style:** Dotted bond lines glowing yellow, molecules in cyan/red, soft pulse-glow effect on the bonds.

---
## Slide 6 — Liquid Water (Packed)
- **Visual:** A crowded cluster of water molecules tightly packed, jiggling, motion-blur arrows showing constant movement. Like a packed bus of molecules.
- **Text overlay:** "LIQUID: packed close = HIGH density"
- **Style:** Dense crowded look, warm motion trails, "tightly packed" callout, density meter showing high.

---
## Slide 7 — Freezing Below 0°C
- **Visual:** Thermometer dropping below 0°C. Molecules slowing down and locking into fixed positions, hydrogen bonds turning solid/rigid.
- **Text overlay:** "Below 0°C → bonds LOCK"
- **Style:** Cool blue palette, frost crystals forming at edges, thermometer with neon red mercury falling.

---
## Slide 8 — The Open Hexagonal Lattice
- **Visual:** A beautiful hexagonal ice crystal lattice (honeycomb-like) with clearly visible EMPTY gaps between molecules. Compare side note to the crowded liquid.
- **Text overlay:** "ICE = open hexagon + EMPTY space"
- **Style:** Geometric neon-cyan lattice glowing, empty gaps highlighted with subtle orange outlines, snowflake motif.

---
## Slide 9 — Less Dense = Floats
- **Visual:** Two cubes side by side: liquid water cube "1.0 g/cm³" and ice cube "0.92 g/cm³". Ice cube floating on water surface with the formula recalled.
- **Text overlay:** "Ice 0.92 < Water 1.0 → FLOATS!"
- **Style:** Bold numbers, "9% lighter" badge in neon green, up-arrow on the ice cube.

---
## Slide 10 — Densest at 4°C
- **Visual:** A vertical temperature gauge/graph showing water density peaking at 4°C, then dropping above and below. Layered tank: 4°C water sinks to bottom, ice on top.
- **Text overlay:** "Water is DENSEST at 4°C"
- **Style:** Line graph with glowing peak marker at 4°C, layered water tank diagram, neon orange highlight on the peak.

---
## Slide 11 — Why It Saves Life
- **Visual:** Cross-section of a frozen lake: ice sheet on top (like a blanket/quilt), liquid water below, fish and a frog swimming alive. Snow falling above.
- **Text overlay:** "Ice = blanket → fish survive winter"
- **Style:** Cozy contrast — cold above, safe blue water below, fish glowing, quilt-pattern hint on the ice layer.

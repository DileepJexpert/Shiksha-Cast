# S23 — How Do Rainbows Form? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A vivid rainbow arcing over rain clouds after a storm, a teen looking up in awe.
- **Text overlay:** "Where Do Rainbow Colours Come From?"
- **Style:** Dark stormy backdrop, glowing rainbow arc, bold white title, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.

---
## Slide 2 — White Light Is Not White
- **Visual:** A beam of white sunlight labeled, hiding seven colours inside, Newton portrait accent.
- **Text overlay:** "Sunlight = 7 Hidden Colours"
- **Style:** White beam splitting hint, neon colour streaks, dark background, small Newton badge.

---
## Slide 3 — Refraction
- **Visual:** A light ray bending as it passes from air into water, with each colour bending a different amount.
- **Text overlay:** "Refraction: Light Bends in Water"
- **Style:** Air-water boundary line, fanning coloured rays, violet bending most, dark theme.

---
## Slide 4 — Inside a Raindrop
- **Visual:** A large raindrop cross-section showing refraction in, reflection at the back, refraction out, colours fanning.
- **Text overlay:** "Each Drop = Tiny Prism"
- **Style:** Glowing droplet diagram with three labeled steps, neon ray paths, dark backdrop.

---
## Slide 5 — Sun Behind You
- **Visual:** A figure with the sun behind and rain in front, dotted lines showing the viewing angle to the rainbow.
- **Text overlay:** "Sun Behind, Rain in Front"
- **Style:** Side-view diagram, glowing angle lines, warm sun glow, dark theme.

---
## Slide 6 — VIBGYOR Order
- **Visual:** A clean rainbow band labeled top-to-bottom Red to Violet with the word VIBGYOR.
- **Text overlay:** "Always in Order: VIBGYOR"
- **Style:** Crisp colour bands, bold acronym, neon labels, dark background.

---
## Slide 7 — Double Rainbow
- **Visual:** Two stacked rainbows, the outer one fainter with reversed colours, dark Alexander's band between.
- **Text overlay:** "Double Rainbow = Reversed Colours"
- **Style:** Two arcs with reversed gradients, dim band highlighted, dark dramatic sky.

---
## Slide 8 — Rainbows Are Circles
- **Visual:** A full circular rainbow seen from a plane window, with ground cutting off the lower half on a second view.
- **Text overlay:** "It's Actually a Full Circle!"
- **Style:** Split view: arc on ground vs full circle from above, neon ring, dark theme.

---
## Slide 9 — Make Your Own
- **Visual:** A kid spraying a water hose mist with sun behind, a small rainbow appearing; CD reflecting colours inset.
- **Text overlay:** "Try It: Hose, Glass, or CD!"
- **Style:** Sunny DIY scene, neon mist sparkle, CD rainbow inset, dark border frame.

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A soda bottle being opened mid-action, bubbles and foam exploding out with a big "CSSS!" sound graphic.
- **Text overlay:** "Why Does Soda FIZZ?"
- **Style:** Dark background, neon bubbles, dynamic motion blur, bold title, fizzy energetic vibe.

## Slide 2 — CO2 the bubble gas
- **Visual:** A soda glass with bubbles, each bubble labeled "CO2", molecule diagram inset.
- **Text overlay:** "Bubbles = Carbon Dioxide (CO2)"
- **Style:** Neon blue bubbles, CO2 molecule (1 carbon + 2 oxygen) glowing, label "carbonation".

## Slide 3 — Pressure dissolves gas
- **Visual:** A factory machine forcing CO2 into liquid under high pressure, gauge needle in the red zone, cold/ice icons.
- **Text overlay:** "High PRESSURE + COLD = gas dissolves in"
- **Style:** Industrial look, neon pressure gauge, frost effects, Henry's Law label.

---
## Slide 4 — Hidden gas in sealed bottle
- **Visual:** A calm sealed bottle outside, but X-ray view inside shows CO2 molecules packed and waiting.
- **Text overlay:** "Sealed bottle = gas HIDDEN under pressure"
- **Style:** Split calm-exterior vs busy-interior, glowing dissolved gas dots, dark teal.

---
## Slide 5 — Opening drops pressure
- **Visual:** Cap popping off, pressure gauge needle dropping fast, CO2 escaping as rising bubbles.
- **Text overlay:** "Open it → pressure DROPS → gas escapes!"
- **Style:** Action shot, "CSSS" graphic, falling gauge, neon bubble trails rising.

---
## Slide 6 — Nucleation sites
- **Visual:** Close-up of a glass surface with a scratch and dust speck, bubbles forming exactly there in a rising line.
- **Text overlay:** "Bubbles are born at NUCLEATION SITES"
- **Style:** Macro view, glowing rough spots, neon bubble streams from specific points.

---
## Slide 7 — Mentos + Diet Coke

- **Text overlay:** "Mentos = THOUSANDS of nucleation sites → FOUNTAIN!"

# S51 — Why Does Bread Rise? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** Split image — a heavy lump of dough on left vs a fluffy sliced bread loaf on right, with a glowing neon arrow between them. Tiny balloon icons floating inside the bread holes.
- **Text overlay:** "Bread फूलता कैसे है?"
- **Style:** Big bold title, neon cyan and warm gold accents, dark charcoal background.

## Slide 2 — Meet the Yeast
- **Visual:** Microscope-style view of round yeast cells, one zoomed cell labelled "Saccharomyces cerevisiae". Counter graphic showing "20 billion / gram".
- **Text overlay:** "Yeast = जीवित Fungus"
- **Style:** Green-blue microbe glow, lab vibe.

## Slide 3 — Fermentation
- **Visual:** Yeast cell eating a glucose molecule (C6H12O6) and emitting bubbles labelled CO2 + a small alcohol droplet. Equation arrow.
- **Text overlay:** "Glucose → CO2 + Alcohol"
- **Style:** Animated-looking molecular diagram, neon orange energy burst.

## Slide 4 — Gluten Network
- **Visual:** Stretchy elastic web/mesh forming from two proteins (glutenin + gliadin) joining hands, hands kneading dough.
- **Text overlay:** "Gluten = Stretchy जाल"
- **Style:** Rubber-band stretch motif, magenta accent lines.

## Slide 5 — The Rise
- **Visual:** Dough balloon inflating in a bowl, before-and-after sizes, CO2 bubbles trapped in mesh.
- **Text overlay:** "Dough का Rise (Proofing)"
- **Style:** Time-lapse arrow, soft warm lighting.

## Slide 6 — Temperature Matters
- **Visual:** Thermometer with three zones — cold (slow yeast), 25-35°C (happy yeast, smiley), 50°C+ (dead yeast skull).
- **Text overlay:** "Perfect गर्माहट: 25–35°C"
- **Style:** Gradient cold-blue to hot-red thermometer.

## Slide 7 — Oven Spring
- **Visual:** Bread puffing up dramatically inside a glowing oven, sudden expansion lines, alcohol vapour escaping.
- **Text overlay:** "Oven Spring — आखिरी Push!"
- **Style:** Hot oven glow, dynamic motion lines.

## Slide 8 — Maillard Reaction
- **Visual:** Golden-brown crust forming, amino acid + sugar molecules shaking hands, aroma swirls rising.
- **Text overlay:** "सुनहरी Crust = Maillard Reaction"
- **Style:** Warm golden gradient, appetising glow.

## Slide 9 — No Yeast? Baking Soda!
- **Visual:** Baking soda box + lemon, instant CO2 fizz, pancake/cake/naan icons.
- **Text overlay:** "Chemical Leavening = Instant Gas"
- **Style:** Side-by-side comparison, fizzy bubbles.

## Slide 10 — Fermentation Everywhere
- **Visual:** Collage of idli, dosa, dahi, bhatura, cheese, chocolate, with tiny microbe icons. Ancient Egypt bread illustration corner.
- **Text overlay:** "Fermentation हर जगह!"
- **Style:** Food collage on dark grid, friendly icons.

## Slide 11 — Recap
- **Visual:** Flow diagram: Yeast → Fermentation → CO2 → Gluten traps → Rise → Oven set → Crust.
- **Text overlay:** "पूरी कहानी एक नजर में"
- **Style:** Clean numbered flowchart, neon connectors.

## Slide 12 — Quiz
- **Visual:** Big glowing question mark, three numbered question cards, pause icon.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! ⏸"
- **Style:** Quiz-show vibe, electric purple.

## Slide 13 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three answer cards revealed with green ticks: CO2, Gluten, Maillard Reaction.
- **Text overlay:** "जवाब: CO2 • Gluten • Maillard"
- **Style:** Green correct-answer glow.

## Slide 14 — Recap & Wrap-up
- **Visual:** Happy bread slice mascot, Subscribe button glowing, Katixo KhojLab logo.
- **Text overlay:** "Subscribe करना मत भूलना!"
- **Style:** Celebratory, channel branding, call-to-action bright.

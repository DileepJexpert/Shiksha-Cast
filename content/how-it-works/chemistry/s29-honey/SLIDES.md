## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** Golden honey dripping from a dipper, with an Egyptian pyramid + ancient sealed honey jar inset
- **Text overlay:** "Why Honey NEVER Spoils" in bold gold with glow
- **Subtext:** "3000-year-old honey is still edible!"
- **Bottom bar:** Katixo KhojLab logo + "S29" badge
- **Style:** Dark background, warm gold accents, glowing honey drip

## Slide 2 — Why Food Rots
- **Visual:** Microbes (bacteria/fungi) feasting on bread/milk; three need-icons: water, temperature, food
- **Text overlay:** "Food Spoils = Microbes Grow"
- **Key note:** "Remove water/temp/food → no spoilage"
- **Style:** Dark theme, magenta microbe glow, three-need infographic

## Slide 3 — Weapon 1: Very Little Water
- **Visual:** Honey jar labeled 17-18% water, 80% sugar; dry desert metaphor for thirsty bacteria
- **Text overlay:** "Weapon 1: Almost No Water"
- **Key note:** "~17–18% water, ~80% sugar = bacteria desert"
- **Style:** Dark theme, gold meter bars, dried-out microbe icon

## Slide 4 — Weapon 2: Osmosis
- **Visual:** Bacterium in honey with water arrows being pulled OUT, cell shriveling
- **Text overlay:** "Weapon 2: Osmosis Sucks Them Dry"
- **Key note:** "Hygroscopic: sugar pulls water out of microbes"
- **Style:** Dark theme, cyan water-flow arrows, shrinking-cell animation

## Slide 5 — Weapon 3: Acidity
- **Visual:** pH scale highlighting honey at 3.2–4.5 vs bacteria-happy 6–7 zone
- **Text overlay:** "Weapon 3: Acidic (pH 3.2–4.5)"
- **Key note:** "Too sour for most bacteria (gluconic acid)"
- **Style:** Dark theme, neon pH gradient bar, sour-face microbe

## Slide 6 — Weapon 4: Hydrogen Peroxide
- **Visual:** Bee adding glucose oxidase enzyme into nectar; tiny H₂O₂ antiseptic sparkles
- **Text overlay:** "Weapon 4: Natural Hydrogen Peroxide"
- **Key note:** "Enzyme glucose oxidase = built-in antiseptic"
- **Style:** Dark theme, glowing bee + enzyme, green antiseptic sparkle

## Slide 7 — How Bees Make It
- **Visual:** Flow: nectar → bee's honey stomach (enzymes) → comb → wing-fanning dries water → wax-sealed cell
- **Text overlay:** "How Bees Craft Honey"
- **Key note:** "Enzymes + fan-drying + wax seal"
- **Style:** Dark theme, honeycomb flow diagram, gold node chain

## Slide 8 — Store It Right
- **Visual:** Good vs bad jar — sealed dry spoon vs open lid soaking moisture, fermentation bubbles
- **Text overlay:** "But Only If Stored Right!"
- **Key note:** "Keep dry, sealed, clean spoon — moisture = enemy"
- **Style:** Dark theme, red warning on wet jar, green tick on sealed jar

## Slide 9 — Crystallization Myth
- **Visual:** Grainy crystallized honey jar with a "NOT SPOILED" badge; warm-water rescue demo
- **Text overlay:** "Crystallized ≠ Spoiled!"
- **Key note:** "Sign of pure honey — warm water restores it"
- **Style:** Dark theme, crystal-texture closeup, reassuring green badge

## Slide 10 — Honey's Superpowers
- **Visual:** Medical Manuka honey on a wound + cough-relief icon; bold red baby warning
- **Text overlay:** "Healing Power (But NOT for Babies!)"
- **Key note:** "Wound care + cough relief; never under age 1"

## Slide 11 — All 4 Weapons Together

- **Text overlay:** "4 Defenses = Immortal Food"
- **Key note:** "No microbe can survive the combo"

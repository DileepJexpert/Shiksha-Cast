# S41 — Why Can You See Through Glass? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** Split screen — left a solid wooden door (opaque), right a clear glass window showing a bright outdoor scene. Glowing question mark between them.
- **Text overlay:** "Why Can You See THROUGH Glass?"
- **Style:** Big bold neon-cyan title, dark navy background, dramatic spotlight glow.

## Slide 2 — What Is Light?
- **Visual:** A beam of white light splitting into a rainbow spectrum; wavelength scale labelled 380nm (violet) to 700nm (red).
- **Text overlay:** "Light = Energy Wave | Absorb · Reflect · Transmit"
- **Style:** Neon rainbow gradient on dark backdrop, three glowing icons for the three outcomes.

## Slide 3 — Electrons & Energy Levels
- **Visual:** A ladder/staircase diagram with an electron jumping up a rung when hit by a matching light photon.
- **Text overlay:** "Electrons jump ONLY with the exact energy"
- **Style:** Glowing electron orbs, neon-green ladder rungs, dark theme.

## Slide 4 — Why Glass Is Transparent
- **Visual:** Silicon dioxide (SiO2) molecular structure; a huge labelled energy gap that visible light arrows cannot cross, so they pass straight through.
- **Text overlay:** "Big energy gap → light passes through"
- **Style:** Cyan light arrows shooting through cleanly, big "GAP" callout, dark blue.

## Slide 5 — Why Wood Is Opaque
- **Visual:** Light arrows hitting wood, getting absorbed (turning into little heat waves) and partly reflecting back as colour.
- **Text overlay:** "Opaque = light absorbed, can't pass"
- **Style:** Warm orange absorption glow vs blocked path, contrast with Slide 4.

## Slide 6 — Glass Blocks UV
- **Visual:** Sun emitting visible + UV + IR rays toward a car window; visible passes, UV gets blocked at the glass.
- **Text overlay:** "Glass = clear to visible, blocks most UV"
- **Style:** Purple UV rays stopped at glass, sunny outdoor scene, neon labels.

## Slide 7 — Reflection at Night
- **Visual:** Night window scene — person inside sees own faint reflection; small "~4%" reflected ray highlighted.
- **Text overlay:** "Why glass = mirror at night"
- **Style:** Moody dark room glow, faint ghost reflection, neon "4%" tag.

## Slide 8 — Refraction / Bending
- **Visual:** Light ray bending as it enters glass; speed labels 300,000 km/s (air) vs 200,000 km/s (glass). Small lens icons.
- **Text overlay:** "Light slows → bends = Refraction"
- **Style:** Glowing bent beam, speed gauges, lens/glasses icons in neon.

## Slide 9 — Amorphous Solid
- **Visual:** Comparison — neat crystal grid (salt) vs random jumbled atoms (glass, like frozen liquid); clean light passing through glass.
- **Text overlay:** "Glass = frozen liquid (amorphous)"
- **Style:** Two atom-lattice panels, neon outlines, clarity arrows.

## Slide 10 — Frosted / Translucent
- **Visual:** Rough frosted glass scattering light arrows in many directions; blurry silhouette behind it.
- **Text overlay:** "Rough surface → scatters light = blurry"
- **Style:** Diffused glow, scattered arrows, bathroom-glass visual, dark theme.

## Slide 11 — QUIZ
- **Visual:** Big glowing brain icon with three numbered question cards. Pause symbol.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! | 1·2·3 → PAUSE"
- **Style:** Energetic neon-yellow accents, countdown vibe, dark backdrop.

## Slide 12 — ANSWERS
- **Visual:** Three answer cards with green ticks, mini-icons echoing Slides 4, 7, 10.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: Energy Gap · 4% Reflection · Scattering"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, satisfying reveal layout, dark theme.

## Slide 13 — RECAP / Next Episode
- **Visual:** Recap montage of glass + light icons; teaser thumbnail of a camera for next episode; Subscribe button glowing.
- **Text overlay:** "Next: How a Camera Captures a Photo! | SUBSCRIBE"
- **Style:** Bold Katixo KhojLab logo, neon Subscribe CTA, upbeat dark finish.

# S40 — Why Do We Sneeze? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A dramatic freeze-frame of a person mid-sneeze with a burst of glowing droplets, big "ACHOO!" graphic.
- **Text overlay:** "Why Do We SNEEZE?"
- **Style:** Dark background, neon spray burst, motion energy, bold title, speed label "150 km/h!".

---
## Slide 2 — Sneeze is a reflex
- **Visual:** A hand pulling back from a hot object next to a sneeze, both labeled "automatic reflex".
- **Text overlay:** "A sneeze = an automatic REFLEX (a clean-up tool)"
- **Style:** Side-by-side reflex comparison, neon arrows, friendly icons, dark teal.

---
## Slide 3 — Sensors in the nose
- **Visual:** Cutaway of a nose interior with glowing sensors detecting incoming dust, pollen, pepper, germs.
- **Text overlay:** "Nose SENSORS detect irritation → alarm!"
- **Style:** Macro nose cross-section, neon sensor dots lighting up, irritant icons, alarm glow.

---
## Slide 4 — Sneeze center in brain
- **Visual:** A brain with the brainstem highlighted, acting like a conductor signaling muscles.
- **Text overlay:** "The SNEEZE CENTER in the brainstem takes charge"
- **Style:** Glowing brain diagram, conductor metaphor, neon signal lines to muscles, dark.

---
## Slide 5 — Building the blast
- **Visual:** Sequence: deep breath in (lungs fill) → eyes shut + throat closes → pressure builds → release.
- **Text overlay:** "Deep breath → pressure builds → BLAST out!"
- **Style:** Step sequence panels, pressure gauge rising, neon build-up to explosion.

---
## Slide 6 — The speed & droplets
- **Visual:** A sneeze blasting droplets several meters, speedometer at 150 km/h, droplet cloud spreading.
- **Text overlay:** "Up to 150 km/h — cover your mouth!"
- **Style:** Speedometer graphic, neon droplet spray, hygiene reminder icon (elbow cover).

---
## Slide 7 — Photic sneeze reflex
- **Visual:** A person stepping into bright sunlight and sneezing, with eye and nose nerves shown close in the brain.
- **Text overlay:** "Sunlight makes 1 in 4 people sneeze! (Photic reflex)"
- **Style:** Sun rays, glowing crossed nerves, "genetic / runs in families" note, bright vs dark contrast.

---
## Slide 8 — Don't hold it in
- **Visual:** A warning graphic of someone pinching their nose to hold a sneeze, red danger to ears/eyes.
- **Text overlay:** "NEVER force-hold a sneeze — it can hurt you"
- **Style:** Caution red, warning triangle, ear/eardrum risk icons, safe-way (tissue/elbow) checkmark.

---
## Slide 9 — Fun facts
- **Visual:** Icon collage: eyes auto-close (myth busted), no sneezing while asleep, animals sneeze too, pepper/piperine.
- **Text overlay:** "Fun facts: eyes shut, animals sneeze too, pepper = piperine"
- **Style:** Playful icon grid, neon edges, myth-bust stamps, light fun tone.

---
## Slide 10 — Full recap chain
- **Visual:** Flow diagram: irritant → nose sensors → sneeze center → muscles → pressure → blast clears nose.
- **Text overlay:** "Irritant → sensors → brain → blast → clean nose!"
- **Style:** Glowing flow arrows, mini icons per step, dark background, clean summary.

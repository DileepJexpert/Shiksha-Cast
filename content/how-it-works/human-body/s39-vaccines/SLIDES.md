# S39 — How Do Vaccines Train Your Body? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A glowing syringe over a stylized human body where tiny soldier-like cells line up for a "mock drill" battle.
- **Text overlay:** "How Do VACCINES Train Your Body?"
- **Style:** Dark medical theme, neon teal and green glow, bold title, heroic mood.

---
## Slide 2 — The immune system army
- **Visual:** White blood cells drawn as soldiers patrolling a bloodstream highway, fighting invading germs.
- **Text overlay:** "Your IMMUNE SYSTEM = a 24/7 army"
- **Style:** Neon red blood vessels, glowing white cell soldiers, dark interior body look.

---
## Slide 3 — Antigens & antibodies
- **Visual:** A germ with marked surface tags (antigens), and Y-shaped antibodies locking onto them like a key in a lock.
- **Text overlay:** "Antigens = ID tags | Antibodies = the weapons"
- **Style:** Glowing Y-shaped antibodies, color-coded antigen tags, lock-and-key visual metaphor.

---
## Slide 4 — Slow first battle
- **Visual:** A clock ticking while germs multiply faster than a small unprepared army can respond.
- **Text overlay:** "First attack = SLOW response (days to react)"
- **Style:** Urgency red tones, multiplying germs, ticking clock, outnumbered cells.

---
## Slide 5 — Immune memory
- **Visual:** Special "memory cells" holding a wanted-poster of the defeated germ, filing it away forever.
- **Text overlay:** "MEMORY CELLS remember the enemy forever"
- **Style:** Glowing memory cells, wanted-poster motif, neon archive vibe, the key concept highlighted.

---
## Slide 6 — What a vaccine does
- **Visual:** A syringe injecting a harmless "trailer" version of a germ; body builds antibodies + memory cells, no illness.
- **Text overlay:** "Vaccine = a safe TRAILER of the real disease"
- **Style:** Movie-trailer metaphor, neon safe-germ, training montage feel, no-illness checkmark.

---
## Slide 7 — Types of vaccines
- **Visual:** Four panels: weakened live germ, killed germ, protein piece, and mRNA recipe card.
- **Text overlay:** "Many types — even mRNA (does NOT change your DNA)"
- **Style:** Four icon panels with neon labels, mRNA recipe card glowing, myth-bust note.

---
## Slide 8 — Herd immunity
- **Visual:** A crowd where most people are shielded (glowing), blocking the germ from reaching a vulnerable baby in the center.
- **Text overlay:** "HERD IMMUNITY protects everyone"
- **Style:** Crowd of glowing shields forming a wall, vulnerable person safe inside, neon green protection.

---
## Slide 9 — Vaccines changed history
- **Visual:** Timeline: 1796 Jenner smallpox, polio eradication, lives-saved counter; a small "sore arm = training" note.
- **Text overlay:** "Smallpox ERADICATED 1980 — millions of lives saved"
- **Style:** Historical timeline ribbon, neon milestones, big lives-saved number, friendly side-effect note.

---
## Slide 10 — Full recap chain
- **Visual:** Flow diagram: antigen → antibody → memory cells → vaccine training → fast real defense → herd immunity.
- **Text overlay:** "Recognize → remember → train → defend → protect all!"
- **Style:** Glowing flow arrows, mini icons per step, dark background, clean summary.

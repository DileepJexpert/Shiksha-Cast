# S18 — Why Is Everyone's Fingerprint Unique — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** Extreme close-up of a glowing fingertip with neon ridge lines, scattered faint fingerprints in the background.
- **Text overlay:** "Why Is Everyone's Fingerprint Unique?"
- **Style:** Dark background, electric blue glowing ridges, bold neon title.

---
## Slide 2 — How Ridges Form
- **Visual:** Illustration of a fetus at ~10–16 weeks with a zoom into skin layers, inner layer growing faster and folding.
- **Text overlay:** "Ridges form in the womb (10–16 weeks)"
- **Style:** Soft warm tones, cross-section skin diagram, labelled layers.

---
## Slide 3 — Genes Plus Randomness
- **Visual:** A DNA helix on one side, swirling random arrows (fluid, movement) on the other, merging into a fingerprint.
- **Text overlay:** "Genes + random womb factors"
- **Style:** Purple DNA glow, chaotic motion arrows, fingerprint at the merge point.

---
## Slide 4 — Even Twins Differ
- **Visual:** Two identical twin faces with two clearly different fingerprints magnified beside them.
- **Text overlay:** "Same DNA. Different fingerprints!"
- **Style:** Split symmetry, contrasting fingerprint close-ups, surprise vibe.

---
## Slide 5 — Three Pattern Types
- **Visual:** Three labelled fingerprint diagrams: Loop, Whorl, Arch, side by side.
- **Text overlay:** "Loop (~60%) · Whorl · Arch"
- **Style:** Clean trio layout, neon outline patterns, clear labels.

---
## Slide 6 — Minutiae Points
- **Visual:** A zoomed fingerprint with marked points: a ridge ending and a bifurcation highlighted.
- **Text overlay:** "Tiny details = Minutiae"
- **Style:** Magnified ridge view, glowing red markers, technical look.

---
## Slide 7 — Why Ridges Exist (Grip & Touch)
- **Visual:** Split image: a fingertip gripping a glass next to a tyre tread on a road, parallel grooves.
- **Text overlay:** "Ridges = grip + sense of touch"
- **Style:** Comparison panels, grip arrows, tyre-tread analogy graphic.

---
## Slide 8 — History & India Connection
- **Visual:** Vintage thumbprint on an old document, portraits-style nod to Azizul Haque and Hem Chandra Bose, Calcutta label.
- **Text overlay:** "India helped build fingerprint science"
- **Style:** Sepia-meets-neon, old document texture, historical tone.

---
## Slide 9 — Fingerprints in Tech
- **Visual:** A phone fingerprint scanner glowing, ridges turning into a digital code stream.
- **Text overlay:** "Your phone stores a CODE, not a photo"
- **Style:** Sleek device glow, green code stream, secure tech vibe.

---
## Slide 10 — Surprising Facts (Koalas!)
- **Visual:** A koala fingertip next to a human fingertip, almost identical ridge patterns under a magnifier.
- **Text overlay:** "Even koalas have fingerprints!"
- **Style:** Playful contrast, magnifier glow, fun-fact framing.

---
## Slide 11 — You Are One of a Kind
- **Visual:** A single bright fingerprint glowing among billions of faded ones, standing out.
- **Text overlay:** "You are unique — to your fingertips!"
- **Style:** One highlighted print, soft crowd of faded prints, uplifting glow.

---
## Slide 12 — Quick Brain Check

- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! ⏸ Pause & Think — 3 Questions"

## Slide 13 — Answers

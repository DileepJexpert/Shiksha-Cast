# S55 — Why Do We Age? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_015.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A glowing human silhouette aging from a baby to an old person across the frame, with floating neon cells drifting around. Deep navy-to-black gradient background.
- **Text overlay:** "WHY DO WE AGE?"
- **Style:** Massive bold title in electric cyan, subtle Katixo Shiksha logo top-left, pulsing particle glow.

---
## Slide 2 — Body Made of Cells
- **Visual:** A human body outline filled with millions of tiny glowing dots, zooming into a cluster of stylized cells like building blocks.
- **Text overlay:** "37 TRILLION CELLS"
- **Style:** Neon green cell clusters, large number highlighted in yellow, dark grid backdrop.

---
## Slide 3 — What Is Aging?
- **Visual:** Split timeline showing a young vibrant cell on the left and a slower dim cell on the right, with a downward arrow labeled efficiency.
- **Text overlay:** "AGING = SLOW DECLINE"
- **Style:** Gradient red-to-blue divider, bold white caption, soft glow on the declining side.

---
## Slide 4 — Telomeres (Shoelace Analogy)
- **Visual:** A chromosome shaped like an X with glowing caps at its ends, placed next to a shoelace whose plastic tip (aglet) is highlighted with a matching glow.
- **Text overlay:** "TELOMERES = SHOELACE TIPS"
- **Style:** Magenta caps, side-by-side comparison, dotted connector line in neon.

---
## Slide 5 — Telomeres Shorten
- **Visual:** A sequence of a cell dividing 4 times, each copy showing visibly shorter colored telomere caps, like a shrinking countdown bar.
- **Text overlay:** "EACH DIVISION = SHORTER"
- **Style:** Progress-bar style row, color fading from green to orange, ticking clock icon in corner.

---
## Slide 6 — Hayflick Limit / Senescence
- **Visual:** A cell hitting a glowing STOP barrier after a counter reads about 50, with a small portrait nod to scientist Leonard Hayflick.
- **Text overlay:** "HAYFLICK LIMIT ~50"
- **Style:** Red stop-sign accents, bold counter in neon, dark spotlight on the frozen cell.

---
## Slide 7 — DNA Damage
- **Visual:** A DNA double helix with a few rungs glitching or broken, hit by incoming UV rays and pollution particles from the side.
- **Text overlay:** "ERRORS PILE UP"
- **Style:** Glitch-effect helix in cyan, hazard-yellow incoming rays, gritty dark texture.

---
## Slide 8 — Free Radicals / Antioxidants
- **Visual:** Angry spiky red free-radical molecules attacking a cell, with calm blue antioxidant orbs (berries, green tea) neutralizing them.
- **Text overlay:** "FREE RADICALS vs ANTIOXIDANTS"
- **Style:** Rust-orange vs cool-blue contrast, shield glow around protected cell.

---
## Slide 9 — Collagen, Elastin, Wrinkles
- **Visual:** Cross-section of young skin with dense springy collagen fibers vs aged skin with sparse broken fibers and surface wrinkles.
- **Text overlay:** "COLLAGEN BREAKS DOWN"
- **Style:** Side-by-side skin layers, spring-coil motif, soft pink-to-grey gradient.

---
## Slide 10 — Mitochondria & Zombie Cells
- **Visual:** A glowing mitochondrion shaped like a power plant fading dim, surrounded by greyish slow zombie cells with droopy faces.
- **Text overlay:** "POWER PLANTS SLOW DOWN"
- **Style:** Dying-battery icon, electric-blue energy fading to grey, eerie green zombie-cell glow.

---
## Slide 11 — Visible Signs Connected
- **Visual:** A central old-person icon with four arrows pointing to wrinkles, grey hair, weak bones, and slow-healing wound, each with a tiny cell cause.
- **Text overlay:** "WHY WE SEE AGING"
- **Style:** Infographic spokes layout, neon labels, clean dark canvas with connecting lines.

---
## Slide 12 — Animals Age Differently
- **Visual:** A lineup of a mouse, a giant tortoise, and the immortal jellyfish Turritopsis, each with its lifespan label glowing above.
- **Text overlay:** "TORTOISE 150+ | IMMORTAL JELLYFISH"
- **Style:** Trading-card style portraits, vibrant per-animal accent colors, wow-factor sparkle on the jellyfish.

---
## Slide 13 — Quiz
- **Visual:** A glowing brain with a pause icon in the center and three numbered question bubbles floating around it.
- **Text overlay:** "QUICK BRAIN CHECK! (3 Questions)"
- **Style:** Quiz-show vibe, bright purple bubbles, big PAUSE prompt at bottom.

---
## Slide 14 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three answer cards flipping open, each pairing the question icon with its answer: Telomeres, Hayflick Limit ~50, Mitochondria.
- **Text overlay:** "ANSWERS REVEALED"
- **Style:** Green checkmark accents, card-flip motif, confident bold typography.

---
## Slide 15 — Recap & Wrap-up
- **Visual:** A healthy glowing person surrounded by icons of good food, exercise, sleep, sunscreen, and a no-smoking sign, with a subscribe button popping in.
- **Text overlay:** "STAY YOUNG — SUBSCRIBE!"
- **Style:** Uplifting bright finish, neon subscribe button, Katixo Shiksha logo centered, warm hopeful glow.

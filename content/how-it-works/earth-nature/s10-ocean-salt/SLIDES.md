# S10 — Why Is the Ocean Salty — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A big ocean wave crashing under a dark sky, with glowing salt crystals sparkling in the spray.
- **Text overlay:** "OCEAN — Paani Khara Kyun Hota Hai?"
- **Style:** Deep teal ocean glow on dark, bold neon title, salt-sparkle accents.

## Slide 2 — How Much Salt
- **Visual:** A 1-litre water bottle next to a 35-gram salt pile; Earth covered in a 150 m salt layer for scale.
- **Text overlay:** "3.5% namak | 1 litre = 35 gram | Zameen pe 150m parat!"
- **Style:** Scale-comparison infographic, glowing numbers.

## Slide 3 — Where Salt Comes From
- **Visual:** Acidic rain (CO2-tinted drops) falling on mountains, slowly eroding rock and releasing minerals.
- **Text overlay:** "Acidic baarish + chattanein = Weathering"
- **Style:** Mountain cutaway, neon mineral particles releasing.

## Slide 4 — Rivers Carry It
- **Visual:** Streams merging into rivers flowing to the sea, tiny salt specks traveling along.
- **Text overlay:** "Nadiyaan namak samundar tak laati hain | River paani feeka kyun?"
- **Style:** Flowing river map, animated-style salt trail.

## Slide 5 — Evaporation Trap
- **Visual:** Sun heating the sea; pure water vapor rising up while salt stays behind in the water.
- **Text overlay:** "Sirf paani uadta hai, namak peeche reh jaata hai"
- **Style:** Cross-section of sea + sun, neon vapor arrows up, salt glow staying.

## Slide 6 — Millions of Years
- **Visual:** A giant bowl/ocean with a timeline arrow showing salt accumulating over billions of years.
- **Text overlay:** "Karodon saal se namak jamta gaya"
- **Style:** Time-lapse motif, deep blue with rising salt level.

## Slide 7 — Hydrothermal Vents
- **Visual:** Seafloor vents spewing hot mineral-rich water, an underwater volcano in the background.
- **Text overlay:** "Samundar ki tah se bhi namak aata hai"
- **Style:** Deep-sea dark scene, glowing orange vent plumes.

## Slide 8 — What the Salt Is Made Of
- **Visual:** Salt crystal breaking into sodium and chloride ions; a small "mineral soup" pot with Mg, Ca, K labels.
- **Text overlay:** "85% Sodium Chloride | Samundar = mineral soup"
- **Style:** Ion diagram, color-coded element tags.

## Slide 9 — Not All Seas Are Equal
- **Visual:** World map highlighting Red Sea/Persian Gulf (very salty) and a person floating on the Dead Sea.
- **Text overlay:** "Dead Sea me bina taire float karo!"
- **Style:** Map + floating-figure illustration, salinity heat colors.

## Slide 10 — Is It Getting Saltier
- **Visual:** Balance scale — salt coming in (rivers) vs salt removed (sea creatures' shells, seafloor rock).
- **Text overlay:** "Namak aata bhi hai, hatta bhi hai = balance"
- **Style:** Balanced scale motif, equilibrium glow.

## Slide 11 — The Story So Far
- **Visual:** Full cycle diagram: rain → weathering → rivers → ocean → evaporation, with vents and removal icons.
- **Text overlay:** "Weathering → Rivers → Evaporation → jama namak"
- **Style:** Circular cycle infographic, neon-labeled stages.

## Slide 12 — Quick Brain Check
- **Visual:** Big pause icon, three numbered question bubbles, a salt crystal in the center.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! Video PAUSE karo — 3 sawaal"
- **Style:** Quiz-show vibe, bold yellow numbers on dark.

## Slide 13 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three checkmark cards: rocks/weathering, evaporation, sodium chloride.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: Weathering | Evaporation | Sodium Chloride"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, clean answer cards.

## Slide 14 — Recap & Season Wrap
- **Visual:** Recap strip of the salt cycle; a glowing "Season Finale" badge and Katixo Shiksha logo with subscribe button.
- **Text overlay:** "Season wrap! Sawaal poochhna mat chhodo | Subscribe karna mat bhoolna!"
- **Style:** Celebratory finale design, neon confetti, logo bottom-right.

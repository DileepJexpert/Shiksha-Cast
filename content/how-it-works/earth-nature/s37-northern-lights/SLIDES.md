# S37 — What Causes the Northern Lights? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A stunning night sky over snowy mountains with waving green and purple aurora curtains reflecting on a frozen lake.
- **Text overlay:** "What Causes the NORTHERN LIGHTS?"
- **Style:** Photoreal aurora with neon green glow, bold white title, starry black sky, magical mood.

---
## Slide 2 — The Sun & solar wind
- **Visual:** A fiery sun on the left blasting a stream of tiny glowing particles toward space, arrows showing high speed.
- **Text overlay:** "It all starts with the SUN — the solar wind"
- **Style:** Orange-red sun, neon yellow particle stream, speed labels (400 km/s), dark space.

---
## Slide 3 — Earth's magnetic shield
- **Visual:** Earth surrounded by curved magnetic field lines acting like a shield, deflecting incoming particles.
- **Text overlay:** "Earth's MAGNETIC FIELD = our invisible shield"
- **Style:** Blue glowing field lines wrapping the globe, particles bouncing off, molten-iron core inset.

---
## Slide 4 — Funnels at the poles
- **Visual:** Cutaway of Earth showing field lines funneling down into the North and South poles, particles sliding in.
- **Text overlay:** "Poles = weak spots → particles sneak in"
- **Style:** Cyan funnel lines at poles, labels Aurora Borealis (N) and Aurora Australis (S), dark globe.

---
## Slide 5 — Collision in the atmosphere
- **Visual:** A fast particle smashing into an oxygen/nitrogen molecule, energy burst on impact.
- **Text overlay:** "Particles SMASH into oxygen & nitrogen"
- **Style:** Comic-style impact flash, glowing molecules, neon collision spark, atmosphere layer band.

---
## Slide 6 — Excited atoms emit light
- **Visual:** An atom glowing brighter (excited), then releasing a burst of colored light, like a neon sign.
- **Text overlay:** "Excited atoms RELEASE energy as LIGHT"
- **Style:** Atom with orbiting electrons, energy-up then light-out animation feel, neon tube comparison.

---
## Slide 7 — Why different colors
- **Visual:** Vertical altitude chart: red oxygen (top, 300km+), green oxygen (middle, 100-300km), blue/purple nitrogen (bottom).
- **Text overlay:** "Green = oxygen | Red = high oxygen | Blue/Purple = nitrogen"
- **Style:** Color-coded altitude bands, glowing labels, height numbers on side axis.

---
## Slide 8 — Solar storms
- **Visual:** A massive solar flare erupting from the sun, a huge cloud of particles racing to Earth, intense aurora result.
- **Text overlay:** "Solar storms → SUPER bright auroras"
- **Style:** Dramatic red-orange flare, CME cloud, mention Carrington Event 1859, intense glow.

---
## Slide 9 — Where to see them
- **Visual:** World map highlighting Norway, Iceland, Finland, Canada, Alaska in the north; Antarctica, NZ in south; plus Jupiter/Saturn insets.
- **Text overlay:** "Best seen near the POLES — even on other planets!"
- **Style:** Glowing map pins, neon polar rings, planet insets with their own auroras.

---
## Slide 10 — Full recap chain
- **Visual:** A left-to-right flow diagram: Sun → solar wind → magnetic field → poles → collision → glowing sky.
- **Text overlay:** "Sun → wind → shield → poles → glow!"
- **Style:** Clean glowing flow arrows, mini-icons per step, dark background, satisfying summary.

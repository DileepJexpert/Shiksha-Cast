# S42 — How Does a Camera Capture a Photo? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A finger pressing a shutter button, with a freeze-frame "click!" moment splitting into a vibrant captured photo.
- **Text overlay:** "How Does a Camera CAPTURE a Photo?"
- **Style:** Big neon-cyan title, dark backdrop, dynamic motion-freeze effect.

## Slide 2 — Camera = Artificial Eye
- **Visual:** Side-by-side human eye and camera, both with light rays bouncing off an object into them.
- **Text overlay:** "No light = no photo"
- **Style:** Glowing light-ray paths, neon eye/camera icons, dark theme.

## Slide 3 — The Lens & Focusing
- **Visual:** Scattered light rays entering a convex lens and converging to a focal point; small upside-down image formed.
- **Text overlay:** "Lens bends light → focus (image is flipped!)"
- **Style:** Glowing converging beams, neon lens curve, inverted mini-image callout.

## Slide 4 — Aperture
- **Visual:** Iris diaphragm opening wide (dark) and small (bright), next to a human pupil for comparison; f-number scale.
- **Text overlay:** "Aperture = light hole | small f = MORE light"
- **Style:** Animated-style iris blades, neon f-stop dial, depth-of-field blur demo.

## Slide 5 — Shutter Speed
- **Visual:** Split image — frozen flying bird (fast shutter) vs glowing car light trails at night (slow shutter).
- **Text overlay:** "Shutter speed = freeze or blur motion"
- **Style:** High-contrast action shot vs long-exposure streaks, neon timing labels.

## Slide 6 — The Image Sensor
- **Visual:** Light hitting a CMOS chip covered in a grid of tiny squares (pixels); each pixel glowing by brightness.
- **Text overlay:** "Sensor: light → electric signal"
- **Style:** Zoomed pixel grid, neon-green glow per pixel, circuit-board aesthetic.

## Slide 7 — Colour & Bayer Filter
- **Visual:** Pixel grid overlaid with red/green/blue Bayer pattern; arrows showing demosaicing into full colour.
- **Text overlay:** "RGB filters build every colour"
- **Style:** Red-green-blue mosaic, neon mixing arrows, dark theme.

## Slide 8 — Analog to Digital
- **Visual:** Wavy analog signal feeding into an ADC chip and coming out as a grid of 0s and 1s forming a photo.
- **Text overlay:** "Signal → numbers → digital image"
- **Style:** Glowing binary stream, pixel-number grid, neon-blue tech vibe.

## Slide 9 — Image Processor / AI
- **Visual:** A dull raw photo on left transforming into a vivid, sharp, bright photo on right via a glowing ISP/AI chip.
- **Text overlay:** "Processor + AI = computational photography"
- **Style:** Before/after split, neon enhancement sparkles, AI brain icon.

## Slide 10 — Megapixels Fact
- **Visual:** A photo zoomed to reveal millions of tiny squares; counter showing "12 MP = ~12,000,000 pixels".
- **Text overlay:** "More megapixels ≠ always better"
- **Style:** Deep zoom into pixel grid, neon counter, myth-buster tag.

## Slide 11 — QUIZ
- **Visual:** Glowing brain icon with three numbered camera-themed question cards. Pause symbol.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! | 1·2·3 → PAUSE"
- **Style:** Neon-yellow energetic accents, countdown vibe, dark backdrop.

## Slide 12 — ANSWERS
- **Visual:** Three answer cards with green ticks, mini-icons echoing Slides 3, 6, 7.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: Focus · Electric Signal · Bayer RGB"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, clean reveal layout, dark theme.

## Slide 13 — RECAP / Next Episode
- **Visual:** Recap montage of lens-sensor-processor icons; teaser of a steaming coffee cup for next episode; glowing Subscribe button.
- **Text overlay:** "Next: Why Does Coffee Wake You Up? | SUBSCRIBE"
- **Style:** Bold Katixo KhojLab logo, neon Subscribe CTA, upbeat dark finish.

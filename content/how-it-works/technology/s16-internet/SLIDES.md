# S16 — How Does the Internet Actually Work — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A glowing world map at night with bright neon lines (submarine cables) crisscrossing the oceans, a teen holding a phone in the foreground silhouette.
- **Text overlay:** "How Does the Internet Actually Work?"
- **Style:** Deep navy background, electric cyan cable lines, big bold white title with neon glow.

---
## Slide 2 — Internet = Network of Networks
- **Visual:** Many small computer icons connected by glowing lines forming a giant mesh, like a spiderweb of devices.
- **Text overlay:** "INTER + NETWORK = A Web of Computers"
- **Style:** Dark grid background, green neon connecting lines, minimalist device icons.

---
## Slide 3 — Servers and Clients
- **Visual:** A massive data center with rows of glowing server racks on one side, a small phone/laptop on the other, an arrow loop labelled request/response.
- **Text overlay:** "Client asks. Server answers."
- **Style:** Cold blue server glow, warm accent on the client device, clean split-screen layout.

---
## Slide 4 — Packets
- **Visual:** A photo breaking apart into many small numbered envelope-like blocks travelling on different glowing paths.
- **Text overlay:** "Big data → tiny PACKETS"
- **Style:** Orange and yellow neon packets on dark background, motion blur trails.

---
## Slide 5 — IP Address
- **Visual:** Devices each tagged with a glowing address label, like houses with numbers on a futuristic street.
- **Text overlay:** "Every device has an address (IP)"
- **Style:** Purple neon labels, dark cityscape silhouette, monospaced number font.

---
## Slide 6 — DNS the Phonebook
- **Visual:** A glowing phonebook morphing a website name into a string of numbers, arrow showing the conversion.
- **Text overlay:** "DNS = Internet's Phonebook"
- **Style:** Teal accents, open-book glow effect, before/after arrow.

---
## Slide 7 — Routers
- **Visual:** Packets arriving at a glowing junction with a traffic-police style router directing them down the best path.
- **Text overlay:** "Routers = Traffic Police of the Net"
- **Style:** Red and cyan signal lights, junction node glowing, directional arrows.

---
## Slide 8 — Protocols TCP/IP and HTTP
- **Visual:** Stacked layered blocks labelled TCP, IP, HTTP/HTTPS like a tech stack tower, packets flowing through.
- **Text overlay:** "Common language = PROTOCOLS"
- **Style:** Layered neon tower, each layer a different glow colour, clean labels.

---
## Slide 9 — Encryption / HTTPS
- **Visual:** A padlock glowing on a browser bar, readable text turning into scrambled code mid-flight.
- **Text overlay:** "HTTPS → your data is locked & safe"
- **Style:** Gold padlock glow, green scrambled code rain in background, secure vibe.

---
## Slide 10 — The Full Journey
- **Visual:** Animated-style flow: phone → DNS → routers → undersea cable → data center → back, all in one looping path.
- **Text overlay:** "All this in under a second!"
- **Style:** Bright path line connecting all stages, speed streaks, dark map base.

---
## Slide 11 — No Central Control / History
- **Visual:** A resilient mesh network where one node breaks but data reroutes around it, vintage ARPANET sketch in corner.
- **Text overlay:** "Built to survive — data always finds a way"
- **Style:** Glowing mesh with one greyed-out broken node, rerouted bright line.

---
## Slide 12 — Quick Brain Check
- **Visual:** A glowing brain with three floating question marks, a pause icon prominently shown.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! ⏸ Pause & Think — 3 Questions"
- **Style:** Bright magenta brain glow, big pause symbol, energetic quiz vibe.

---
## Slide 13 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three glowing tick marks with short answer labels appearing one by one.
- **Text overlay:** "1. Packet switching  2. DNS  3. Secure"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, clean numbered list, celebratory glow.

---
## Slide 14 — Recap & Next Episode
- **Visual:** Collage of all key icons (packets, router, DNS, padlock) with a teaser image of the moon following a running kid.
- **Text overlay:** "Next: Why does the Moon follow you? Subscribe!"

# S09 — How Does GPS Know Where You Are — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A phone with a glowing blue location dot on a map, beams connecting up to satellites in orbit above Earth.
- **Text overlay:** "GPS — Teri Location Kaise Pata Chalti Hai?"
- **Style:** Earth-from-space at night, neon blue beams, bold cyan title.

## Slide 2 — What Is GPS
- **Visual:** Earth surrounded by ~30 orbiting satellites in a constellation; a callout for India's NavIC.
- **Text overlay:** "GPS = 24–31 satellites, ~20,000 km upar | India = NavIC"
- **Style:** Orbital constellation diagram, neon orbit rings.

## Slide 3 — Satellites Send Signals
- **Visual:** One satellite broadcasting a radio signal carrying two tags: its location + send-time. Phone receiving only.
- **Text overlay:** "Signal me: satellite ki location + bhejne ka time | One-way!"
- **Style:** Signal-wave graphics, green data tags.

## Slide 4 — Measuring Distance
- **Visual:** A stopwatch + a signal traveling at light speed; formula displayed.
- **Text overlay:** "Distance = Speed × Time | Light speed ~3 lakh km/s"
- **Style:** Equation highlight, neon stopwatch motif.

## Slide 5 — One Satellite Isn't Enough
- **Visual:** A single satellite with a huge sphere around it; a question mark showing the unknown exact point.
- **Text overlay:** "Ek distance = ek sphere, ek pakka point nahi"
- **Style:** Translucent sphere on dark, amber question glow.

## Slide 6 — Trilateration Explained
- **Visual:** Map of India with three overlapping circles from Delhi, Jaipur, Agra meeting at one point.
- **Text overlay:** "Trilateration — teen circles ek point pe milte hain"
- **Style:** Map overlay, three neon-colored circles intersecting.

## Slide 7 — Why 4 Satellites
- **Visual:** Four satellites linking to a phone; labels for latitude, longitude, altitude, and clock fix.
- **Text overlay:** "3 = 2D | 4th = altitude + clock fix"
- **Style:** Four-beam diagram, color-coded purpose labels.

## Slide 8 — Einstein in Your Pocket
- **Visual:** Satellite clock vs ground clock with tiny time difference; relativity icons (speed slows, gravity speeds up).
- **Text overlay:** "Relativity correction | Warna roz kai km galat!"
- **Style:** Dual-clock with relativity glow, blue/purple.

## Slide 9 — Why GPS Isn't Perfect
- **Visual:** Tall buildings bouncing signals (multipath), atmosphere slowing waves, a tunnel blocking signal.
- **Text overlay:** "Multipath, ionosphere, dewaarein = error"
- **Style:** City + atmosphere cutaway, red error markers.

## Slide 10 — Assisted GPS
- **Visual:** Phone combining GPS + Wi-Fi + cell towers + motion sensors into one accurate dot.
- **Text overlay:** "Assisted GPS = GPS + Wi-Fi + towers + sensors"
- **Style:** Multi-source fusion diagram, teal accents.

## Slide 11 — GPS Everywhere
- **Visual:** Icon grid — maps, food/cab delivery, farming tractors, ships, planes, rescue teams, banking.
- **Text overlay:** "Maps, delivery, kheti, rescue, banking — sab GPS pe"
- **Style:** Icon montage, vibrant neon outlines.

## Slide 12 — Quick Brain Check
- **Visual:** Big pause icon, three numbered question bubbles, a satellite in the center.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! Video PAUSE karo — 3 sawaal"
- **Style:** Quiz-show vibe, bold yellow numbers on dark.

## Slide 13 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three checkmark cards: speed×time, trilateration/4 satellites, Einstein.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: Speed×Time | Trilateration (4 sats) | Einstein"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, clean answer cards.

## Slide 14 — Recap & Next Episode
- **Visual:** Recap strip of satellites, trilateration circles, phone dot; teaser card with a salty wave and salt crystals.
- **Text overlay:** "Next: Why Is the Ocean Salty? | Subscribe karna mat bhoolna!"
- **Style:** Recap on left, glowing ocean teaser on right. Katixo KhojLab logo bottom-right.

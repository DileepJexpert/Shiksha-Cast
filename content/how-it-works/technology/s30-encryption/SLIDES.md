# S30 — How Do Passwords and Encryption Keep Secrets? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A chat message turning into a glowing padlock, traveling across a web of servers past a blocked hacker
- **Text overlay:** "How Encryption Keeps SECRETS" in bold neon-blue
- **Subtext:** "The invisible lock on your digital life"
- **Bottom bar:** Katixo KhojLab logo + "S30" badge
- **Style:** Dark cyber background, neon-blue/green accents, lock glow

## Slide 2 — Plaintext → Ciphertext
- **Visual:** Readable message scrambling into jumbled symbols and back again
- **Text overlay:** "Encryption = Scramble the Message"
- **Key terms:** "Plaintext → Ciphertext → (decrypt) back"
- **Style:** Dark theme, glitch-scramble effect, neon arrows

## Slide 3 — Caesar Cipher History
- **Visual:** Roman emperor + alphabet shifted by 3; CAT → FDW demo
- **Text overlay:** "Caesar Cipher: Shift by 3"
- **Key note:** "Ancient idea — but only 25 ways = easy to crack"
- **Style:** Dark theme, parchment-meets-neon, sliding alphabet strip

## Slide 4 — The Key
- **Visual:** A literal key turning scrambled text into readable text; 256-bit length meter
- **Text overlay:** "The KEY Is the Real Secret"
- **Key note:** "Longer key (up to 256 bits) = stronger lock"
- **Style:** Dark theme, glowing key icon, strength meter

## Slide 5 — Symmetric Encryption
- **Visual:** One key locking AND unlocking a safe; worry icon over sharing the key
- **Text overlay:** "Symmetric: One Key for Both"
- **Key note:** "Fast — but how to share the key safely?"
- **Style:** Dark theme, single-key graphic, red question on key transfer

## Slide 6 — Asymmetric Encryption
- **Visual:** Two linked keys — public (shared to all) locks, private (kept secret) unlocks
- **Text overlay:** "Asymmetric: Public + Private Keys"
- **Key note:** "Public locks, only private unlocks"
- **Style:** Dark theme, paired-key glow, blue public / gold private

## Slide 7 — Letterbox Analogy / RSA
- **Visual:** Open letterbox anyone can drop mail into; only owner's key opens it; prime-number motif
- **Text overlay:** "Like a Letterbox (RSA)"
- **Key note:** "Built on huge primes: easy to multiply, hard to reverse"
- **Style:** Dark theme, mailbox illustration, prime-number sparkle

## Slide 8 — Passwords Are Hashed
- **Visual:** Password going through a one-way hashing machine; arrow back crossed out
- **Text overlay:** "Sites Don't Store Your Password!"
- **Key note:** "Hashing = one-way, can't be reversed"
- **Style:** Dark theme, one-way funnel, red blocked reverse-arrow

## Slide 9 — Hashing + Salt
- **Visual:** "hello123" → long random hash; two identical passwords getting different hashes via salt
- **Text overlay:** "Hashing + Salt"
- **Key note:** "Login = re-hash & compare; salt makes each unique"
- **Style:** Dark theme, hash-string graphic, salt-sprinkle icon

## Slide 10 — Strong Passwords
- **Visual:** Weak "12345" cracked in seconds vs long passphrase resisting brute force; 2FA shield
- **Text overlay:** "Build a STRONG Password"
- **Key note:** "12+ chars, mix types, unique, use 2-step verification"
- **Style:** Dark theme, cracked vs shielded contrast, neon checklist

## Slide 11 — HTTPS & End-to-End
- **Visual:** Browser bar with padlock + https; TLS handshake; WhatsApp end-to-end lock chain
- **Text overlay:** "HTTPS + End-to-End Encryption"
- **Key note:** "TLS shares key, then fast encryption; only sender & receiver read"
- **Style:** Dark theme, browser mockup, glowing padlock + chain

## Slide 12 — Quiz
- **Visual:** "Quick Brain Check!" banner with 3 numbered cards + pause icon
- **Text overlay:** "3 Questions — PAUSE & Think!"
- **Questions:** "1. Two keys in asymmetric, which is shared?  2. One-way process for passwords?  3. Browser sign of encryption?"
- **Style:** Game-show style, neon quiz cards, dark background

## Slide 13 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three answer cards with green checkmarks
- **Text overlay:** "Answers!"
- **Answers:** "1. Public (shared) & private keys  2. Hashing (+ salt)  3. Padlock icon + https"
- **Style:** Dark theme, green tick glow, clean layout

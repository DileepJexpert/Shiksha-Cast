# S50 — Why Does Your Gut Have Trillions of Bacteria? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_015.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A glowing cross-section silhouette of a teenager with the belly area lit up, swarming with thousands of tiny glowing bacteria dots. Dark background with a faint neon teal glow radiating from the gut.
- **Text overlay:** "38–100 TRILLION friends inside YOU?"
- **Style:** Massive bold neon headline, channel logo top-left, slight zoom-in motion feel. High-contrast, mysterious mood.

---
## Slide 2 — Where They Live (Microbiome City)
- **Visual:** Stylized human digestive tract diagram with the large intestine highlighted and rendered as a glowing futuristic city full of bacteria-citizens. A small scale icon comparing 1–2 kg weight to a brain.
- **Text overlay:** "Welcome to your MICROBIOME"
- **Style:** Neon green and purple city lights, labeled large intestine, clean infographic vibe.

---
## Slide 3 — Symbiosis (The Deal)
- **Visual:** A balanced handshake graphic — on one side a human icon offering a house and a plate of food, on the other side bacteria offering wrenches, vitamins, and shields. A glowing equals sign in the middle.
- **Text overlay:** "SYMBIOSIS: Win–Win deal"
- **Style:** Split-screen layout, warm vs cool color contrast, friendly partnership tone.

---
## Slide 4 — Digesting Fiber
- **Visual:** Close-up of fiber-rich foods (vegetables, lentils, whole grains) with strands of fiber flowing into the gut, where bacteria break them into glowing energy compounds feeding intestine cells.
- **Text overlay:** "They digest FIBER we can't"
- **Style:** Macro food photography style with neon breakdown animation feel, arrows showing fiber to energy.

---
## Slide 5 — Vitamin Factory
- **Visual:** Tiny bacteria working at glowing factory conveyor belts producing labeled vitamin capsules (Vitamin K, B12, Biotin). A small factory-inside-the-gut motif.
- **Text overlay:** "Your gut's VITAMIN factory"
- **Style:** Playful industrial neon factory, glowing capsule icons, energetic.

---
## Slide 6 — Immune System Training
- **Visual:** Good bacteria acting as drill instructors training immune cells (white blood cells) like soldiers near the gut wall, blocking incoming bad germs at a glowing wall.
- **Text overlay:** "70% of immunity lives here"
- **Style:** Shield and barrier motifs, neon blue defensive glow, action-packed.

---
## Slide 7 — Gut-Brain Axis
- **Visual:** A glowing nerve highway connecting the brain and the gut, with chemical signal sparks traveling both ways. A teen with a thought bubble showing mood emojis.
- **Text overlay:** "Gut & Brain TEXT each other"
- **Style:** Neon signal lines, brain top, gut bottom, dynamic two-way arrows.

---
## Slide 8 — Good vs Bad Balance
- **Visual:** A tug-of-war or balance scale between friendly smiling good bacteria and spiky angry bad bacteria, the scale currently tipped toward good.
- **Text overlay:** "Good vs Bad: keep the BALANCE"
- **Style:** Contrast of green friendly bacteria vs red menacing bacteria, balance-scale focal point.

---
## Slide 9 — Probiotics (Curd/Dahi)
- **Visual:** A bowl of dahi/curd glowing with friendly bacteria marching out of it into a happy gut. Side icons of buttermilk and fermented foods.
- **Text overlay:** "PROBIOTICS = live good bacteria"
- **Style:** Warm desi food aesthetic, neon friendly bacteria, inviting and relatable.

---
## Slide 10 — Prebiotics (Fiber Food)
- **Visual:** Plates of fiber-rich foods (banana, onion, lentils, whole grains, fruits) with arrows showing fiber feeding the good bacteria in the large intestine like a feast.
- **Text overlay:** "PREBIOTICS = food for them"
- **Style:** Bright food layout vs glowing gut, clear probiotics vs prebiotics distinction callout.

---
## Slide 11 — What Harms Them (Warning)
- **Visual:** A warning sign over junk food, sugary drinks, and antibiotic pills. A field illustration where fire burns both weeds and good crops to show antibiotics killing good bacteria too.
- **Text overlay:** "WARNING: don't harm your microbes"
- **Style:** Caution yellow-and-red alert accents, dramatic but informative.

---
## Slide 12 — Big Picture Recap of Roles
- **Visual:** A central glowing gut with four neon spokes radiating to icons: fiber digestion, vitamins, immune shield, and brain mood. An invisible-world wonder vibe.
- **Text overlay:** "An invisible world keeps you ALIVE"
- **Style:** Hub-and-spoke infographic, awe-inspiring cosmic-science mood.

---
## Slide 13 — Quiz
- **Visual:** Bold quiz card layout with a glowing brain icon and three numbered question slots, plus a large pause icon at the bottom.
- **Text overlay:** "QUICK BRAIN CHECK! — Pause & think"
- **Style:** Game-show energy, neon question marks, three clear question blocks.

---
## Slide 14 — Answers
- **Visual:** Same quiz card flipped to reveal three answers with green check marks: Microbiome, Fiber, Probiotics.
- **Text overlay:** "ANSWERS: Microbiome · Fiber · Probiotics"
- **Style:** Reveal layout with checkmarks, celebratory neon green accents.

---
## Slide 15 — Recap + Subscribe
- **Visual:** Montage of the episode's key icons (bacteria, fiber, vitamins, shield, brain) circling a happy teen, with a glowing milestone badge and a big subscribe button.
- **Text overlay:** "Take care of your gut friends — SUBSCRIBE!"
- **Style:** Upbeat closing frame, channel logo, glowing subscribe call-to-action, milestone celebration feel.

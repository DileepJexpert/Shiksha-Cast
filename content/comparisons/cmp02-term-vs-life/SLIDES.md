# CMP02 — Term Insurance vs Life (Endowment) Insurance — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Side-by-side VS comparison, two columns, check/cross icons, table feel

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** An insurance agent at a family's dining table showing two glossy brochures — one cheap "no money back", one expensive "money back". Family looking puzzled.
- **Text overlay:** "Term vs Life Insurance — Kaunsa Sahi?"
- **Style:** Bold title, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left, two coloured tags (orange Term, teal Endowment). Small disclaimer chip: "General info, not advice."

## Slide 2 — Intro: Insurance ka asli kaam
- **Visual:** A shield protecting a family, with a salary/income icon; a crossed-out "money plant" to show insurance ≠ earning tool.
- **Text overlay:** "Insurance = Parivar ki suraksha, kamai ka zariya NAHI"
- **Style:** Clean concept slide, shield motif, splits into two paths (Protection / Protection+Saving).

## Slide 3 — Term insurance explained
- **Visual:** Timeline: small annual premium coins flowing in; if death → big lump sum (₹1 crore) to family; if survives → zero return (greyed).
- **Text overlay:** "Term = Sirf protection | Chhota premium, bada cover | Jiyo to kuch wapas nahi"
- **Style:** Orange theme, timeline with two outcomes, lump-sum highlighted.

## Slide 4 — Endowment / Life insurance explained
- **Visual:** Premium splitting into two streams — one to "Life Cover" shield, one to "Saving/Invest" piggy bank; maturity returns money + bonus.
- **Text overlay:** "Endowment = Protection + Saving | Maturity par paisa wapas | Premium kai guna zyada"
- **Style:** Teal theme, split-stream diagram, piggy bank + bonus tag.

## Slide 5 — Cost & cover comparison
- **Visual:** Two-column table for a 30-yr healthy person, ₹1 crore cover. Rows: Premium/year, Cover size, Money back.
- **Text overlay:** "Term ~₹10-15k/yr | Endowment lakhs/yr (approx) | Same paisa = Term bada cover"
- **Style:** Table grid, rupee icons, big contrast between the two premium figures.

## Slide 6 — The return angle
- **Visual:** A slow-growing bar (endowment ~4-6%) next to inflation line; caption that mixing protection + saving weakens both.
- **Text overlay:** "Endowment return ~4-6% (approx) | Protection + saving milane se dono kamzor"
- **Style:** Bar chart vs inflation dashed line, honest/neutral tone.

## Slide 7 — Buy term, invest the rest
- **Visual:** Cheap term premium + a separate arrow of saved money going into mutual fund / PPF, growing taller over time, plus a big cover shield.
- **Text overlay:** "Buy term, invest the rest | Bada cover + zyada paisa (discipline chahiye)"
- **Style:** Two-track diagram, growth arrow, small disclaimer: "General info."

## Slide 8 — Term pros & cons
- **Visual:** Pros/cons card, green ticks vs red crosses.
- **Text overlay:** "Term ✓ Sasta, bada cover, simple  ✗ Jiyo to wapas nahi"
- **Style:** Balanced card, note: "Sahi info do, warna claim dikkat."

## Slide 9 — Endowment pros & cons
- **Visual:** Pros/cons card in teal.
- **Text overlay:** "Endowment ✓ Maturity paisa, forced saving  ✗ Mehnga, chhota cover, kamzor return"
- **Style:** Balanced card, fair tone.

## Slide 10 — Who should pick what
- **Visual:** Two personas — earning parent with dependents → Term badge; high-income person with cover already + no self-invest habit → optional Endowment slice.
- **Text overlay:** "Pehle protection, baad mein saving | Dependents ho → Term zaroori"
- **Style:** Persona cards, ordered priority arrow.

## Slide 11 — Important warning
- **Visual:** A tiny endowment policy shield far too small to cover a whole family; red alert icon.
- **Text overlay:** "Sirf endowment ko poora insurance mat samjho | Term cover = salary ka 10-15x"
- **Style:** Warning slide, red accent, undersized-shield visual.

## Slide 12 — Recap
- **Visual:** Two summary chips: Term (orange) "sasta, bada cover" vs Endowment (teal) "mehnga, paisa wapas". Centre note: "Maqsad alag hai."
- **Text overlay:** "Term = protection | Endowment = forced saving | Pehle term, phir invest"
- **Style:** Recap board, takeaway emphasised, "consult a planner" note.

## Slide 13 — Verdict + CTA + Disclaimer
- **Visual:** Protected happy family; Katixo KhojLab subscribe button + bell.
- **Text overlay:** "Insurance ka pehla kaam = suraksha | Katixo KhojLab subscribe karein! | Ye general info hai, financial advice nahi."
- **Style:** Warm closing, big subscribe CTA, disclaimer clearly visible.

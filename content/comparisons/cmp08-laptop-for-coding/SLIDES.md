# CMP08 — Laptop Buying Guide for Coding / Students — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Buying-guide + comparison, spec cards, check/cross icons, priority table feel

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A student/parent at a laptop shop, salesman throwing big words (RAM, SSD, processor) in speech bubbles, confused face + rupee tag.
- **Text overlay:** "Coding Ke Liye Laptop — Kaise Chunein?"
- **Style:** Bold title, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left, code-screen + price-tag motif. Tag: "Students & coders guide."

## Slide 2 — Myth: gaming laptop NOT needed
- **Visual:** A cross over an expensive flashy gaming laptop; a green tick on a balanced, sensible laptop running a code editor + browser.
- **Text overlay:** "Coding ke liye mehenga gaming laptop ZAROORI NAHI | Balanced laptop kaafi"
- **Style:** Myth-buster panel, cross-vs-tick, "strong in right things" note.

## Slide 3 — RAM (most important)
- **Visual:** RAM sticks; multiple apps (editor + many browser tabs + tool) running smoothly vs a low-RAM laptop stuttering.
- **Text overlay:** "RAM = ek saath kaam | Kam se kam 8GB | Budget ho to 16GB future-proof"
- **Style:** Priority-1 badge, smooth-vs-stutter contrast.

## Slide 4 — SSD (almost essential)
- **Visual:** Old slow HDD vs fast SSD; SSD laptop boots fast, apps open instantly; HDD laptop with loading spinner.
- **Text overlay:** "SSD = bahut tez | Bina SSD mat lo | 256GB theek, 512GB behtar"
- **Style:** HDD-vs-SSD speed comparison, "essential" badge.

## Slide 5 — Processor (the brain)
- **Visual:** A CPU chip labelled as the "brain"; a tier ladder showing i5 / Ryzen 5 and above as the sweet zone.
- **Text overlay:** "Processor = dimaag | Intel i5+ ya AMD Ryzen 5+ | Top-end zaroori nahi"
- **Style:** CPU chip + tier-ladder, "can't upgrade later" note.

## Slide 6 — Priority order
- **Visual:** A ranked list: 1) SSD, 2) RAM, 3) Processor, then comfort items (keyboard, screen, battery). Graphics card marked optional.
- **Text overlay:** "Priority: SSD → RAM → Processor → keyboard/screen/battery | Graphics card = optional"
- **Style:** Numbered priority stack, money-where-it-matters theme.

## Slide 7 — Screen, keyboard, battery
- **Visual:** Three comfort icons: a clear eye-friendly screen, a comfy keyboard for long typing, a long-life battery (library/college use).
- **Text overlay:** "Screen saaf, aankhon ko aaram | Comfy keyboard | Battery lambi = college friendly"
- **Style:** Three comfort cards, daily-use comfort emphasis.

## Slide 8 — Operating system
- **Visual:** Three OS logos conceptually: Windows (easy/cheap), Mac (great but costly), Linux (free, on any laptop).
- **Text overlay:** "Windows = aasan & sasta | Mac = badhiya par mehenga | Linux = free, kisi par bhi"
- **Style:** Three-column OS comparison, "start with Windows/Linux" note.

## Slide 9 — Budget guide
- **Visual:** Three budget tiers: low (i5/Ryzen5 + 8GB + SSD sweet spot), more (16GB + bigger SSD), very low (good refurbished with SSD).
- **Text overlay:** "Kam budget: i5/Ryzen5 + 8GB + SSD | Zyada: 16GB + badi SSD | Bahut kam: achha refurbished"
- **Style:** Tiered budget cards, "specs over looks" note.

## Slide 10 — Common mistakes
- **Visual:** Three red-cross cards: cheapest no-SSD low-RAM laptop; over-buying flashy gaming laptop; paying for useless extras.
- **Text overlay:** "Galtiyan: sasta bina SSD | Bekaar mehenga gaming | Faltu features ke paise"
- **Style:** Mistake-warning panel, honest cautionary tone.

## Slide 11 — Buying checklist
- **Visual:** A 6-point tick checklist: SSD? RAM 8GB+? i5/Ryzen5+? comfy keyboard/screen? decent battery? fits budget, no waste?
- **Text overlay:** "Checklist: SSD ✓ | 8GB+ RAM ✓ | i5/Ryzen5+ ✓ | keyboard+screen ✓ | battery ✓ | budget ✓"
- **Style:** Checklist card, practical pre-purchase tool.

## Slide 12 — Laptop is just a tool
- **Visual:** A learner coding happily on a modest laptop; a thought bubble of skills/ideas glowing — message: skill > price.
- **Text overlay:** "Laptop = tool | Mehenga laptop = achha coder NAHI | Skill par energy lagao"
- **Style:** Motivational card, modest-laptop-success theme.

## Slide 13 — Recap
- **Visual:** Summary chips: SSD, 8GB+ RAM, i5/Ryzen5, comfy keyboard, battery; centre note "Sweet spot dhoondo, dikhawa nahi."
- **Text overlay:** "Zaroori: SSD + RAM + processor | Phir keyboard/screen/battery | Budget sweet spot"
- **Style:** Recap board, takeaway emphasised, balanced tone.

## Slide 14 — Verdict + CTA
- **Visual:** A confident student walking out of a shop with the right laptop; Katixo KhojLab subscribe button + bell.
- **Text overlay:** "Sahi laptop = sahi cheezon mein mazboot | Katixo KhojLab subscribe karein!"
- **Style:** Warm closing, big subscribe CTA, share-with-students note.

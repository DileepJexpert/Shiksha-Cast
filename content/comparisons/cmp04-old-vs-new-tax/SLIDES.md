# CMP04 — Old vs New Income Tax Regime — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Side-by-side VS comparison, two columns, check/cross icons, table feel

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person at a laptop filing tax returns, two doors behind labelled "Old Regime" and "New Regime", confused which to choose. Rupee leaking from wrong choice.
- **Text overlay:** "Old vs New Tax Regime — Kaunsa Sasta?"
- **Style:** Bold title, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left, two tags (maroon Old, navy New). Disclaimer chip: "General info, rules badalte hain."

## Slide 2 — Intro: Income tax kya hai
- **Visual:** A slab/staircase showing income rising and tax rate rising per step; two branching paths labelled Old / New.
- **Text overlay:** "Tax slab mein lagta hai | 2 raaste: Old & New"
- **Style:** Staircase slab infographic, clean, sets up the two-path comparison.

## Slide 3 — Old regime explained
- **Visual:** Higher slab rates on left, but a big basket of deductions (80C, home loan, HRA, health insurance) shrinking the taxable income.
- **Text overlay:** "Old = Rates unchi, par DEDUCTIONS bahut (80C, HRA, home loan)"
- **Style:** Maroon theme, deduction basket icons, taxable-income bar shrinking.

## Slide 4 — New regime explained
- **Visual:** Lower slab rates, "default" stamp, but a crossed-out deductions basket; small standard-deduction tag remains.
- **Text overlay:** "New = Rates kam, par bade deductions NAHI (default regime)"
- **Style:** Navy theme, low-rate badges, crossed-out deductions, "easy/simple" mood.

## Slide 5 — The core difference (one line)
- **Visual:** Two balance pans: Old = high rate − big deductions; New = low rate, no deductions. Question: "Aapki chhoot kitni?"
- **Text overlay:** "Old: rates unchi, chhoot le-lekar ghatao | New: rates kam, chhoot bhool jao"
- **Style:** Balance-scale comparison, the deciding question highlighted.

## Slide 6 — Real example (₹12 lakh)
- **Visual:** Rohit, income ₹12L. Case A (no deductions) → New cheaper. Case B (full 80C + home loan + HRA) → Old cheaper. Two outcome cards.
- **Text overlay:** "Same income ₹12L, par sahi regime alag | Chhoot zyada → Old | Chhoot kam → New"
- **Style:** Two-case persona cards, contrasting outcomes.

## Slide 7 — How to decide (calculator)
- **Visual:** A tax calculator on the income-tax portal showing both regimes side by side, lower number circled in green.
- **Text overlay:** "Apni total chhoot jodo → calculator par dono compare karo → sasta wala chuno"
- **Style:** Calculator screenshot mock, green-circled cheaper figure, "free portal calculator" note.

## Slide 8 — Old regime pros & cons
- **Visual:** Pros/cons card, green ticks vs red crosses, document/proof stack icon.
- **Text overlay:** "Old ✓ Chhoot zyada → tax kam, saving habit  ✗ Hisaab zyada, proof sambhalo"
- **Style:** Maroon card, balanced honest tone.

## Slide 9 — New regime pros & cons
- **Visual:** Pros/cons card in navy, "no documents" relaxed icon.
- **Text overlay:** "New ✓ Aasan, no proof, rates kam  ✗ Bade deductions nahi mil sakte"
- **Style:** Navy card, fair tone.

## Slide 10 — Who should pick what
- **Visual:** Two personas — low deductions / no home loan / wants simplicity → New; home loan + full 80C + HRA → Old.
- **Text overlay:** "Chhoot kam + aasan → New | Home loan + 80C + HRA → Old"
- **Style:** Persona cards, deductions as the deciding axis, note "calculator se confirm."

## Slide 11 — Important reminders
- **Visual:** Three alert chips: 1) New is default, choose carefully; 2) Salaried can switch yearly; 3) Rules change every budget.
- **Text overlay:** "New = default | Salaried har saal switch kar sakte | Rules budget mein badalte"
- **Style:** Warning slide, three numbered alert chips.

## Slide 12 — Recap
- **Visual:** Two summary chips: Old (maroon) "rates unchi, chhoot zyada" vs New (navy) "rates kam, chhoot nahi". Centre note: "Aapki total chhoot decide karti hai."
- **Text overlay:** "Old = deductions | New = simple | Har saal compare karo"
- **Style:** Recap board, takeaway emphasised, "rules change" reminder.

## Slide 13 — Verdict + CTA + Disclaimer
- **Visual:** Relaxed person who filed smartly, money saved jar; Katixo KhojLab subscribe button + bell.
- **Text overlay:** "Andaaze se nahi, hisaab se chuno | Katixo KhojLab subscribe karein! | Ye general info hai, financial advice nahi."
- **Style:** Warm closing, big subscribe CTA, disclaimer clearly visible.

# CMP07 — Inverter AC vs Normal AC — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Side-by-side VS comparison, two columns, check/cross icons, table feel

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A family in a hot room, a salesman pointing at two AC boxes — "Inverter" and "Normal". Sweat drops + a confused thought bubble with a rupee/bijli sign.
- **Text overlay:** "Inverter AC vs Normal AC — Kaunsa Lein?"
- **Style:** Bold title, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left, two tags (green Inverter, blue Normal). Cool/warm contrast background.

## Slide 2 — Normal (fixed-speed) AC explained
- **Visual:** A compressor with a big ON/OFF switch slamming between full-power and fully-off; a temperature line bouncing up and down.
- **Text overlay:** "Normal AC = compressor sirf ON ya OFF | Baar-baar chaalu-band | Bijli zyada"
- **Style:** Blue theme, on-off toggle, jagged temperature line, sound-waves icon.

## Slide 3 — Inverter AC explained
- **Visual:** Same compressor but with a speed dial that smoothly slows down/speeds up; a smooth steady temperature line.
- **Text overlay:** "Inverter AC = compressor speed ghatata-badhata | Pura band NAHI | Bijli kam"
- **Style:** Green theme, speed-dial, smooth flat temperature line, quiet icon.

## Slide 4 — Power saving
- **Visual:** Two electricity meters side by side after long use — Normal spinning fast/high bill, Inverter spinning slow/lower bill.
- **Text overlay:** "Lambe use par Inverter = achhi bijli bachat | Khaaskar roz kai ghante"
- **Style:** Two-meter comparison, bill stacks differ, "depends on use" honest note.

## Slide 5 — Price comparison
- **Visual:** Two price tags — Normal cheaper upfront, Inverter a few-thousand more, with a small "?" about how much you'll run it.
- **Text overlay:** "Inverter shuru mein mehenga | Bachat se dheere paisa wapas | Kitna chalaoge?"
- **Style:** Price-tag comparison, upfront-vs-long-term framing.

## Slide 6 — Noise & even cooling
- **Visual:** Normal = wavy temperature + louder speaker icon; Inverter = flat temperature + quiet/sleeping icon (bedroom mood).
- **Text overlay:** "Normal: temperature upar-neeche, awaaz zyada | Inverter: ek-si thandak, kam awaaz"
- **Style:** Two-panel comfort comparison, bedroom/study cue for Inverter.

## Slide 7 — Payback example
- **Visual:** A ~₹8,000 price gap bridged over a few summers by monthly bill savings; after that, net savings every year (heavy use). Light use = long bridge.
- **Text overlay:** "~₹8k mehenga | Bhaari use = kuch summers mein paisa wapas | Kam use = barso lagenge"
- **Style:** Bridge/timeline visual, "your real usage decides" emphasis.

## Slide 8 — Star rating note
- **Visual:** AC unit with 3-star vs 5-star labels; arrow showing more stars = less electricity. Applies to BOTH types.
- **Text overlay:** "Star rating bhi dekho | Zyada star = kam bijli | 5-star Inverter = sabse zyada bachat"
- **Style:** Star-rating badge focus, applies-to-both note.

## Slide 9 — Normal AC pros & cons
- **Visual:** Pros/cons card in blue, green ticks vs red crosses, "rarely-used room" icon.
- **Text overlay:** "Normal ✓ Sasta, simple, kam use ke liye  ✗ Bijli zyada, awaaz, temp upar-neeche"
- **Style:** Balanced card, fair tone.

## Slide 10 — Inverter AC pros & cons
- **Visual:** Pros/cons card in green, electricity-saving + quiet icons, plus a costlier-repair caution.
- **Text overlay:** "Inverter ✓ Bijli bachat, ek-si thandak, kam awaaz  ✗ Mehenga, repair thodi jatil"
- **Style:** Balanced card, "long-term investment if used heavily" note.

## Slide 11 — Who should pick what
- **Visual:** Two personas — heavy daily/bedroom user → Inverter; tight budget / rarely-used guest room → Normal.
- **Text overlay:** "Roz kai ghante / bedroom → Inverter | Tight budget / kam use room → Normal"
- **Style:** Persona cards, usage as the deciding axis.

## Slide 12 — Buying tips
- **Visual:** Checklist: right tonnage for room size, star rating, heavy-use → Inverter; a "total cost over years" calculator.
- **Text overlay:** "Sahi ton chuno | Star rating dekho | Total kharch dekho, sirf price nahi"
- **Style:** Checklist card, practical buyer tips.

## Slide 13 — Recap
- **Visual:** Two summary chips: Normal (blue) "sasta, bijli zyada" vs Inverter (green) "mehenga, bachat zyada". Centre: "Use ke hisaab se chuno."
- **Text overlay:** "Normal = sasta, kam use | Inverter = bachat, bhaari use | Star + ton bhi dekho"
- **Style:** Recap board, takeaway emphasised.

## Slide 14 — Verdict + CTA
- **Visual:** Family relaxing comfortably in a cool room with the right AC; Katixo KhojLab subscribe button + bell.
- **Text overlay:** "Sahi wahi jo aapki zaroorat se mile | Katixo KhojLab subscribe karein!"
- **Style:** Warm cool closing, big subscribe CTA.

# CMP03 — SIP (Mutual Fund) vs FD (Fixed Deposit) — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Side-by-side VS comparison, two columns, check/cross icons, table feel

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person holding saved cash, with two signposts: one to a safe bank vault (FD), one to a growing-graph mutual fund (SIP). Confused expression.
- **Text overlay:** "SIP vs FD — Paisa Kahan Badhega?"
- **Style:** Bold title, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left, two tags (green FD, blue SIP). Disclaimer chip: "General info, not advice."

## Slide 2 — FD explained
- **Visual:** A locked bank vault with a fixed % tag, calendar showing a fixed tenure, guaranteed amount coming out at the end.
- **Text overlay:** "FD = Fixed amount + fixed time = Fixed (pakka) interest | Bazaar ka asar NAHI"
- **Style:** Green theme, vault + calendar + guaranteed payout icons, certainty mood.

## Slide 3 — SIP explained
- **Visual:** Monthly coins (₹500/₹2000) flowing into a mutual fund basket invested in stocks/bonds, return arrow that wiggles up and down.
- **Text overlay:** "SIP = Har mahine fixed amount → mutual fund | Return fixed NAHI | Risk + grow ka mauka"
- **Style:** Blue theme, monthly flow, wavy return line, note: "Market risk ke adheen."

## Slide 4 — Return & risk comparison
- **Visual:** Two-column table. Rows: Return (approx), Guaranteed?, Risk, Bazaar asar.
- **Text overlay:** "FD ~6-7% pakka | SIP ~10-12% (long term, no guarantee)"
- **Style:** Table grid, % badges, green ticks vs caution icons.

## Slide 5 — Compounding example
- **Visual:** Two growth curves from ₹5,000/month for 20 yrs (₹12L invested). FD curve → ~₹26L; SIP curve → ~₹50L. Curves diverge dramatically.
- **Text overlay:** "₹5k/month × 20 yrs | FD ~₹26L | SIP ~₹50L (approx, not guaranteed)"
- **Style:** Two diverging curves, invested-amount baseline marked, "approx" disclaimer.

## Slide 6 — Safety & liquidity
- **Visual:** FD = shield with deposit-insurance badge, small break-penalty note. SIP = withdraw-anytime icon but a "sell low if market down" caution.
- **Text overlay:** "FD: safe, todne par penalty | SIP: kabhi bhi nikalo, par 5-7 saal ruko"
- **Style:** Two-panel, balanced honest tone, time-horizon highlighted.

## Slide 7 — Tax angle
- **Visual:** FD interest added to income → taxed at slab; SIP equity taxed mainly on withdrawal at lower long-term rates. Tax man icon.
- **Text overlay:** "FD interest = slab tax | SIP equity = mostly on exit (rules badalte hain)"
- **Style:** Two-column tax comparison, note: "Current niyam check karein."

## Slide 8 — FD pros & cons
- **Visual:** Pros/cons card, green ticks vs red crosses, elderly-friendly icon.
- **Text overlay:** "FD ✓ Safe, return pakka, simple  ✗ Return kam, mehngai jitna"
- **Style:** Balanced card, fair tone.

## Slide 9 — SIP pros & cons
- **Visual:** Pros/cons card in blue, rupee-cost-averaging mini graph, patience/clock icon.
- **Text overlay:** "SIP ✓ Long-term grow, chhota start, averaging  ✗ No guarantee, dhairya chahiye"
- **Style:** Balanced card, warning: "Girte bazaar mein SIP band karna = badi galti."

## Slide 10 — Who should pick what
- **Visual:** Two personas — short goal / no risk / senior → FD; long goal (retirement, child education 10-15 yrs) → SIP. Goal + time-horizon icons.
- **Text overlay:** "1-2 saal/emergency → FD | 10-15 saal goal → SIP"
- **Style:** Persona cards, time-horizon as the deciding axis.

## Slide 11 — It's not either/or
- **Visual:** A balanced scale or two baskets: short-term + emergency money in FD basket, long-term money in SIP basket.
- **Text overlay:** "Jawab = DONO | Zaroori paisa FD, lamba paisa SIP | Sab ande ek tokri mein mat rakho"
- **Style:** Balance/diversification visual, both colours combined.

## Slide 12 — Recap
- **Visual:** Two summary chips: FD (green) "safe, kam return" vs SIP (blue) "risk, zyada grow". Centre note: "FD bachaata, SIP badhaata."
- **Text overlay:** "FD = bachao | SIP = badhao | Goal + time se baanto"
- **Style:** Recap board, takeaway emphasised, "market risk" reminder.

## Slide 13 — Verdict + CTA + Disclaimer
- **Visual:** Person relaxed with both a safe vault and a growing graph; Katixo KhojLab subscribe button + bell.
- **Text overlay:** "Sahi wahi jo aapke goal se mile | Katixo KhojLab subscribe karein! | Ye general info hai, financial advice nahi."
- **Style:** Warm closing, big subscribe CTA, disclaimer clearly visible.

# CMP06 — NPS vs PPF vs EPF (Retirement Savings) — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Side-by-side VS comparison, three columns, check/cross icons, table feel

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** An older person relaxing in retirement, with three labelled savings jars in front: NPS, PPF, EPF. Question mark above, "which one?".
- **Text overlay:** "NPS vs PPF vs EPF — Retirement Ke Liye Kaunsa?"
- **Style:** Bold title, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left, three colour tags (NPS purple, PPF green, EPF blue). Disclaimer chip: "General info, not advice."

## Slide 2 — EPF explained
- **Visual:** A salaried person; arrows show both employee salary and employer both contributing into one EPF box, with a fixed govt interest tag.
- **Text overlay:** "EPF = Salaried ke liye | Aap + company dono dalte ho | Fixed safe byaaj"
- **Style:** Blue theme, dual-contribution arrows, auto-monthly icon.

## Slide 3 — PPF explained
- **Visual:** A 15-year calendar/timeline, anyone (job/business/housewife) opening a PPF account, govt-guarantee shield, market-immune badge.
- **Text overlay:** "PPF = Sab ke liye | 15 saal | Govt guarantee, pura safe | Bazaar ka asar NAHI"
- **Style:** Green theme, long-timeline + shield icons, certainty mood.

## Slide 4 — NPS explained
- **Visual:** Money splitting into stocks + bonds (market-linked), with a pension stream at age 60 (part lump sum, part pension).
- **Text overlay:** "NPS = Pension scheme | Paisa bazaar + bonds mein | Return fixed NAHI | Grow ka mauka"
- **Style:** Purple theme, market-split + pension icons, "market-linked" note.

## Slide 5 — Return & risk comparison
- **Visual:** Three-column table. Rows: Return type, Guaranteed?, Risk, Bazaar asar.
- **Text overlay:** "EPF & PPF = fixed, safe, seemit | NPS = market-linked, grow + risk"
- **Style:** Table grid, green ticks vs caution icons, three columns.

## Slide 6 — Who can open
- **Visual:** Three personas: salaried → EPF; everyone → PPF; almost everyone → NPS. Self-employed highlighted toward PPF + NPS.
- **Text overlay:** "EPF = sirf salaried | PPF = sab | NPS = lagbhag sab | Self-employed → PPF + NPS"
- **Style:** Persona row, eligibility as the deciding axis.

## Slide 7 — Lock-in / withdrawal
- **Visual:** Three locks of different lengths: PPF 15-yr (partial later), EPF (on job-change/retire), NPS (mainly till 60, then pension).
- **Text overlay:** "PPF 15 saal | EPF job-change/retire | NPS ~60 tak, phir pension"
- **Style:** Lock-timeline visual, long-term strength framed positively.

## Slide 8 — Tax angle
- **Visual:** Tax-saving badges on all three; PPF interest+maturity tax-free crown; tax-man icon with "rules change" note.
- **Text overlay:** "Teeno = jama par tax chhoot | PPF byaaj+maturity aksar tax-free | Niyam badalte hain"
- **Style:** Three-column tax comparison, note: "Current niyam check karein."

## Slide 9 — EPF pros & cons
- **Visual:** Pros/cons card in blue, employer-contribution icon, auto-save clock.
- **Text overlay:** "EPF ✓ Company bhi deti, safe, auto-save  ✗ Sirf salaried, return seemit"
- **Style:** Balanced card, fair tone, note: "Akele kaafi nahi."

## Slide 10 — PPF vs NPS pros & cons
- **Visual:** Two pros/cons cards: PPF (green) safe + tax-free vs long lock-in; NPS (purple) grow + pension vs market risk + forced annuity part.
- **Text overlay:** "PPF ✓ Safe, tax-free ✗ Return seemit, 15 saal | NPS ✓ Grow, pension ✗ Risk, pension lazmi"
- **Style:** Two balanced cards, honest fair tone.

## Slide 11 — Who should pick what
- **Visual:** Decision map: salaried → EPF base; full safety → PPF; long-term growth + risk-ok → NPS; self-employed → PPF + NPS combo.
- **Text overlay:** "Salaried → EPF base | Pura safe → PPF | Grow + risk-ok → NPS"
- **Style:** Decision-map cards, goal + risk as deciding axes.

## Slide 12 — It's not either/or
- **Visual:** A balanced stack: EPF foundation + PPF safe layer + NPS growth layer; a clock urging "start early" with a compounding curve.
- **Text overlay:** "Jawab = MEL | EPF + PPF + NPS | Jaldi shuru karo = compounding ka jadoo"
- **Style:** Layered-portfolio visual, "start early" emphasised.

## Slide 13 — Recap
- **Visual:** Three summary chips: EPF (blue) "salaried, auto, safe", PPF (green) "safe, tax-free", NPS (purple) "grow + pension, risk". Centre: "Jaldi shuru = zyada paisa."
- **Text overlay:** "EPF = auto base | PPF = safe & tax-free | NPS = grow + pension | Jaldi shuru karo"
- **Style:** Recap board, takeaway emphasised, fairness reminder.

## Slide 14 — Verdict + CTA + Disclaimer
- **Visual:** Person calm in retirement with all three jars full; Katixo KhojLab subscribe button + bell.
- **Text overlay:** "Sahi wahi jo aapke goal se mile | Katixo KhojLab subscribe karein! | Ye general info hai, financial advice nahi."
- **Style:** Warm closing, big subscribe CTA, disclaimer clearly visible.

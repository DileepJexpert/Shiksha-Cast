# CMP05 — Credit Card vs Debit Card — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Side-by-side VS comparison, two columns, check/cross icons, table feel

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person opening a wallet showing two cards side by side — a blue Debit card and an orange Credit card. Confused expression with two thought bubbles.
- **Text overlay:** "Credit Card vs Debit Card — Aapke Liye Kaunsa?"
- **Style:** Bold title, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left, two tags (blue Debit, orange Credit). Disclaimer chip: "General info, not advice."

## Slide 2 — Debit card explained
- **Visual:** A debit card with an arrow pulling money directly out of a bank account, instant deduction shown. ATM icon beside it.
- **Text overlay:** "Debit = Apna paisa | Account se TURANT katta | Jitna hai utna hi kharch"
- **Style:** Blue theme, account-linked icon, instant-deduction arrow, simplicity mood.

## Slide 3 — Credit card explained
- **Visual:** A credit card drawing money from a bank's lending limit pool, a calendar with a due date, and a monthly bill slip.
- **Text overlay:** "Credit = Bank ka udhaar | Pehle kharch, baad mein bill | Due date tak pura bharo"
- **Style:** Orange theme, limit pool + bill + due-date icons, "zero interest if paid in full" note.

## Slide 4 — Whose money is it
- **Visual:** Two-column split. Debit side: "your money" wallet. Credit side: "bank's money" with a discipline scale (tool vs trap).
- **Text overlay:** "Debit = apna paisa, koi karz nahi | Credit = bank ka paisa, anushasan ka imtihaan"
- **Style:** Two-column, tool-vs-trap metaphor, balanced honest tone.

## Slide 5 — Interest / ब्याज
- **Visual:** Debit = ₹0 interest tag. Credit = two paths: pay full → 0% (green), pay minimum → high % (red flame, ~30-40%).
- **Text overlay:** "Debit: 0% | Credit: pura bharo = 0% | minimum bharo = ~30-40% saalana"
- **Style:** Two-path diagram, green safe path vs red danger path, big % warning.

## Slide 6 — Credit card benefits
- **Visual:** Reward points, cashback coins, fraud-dispute shield, and a rising credit-score meter.
- **Text overlay:** "Credit ✓ Rewards, cashback, dispute easy, credit score banta hai"
- **Style:** Orange theme, benefit icons row, credit-score gauge highlighted.

## Slide 7 — Credit card dangers
- **Visual:** A wallet feeling "light" while a bill grows heavy; a minimum-payment trap spiral pulling someone into debt.
- **Text overlay:** "Khatra: zyada kharch + sirf minimum bharna = karz ka jaal"
- **Style:** Warning red tones, debt-spiral visual, honest caution mood.

## Slide 8 — Real example
- **Visual:** A ₹15,000 phone bought on credit. Path A: full payment → reward + ₹0 interest (green). Path B: minimum only → much higher total (red).
- **Text overlay:** "₹15k phone | Pura bharo = reward + 0 byaaj | Minimum = kahin zyada chukao"
- **Style:** Split-path example, same product two outcomes, fair tone.

## Slide 9 — Debit pros & cons
- **Visual:** Pros/cons card in blue, green ticks vs red crosses, budget-friendly icon.
- **Text overlay:** "Debit ✓ No karz, budget easy, simple  ✗ Kam rewards, refund slow"
- **Style:** Balanced card, fair tone.

## Slide 10 — Credit pros & cons
- **Visual:** Pros/cons card in orange, rewards + credit-score icons, plus a high-interest warning.
- **Text overlay:** "Credit ✓ Rewards, credit score, emergency mohlat  ✗ Bhaari byaaj, over-spend risk"
- **Style:** Balanced card, warning: "Sirf agar pura bill time par bhar sako."

## Slide 11 — Who should pick what
- **Visual:** Two personas — student / learning discipline → Debit; steady income + pays full bill → Credit.
- **Text overlay:** "Anushasan seekh rahe / student → Debit | Niyamit income + pura bill → Credit"
- **Style:** Persona cards, habit as the deciding axis.

## Slide 12 — It's not either/or
- **Visual:** A balanced setup: daily spends + cash on Debit, planned purchases on Credit (bill paid in full). Both cards working together.
- **Text overlay:** "Dono rakho | Rozmarra = Debit | Planned = Credit | Pura bill HAMESHA time par"
- **Style:** Both colours combined, golden-rule banner highlighted.

## Slide 13 — Recap
- **Visual:** Two summary chips: Debit (blue) "apna paisa, safe" vs Credit (orange) "rewards, par anushasan". Centre note: "Card nahi, aadat decide karti hai."
- **Text overlay:** "Debit = simple & safe | Credit = rewards, time par bharo | Aadat se decide karo"
- **Style:** Recap board, takeaway emphasised, balanced reminder.

## Slide 14 — Verdict + CTA + Disclaimer
- **Visual:** Person confidently holding both cards, calm and in control; Katixo KhojLab subscribe button + bell.
- **Text overlay:** "Sahi wahi jo aapki aadat se mile | Katixo KhojLab subscribe karein! | Ye general info hai, financial advice nahi."
- **Style:** Warm closing, big subscribe CTA, disclaimer clearly visible.

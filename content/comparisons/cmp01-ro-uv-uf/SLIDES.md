# CMP01 — RO vs UV vs UF Water Purifier — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Side-by-side VS comparison, two/three columns, check/cross icons, table feel

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A family standing in an electronics showroom looking confused at three water purifiers lined up, big question marks floating above. Three product silhouettes labelled RO, UV, UF.
- **Text overlay:** "RO vs UV vs UF — Sahi Purifier Kaunsa?"
- **Style:** Bold title, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left, three coloured tags (blue RO, purple UV, green UF). Energetic hook mood.

## Slide 2 — Intro: Do tarah ki gandagi
- **Visual:** A glass of water split into two halves — left half shows tiny salt crystals/minerals icons, right half shows germ/bacteria icons.
- **Text overlay:** "Paani mein 2 problem: 1) Ghule salt/TDS  2) Germs"
- **Style:** Clean infographic, two labelled zones, simple icons. Sets up the comparison framework.

## Slide 3 — RO explained
- **Visual:** Cross-section of an RO membrane: water pushed under pressure through a fine barrier, salt crystals + heavy-metal icons blocked on one side, clean drop coming out.
- **Text overlay:** "RO = Reverse Osmosis | Hatata hai: Khaarapan, TDS, Heavy metals | Lekin: Bijli + paani waste"
- **Style:** Blue theme. Diagram with arrows, blocked vs passed icons.

## Slide 4 — UV explained
- **Visual:** A UV lamp glowing inside a water chamber, purple rays hitting bacteria that turn into "X" dead germs; salt crystals still floating untouched.
- **Text overlay:** "UV = Ultraviolet | Maarta hai: Germs | Nahi hatata: Khaarapan/TDS"
- **Style:** Purple theme. Glowing lamp, dead-germ X icons, salt icons greyed but present.

## Slide 5 — UF explained
- **Visual:** UF membrane with slightly larger pores; mud, dirt and large bacteria blocked, but tiny salt + tiny virus dots passing through.
- **Text overlay:** "UF = Ultra Filtration | Rokta: Gandagi, bacteria | Bijli NAHI chahiye | Nahi hatata: Khaarapan"
- **Style:** Green theme. Pore diagram, no-electricity icon highlighted.

## Slide 6 — Cost comparison
- **Visual:** Three-column price table. Columns: RO / UV / UF. Rows: Price, Maintenance/year, Bijli, Paani waste.
- **Text overlay:** "RO ₹10-20k | UV ₹7-12k | UF kuch hazaar | (daam approx)"
- **Style:** Clean table grid, rupee icons, green ticks / red crosses for bijli & waste rows.

## Slide 7 — Real example: TDS decides
- **Visual:** Two houses side by side. House A (city, municipal tap, low TDS) → UV/UF badge. House B (borewell, khaara paani, TDS 500+) → RO badge. A TDS meter icon in centre.
- **Text overlay:** "Pehle TDS check karwao, phir purifier chuno"
- **Style:** Split scene, TDS meter as the deciding instrument, labelled badges.

## Slide 8 — The RO+UV+UF combo
- **Visual:** A single purifier unit with three stacked stages glowing in blue, purple, green; arrows showing water flowing through all three.
- **Text overlay:** "Combo = Complete protection, par sabse mehnga | Lo tabhi jab paani ko teeno chahiye"
- **Style:** Layered cutaway of one machine, three coloured stages labelled.

## Slide 9 — RO pros & cons
- **Visual:** Two-column pros/cons card. Left green ticks, right red crosses.
- **Text overlay:** "RO ✓ Khaarapan/heavy metals hatata  ✗ Bijli, paani waste, minerals bhi nikalte"
- **Style:** Balanced card layout, honest tone, mineral-add-back note small at bottom.

## Slide 10 — UV & UF pros & cons
- **Visual:** Two cards side by side — UV card (purple) and UF card (green), each with tick/cross list.
- **Text overlay:** "UV ✓ Germ ✗ Khaarapan | UF ✓ Sasta, bijli-free ✗ Khaarapan, chhote virus"
- **Style:** Twin cards, colour-coded, symmetric.

## Slide 11 — Who should pick what
- **Visual:** Decision flow: TDS high → RO; low TDS + germ → UV/UF; mud, no electricity → UF; khaara + germ → combo.
- **Text overlay:** "Rule: TDS 500+ → RO | Germ daar → UV/UF | Gandagi+no bijli → UF | Dono → Combo"
- **Style:** Simple flowchart with arrows and icons, 4 branches.

## Slide 12 — Recap
- **Visual:** Three coloured summary chips (RO blue, UV purple, UF green) each with one-line strength, plus a big centre note "Koi ek best nahi — paani ki quality decide karti hai."
- **Text overlay:** "RO=TDS ka boss | UV=germ killer | UF=sasta filter"
- **Style:** Recap board, clean, takeaway emphasised.

## Slide 13 — Verdict + CTA
- **Visual:** Happy family drinking clean water; Katixo KhojLab subscribe button + bell animation cue.
- **Text overlay:** "Sahi purifier = aapke paani ki problem solve kare | Katixo KhojLab subscribe karein!"
- **Style:** Warm closing scene, big subscribe CTA, share icons.

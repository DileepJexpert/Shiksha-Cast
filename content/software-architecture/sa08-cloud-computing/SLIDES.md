# SA08 — Cloud Computing Kya Hai — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Developer/architecture diagrams, dark, boxes-and-arrows, monospace accents
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** Old way: a room of expensive servers with cooling/wires (weeks to scale) vs new way: cloud icon delivering resources in minutes. Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.
- **Text overlay:** "Cloud Computing Kya Hai?"
- **Style:** Bold title, neon red accent on "Cloud", buy-vs-rent motif.
---
## Slide 2 — What is cloud computing
- **Visual:** Resources (servers, storage, DB, network) flowing over the internet from a provider's data center; pay-as-you-go meter.
- **Text overlay:** "Resources on-demand, internet pe"
- **Style:** Data-center → internet → user diagram, AWS/Azure/GCP chips, usage meter.
---
## Slide 3 — Analogy: electricity grid
- **Visual:** A wall switch and meter drawing power from a shared grid; no personal power plant needed.
- **Text overlay:** "Cloud = bijli ki tarah"
- **Style:** Switch + meter illustration, shared-grid arrows, "pay for what you use" caption.
---
## Slide 4 — Benefits
- **Visual:** Five benefit chips — Scalability/Elasticity, Cost saving, Speed, Global reach, Reliability.
- **Text overlay:** "Scale, save, speed, global"
- **Style:** Five-chip grid, neon icons, elasticity up/down arrows.
---
## Slide 5 — Service models intro
- **Visual:** A stack of layers (Hardware → OS → Runtime → App) with three columns IaaS/PaaS/SaaS showing provider taking more of the stack.
- **Text overlay:** "IaaS · PaaS · SaaS"
- **Style:** Layered stack, shaded "managed by provider" regions growing left-to-right.
---
## Slide 6 — IaaS
- **Visual:** Provider gives VMs, storage, networking; you install OS/app/updates. Most control badge. Examples EC2, GCE.
- **Text overlay:** "IaaS = infrastructure rent"
- **Style:** Building-blocks diagram, "you manage OS+app" labels, monospace examples.
---
## Slide 7 — PaaS
- **Visual:** Provider gives full platform (OS+runtime+DB+scaling); you just upload code. Examples Heroku, App Engine, Beanstalk.
- **Text overlay:** "PaaS = ready platform, bas code"
- **Style:** Platform tray with code upload arrow, "provider manages infra" highlight.
---
## Slide 8 — SaaS
- **Visual:** Ready-to-use software in a browser, just login. Examples Gmail, Google Docs, Netflix, Zoom.
- **Text overlay:** "SaaS = ready software, just login"
- **Style:** Browser window mock, app logos row, "end user" label.
---
## Slide 9 — Pizza analogy
- **Visual:** Four pizza panels — On-prem (make all), IaaS (kit + your oven), PaaS (delivery, you set table), SaaS (dine at restaurant).
- **Text overlay:** "Pizza as a Service"
- **Style:** Four illustrated panels, "less hassle going up" arrow, fun caption.
---
## Slide 10 — Deployment models & serverless
- **Visual:** Public/Private/Hybrid cloud chips; plus a serverless function (AWS Lambda) running only when called, pay-per-run.
- **Text overlay:** "Public/Private/Hybrid + Serverless"
- **Style:** Cloud-type chips + lambda icon, "servers hidden" note, monospace `Lambda`.
---
## Slide 11 — Cautions & interview points
- **Visual:** Cost-overrun meter, vendor lock-in chain, shared-responsibility split (provider vs you); "up the stack = less control".
- **Text overlay:** "Cost, lock-in, shared security"
- **Style:** Warning chips, split shield diagram, control-vs-hassle arrow.
---
## Slide 12 — 3-point recap
- **Visual:** Three numbered cards — 1) On-demand resources, pay-as-you-go, 2) IaaS/PaaS/SaaS, 3) Up the stack → provider manages more.
- **Text overlay:** "Recap: 3 points"
- **Style:** Numbered cards, monospace keywords, neon dividers.
---
## Slide 13 — CTA / Outro
- **Visual:** Katixo KhojLab logo centre, subscribe glow, "System Design Series" banner, small cloud icon.
- **Text overlay:** "Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Bold outro, neon subscribe CTA, dark canvas.

# S03 — Your Blood Factory — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_015.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Medical/biology theme, deep red + dark background, anatomical illustrations

---

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** Dramatic close-up of blood cells flowing through a vessel (3D render or microscopy style)
- **Text overlay:** "Your BLOOD FACTORY" in bold white + red glow
- **Stat callout:** "1,00,000 km of blood vessels. 7,500 litres/day."
- **Bottom bar:** Katixo Shiksha logo + "S03" badge
- **Mood:** Cinematic, inside-the-body aesthetic

## Slide 2 — Blood Components Overview
- **Visual:** Test tube or blood drop split into layers:
  - Top 55%: Plasma (yellow/amber)
  - Bottom 45%: Red layer (RBCs), thin white layer (WBCs + platelets)
- **Labels:** Plasma, Red Blood Cells, White Blood Cells, Platelets
- **Text overlay:** "4 Components. 4 Critical Jobs."
- **Style:** Clean infographic with percentage labels

## Slide 3 — Red Blood Cells (RBCs)
- **Visual:** 3D render of biconcave disc-shaped RBCs floating
- **Callout:** "25 TRILLION in your body" + "No nucleus — kicked it out for more cargo space"
- **Diagram:** RBC with haemoglobin molecules inside, oxygen (O₂) attached
- **Text overlay:** "Oxygen Delivery Trucks"
- **Style:** Microscopy aesthetic, deep red cells on dark background

## Slide 4 — Why Blood Is Red
- **Visual:** Haemoglobin protein structure with iron atom highlighted
- **Split comparison:** Oxygenated blood (bright red, O₂ attached) vs Deoxygenated (dark maroon, CO₂)
- **Myth buster callout:** "Your blood is NEVER blue" + diagram showing light penetration through skin
- **Style:** Molecular biology visual, color-coded oxygen/CO₂

## Slide 5 — RBC Shape and Lifespan
- **Visual:** Biconcave disc shape with labels: "Max surface area" + "Flexible — squeezes through capillaries"
- **Timeline:** Birth (bone marrow) → 120 days of service → Recycled (spleen + liver)
- **Stat:** "2 million destroyed per second. 2 million new ones made."
- **Style:** Lifecycle infographic with timeline arrow

## Slide 6 — Bone Marrow: The Factory
- **Visual:** Cross-section of a bone (hip bone or femur) showing spongy red marrow inside
- **Diagram:** Stem cell → differentiates into RBC / WBC / Platelet (branching tree)
- **Stat:** "200 BILLION new red blood cells every day"
- **Text overlay:** "Bone Marrow = Your Blood Factory"
- **Style:** Anatomical cutaway, warm red/orange tones for marrow

## Slide 7 — White Blood Cells (WBCs)
- **Visual:** Military/defence force metaphor — different WBC types as soldiers:
  - Neutrophils = frontline infantry (eating bacteria)
  - Lymphocytes = special ops (B-cells: antibody snipers, T-cells: assassins)
  - Macrophages = cleanup crew (swallowing debris)
- **Text overlay:** "< 1% of Blood. 100% of Defence."
- **Style:** Illustrated battle scene, immune cells vs bacteria

## Slide 8 — Diapedesis (WBCs Leave Blood Vessels)
- **Visual:** Cross-section of blood vessel wall showing a WBC squeezing through the gap between vessel wall cells
- **Step diagram:** Chemical alarm signal → WBC detects → Squeezes through gap → Reaches infection
- **Text overlay:** "White Blood Cells Can Leave the Bloodstream"
- **Style:** Detailed anatomical diagram, dramatic close-up

## Slide 9 — Platelets and Clotting
- **Visual:** Step-by-step wound healing:
  1. Cut in skin → blood vessel exposed
  2. Platelets rush to the site
  3. Platelet plug forms
  4. Fibrin mesh creates solid clot
- **Text overlay:** "12+ Clotting Factors. One Chain Reaction."
- **Style:** Medical illustration, zoomed-in wound cross-section

## Slide 10 — Blood Types
- **Visual:** Grid showing all 8 blood types with antigen markers:
  - A+ A- (A antigens on cell)
  - B+ B- (B antigens)
  - AB+ AB- (both antigens)
  - O+ O- (no antigens)
- **Diagram:** RBC surface with antigen markers illustrated as colored flags/tags
- **Text overlay:** "Wrong Match = Immune System Attacks"
- **Style:** Clean medical chart, color-coded blood types

## Slide 11 — Circulatory System: Two Loops
- **Visual:** Heart diagram showing both circuits:
  - Right side → Pulmonary circuit (to lungs, picks up O₂) → shown in blue/red
  - Left side → Systemic circuit (to whole body) → shown in red/blue
- **Stat callout:** "Left ventricle can squirt blood nearly 10 metres"
- **Text overlay:** "Two Pumps in One Heart"
- **Style:** Anatomical heart diagram, clear color-coded circuits

## Slide 12 — Iron and Anaemia
- **Visual:** Small iron nail illustration + "3-4 grams of iron in your blood"
- **Diagram:** Iron recycling cycle: Old RBC → Spleen breaks down → Iron recovered → Bone marrow reuses
- **Warning box:** "Iron deficiency = anaemia = constant fatigue"
- **Text overlay:** "Every Iron Atom Gets Recycled"
- **Style:** Informational, health awareness aesthetic, orange/rust tones

## Slide 13 — Quiz Slide
- **Q1:** "What protein carries oxygen? What element makes blood red?"
- **Q2:** "Where are new blood cells manufactured?"
- **Q3:** "What determines your blood type?"
- **Text overlay:** "PAUSE & ANSWER" in bold red
- **Style:** Quiz layout on dark background, medical theme

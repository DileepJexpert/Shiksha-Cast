---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A teen with a shocked face, tongue out, cartoon flames around the mouth, watery eyes, holding a red chili.
- **Text overlay:** "Spicy Food: Real Fire or Brain Trick?"
- **Style:** Dark background, fiery red and orange neon flames, bold white title, Katixo Shiksha logo top-left.

---
## Slide 2 — Meet Capsaicin
- **Visual:** A cross-section of a chili pepper highlighting the white placenta where capsaicin lives; molecule diagram floating.
- **Text overlay:** "The Villain: Capsaicin"
- **Style:** Glowing red molecule, cutaway chili in neon outline, dark backdrop.

---
## Slide 3 — TRPV1 Heat Sensor
- **Visual:** A tongue surface zoomed in showing receptor sensors, one labeled TRPV1 glowing like a tiny thermometer.
- **Text overlay:** "TRPV1: Your Heat Detector"
- **Style:** Microscopic tongue texture, neon-orange glowing receptor, thermometer icon at 43°C.

---
## Slide 4 — The Brain Gets Fooled
- **Visual:** Capsaicin molecule opening the TRPV1 receptor like a key, signal racing to a glowing brain shouting "HOT!"
- **Text overlay:** "Brain Thinks: It's Burning!"
- **Style:** Key-and-lock metaphor, neon signal line to brain, dark dramatic background.

---
## Slide 5 — Body's Reactions
- **Visual:** A face showing watery eyes, runny nose, sweating forehead, with small alarm icons around it.
- **Text overlay:** "Tears, Sweat, Runny Nose: False Alarm!"
- **Style:** Expressive face illustration, neon blue droplets, red alarm icons, dark theme.

---
## Slide 6 — The Pleasure Twist
- **Visual:** Brain releasing glowing endorphin and dopamine particles, a happy face emerging after the burn.
- **Text overlay:** "Pain → Endorphins → Pleasure!"
- **Style:** Brain with sparkling neon-pink particles, gradient from red pain to green calm, dark backdrop.

---
## Slide 7 — Milk vs Water
- **Visual:** Split screen: water spreading flames wider on left, milk with casein grabbing capsaicin droplets on right.
- **Text overlay:** "Drink Milk, Not Water!"
- **Style:** Left side fiery, right side cool blue, casein protein shown as little hands, dark divider line.

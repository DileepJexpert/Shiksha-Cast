# S54 — How Does Your Brain Store Memories? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_015.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A glowing human head silhouette in profile against a deep navy background, with a luminous neon-blue brain inside crackling with tiny electric sparks. Faint floating icons of a bicycle and a smiling face orbit the head.
- **Text overlay:** "HOW DOES YOUR BRAIN STORE MEMORIES?"
- **Style:** Large bold title in electric cyan, subtle glow, Katixo Shiksha logo bottom-right. Cinematic dark gradient.

---
## Slide 2 — 86 Billion Neurons
- **Visual:** A dense field of thousands of tiny tree-like neuron shapes glowing across the dark canvas, resembling a city seen from space at night. Zoom-in inset shows one neuron with branching dendrites.
- **Text overlay:** "~86 BILLION NEURONS"
- **Style:** Warm gold neuron glows on deep purple-black. Inset framed with thin neon border. Number in giant bold type.

---
## Slide 3 — Synapses & Signals
- **Visual:** Close-up of two neuron endings facing each other with a glowing gap between them. Bright particle dots (neurotransmitters) stream across the gap. A jagged electric bolt travels down one neuron.
- **Text overlay:** "SYNAPSE: ~TRILLIONS OF CONNECTIONS"
- **Style:** Hot magenta electric bolt, cyan neurotransmitter particles, labels with thin leader lines. High-contrast dark.

---
## Slide 4 — Memory Is a Pattern, Not a File
- **Visual:** Split screen. Left side: a crossed-out grey hard-drive / file icon. Right side: a glowing constellation of many neurons lighting up together in a connected web pattern.
- **Text overlay:** "MEMORY = A PATTERN, NOT A FILE"
- **Style:** Left muted grey with red X, right vivid neon network. Bold contrast to drive the point home.

---
## Slide 5 — Fire Together, Wire Together
- **Visual:** Two neurons repeatedly flashing in sync, with a glowing pathway between them growing thicker frame by frame, shown as a widening trail through a dark forest.
- **Text overlay:** "NEURONS THAT FIRE TOGETHER, WIRE TOGETHER"
- **Style:** Animated-feel progression arrows, warm amber pathway, Hebbian learning label in small caps.

---
## Slide 6 — Long-Term Potentiation (LTP)
- **Visual:** A single synapse shown at three stages, left to right, each with more glowing receptor dots and a brighter, thicker signal beam passing through.
- **Text overlay:** "LTP: PRACTICE STRENGTHENS SYNAPSES"
- **Style:** Three-panel progression, intensifying cyan glow, bold acronym LTP highlighted in neon green.

---
## Slide 7 — Sensory Memory
- **Visual:** A child swinging a sparkler in the dark leaving a glowing light trail in the air, with the trail rapidly fading at the edges.
- **Text overlay:** "SENSORY MEMORY: < 1 SECOND"
- **Style:** Dark night scene, bright sparkler trail, fading motion blur, duration stat in bold.

---
## Slide 8 — Short-Term / Working Memory
- **Visual:** A glowing mental whiteboard floating in dark space showing about 7 small icon-chips (digits and shapes), with a countdown timer reading 15–30s ticking beside it.
- **Text overlay:** "WORKING MEMORY: ~7 ITEMS, ~15–30 SEC"
- **Style:** Neon whiteboard frame, chips in assorted bright colors, ticking timer in red. Clean and punchy.

---
## Slide 9 — Long-Term Memory
- **Visual:** An immense glowing library / vault stretching into infinity in dark space, shelves filled with softly glowing memory orbs labelled with names, languages, and childhood scenes.
- **Text overlay:** "LONG-TERM MEMORY: NEARLY UNLIMITED"
- **Style:** Deep perspective, warm golden orbs against cool dark vault, sense of vast scale.

---
## Slide 10 — The Hippocampus
- **Visual:** A highlighted seahorse-shaped hippocampus glowing inside a translucent brain, with a friendly librarian figure placing glowing memory books onto a shelf as a metaphor inset.
- **Text overlay:** "HIPPOCAMPUS: BUILDS NEW MEMORIES (CONSOLIDATION)"
- **Style:** Brain in cool teal, hippocampus highlighted in bright orange, metaphor inset with neon frame.

---
## Slide 11 — Sleep Consolidates Memory
- **Visual:** A sleeping child under a starry blanket, with the brain gently glowing and faint replayed scenes (numbers, a bicycle, words) drifting upward like dreams being filed away.
- **Text overlay:** "SLEEP STRENGTHENS MEMORIES"
- **Style:** Calm indigo night palette, soft glowing dream-wisps, gentle warm brain glow. Cozy and inviting.

---
## Slide 12 — Muscle Memory & Forgetting
- **Visual:** Split scene. Top: a kid learning to ride a bicycle, wobbling then riding smoothly, with the cerebellum highlighted in the brain. Bottom: a fading, dimming neural pathway labelled forgetting.
- **Text overlay:** "MUSCLE MEMORY (CEREBELLUM) | UNUSED = FADES"
- **Style:** Top energetic and bright, bottom muted and dimming. Cerebellum highlighted in lime green.

---
## Slide 13 — Quiz / Brain Check
- **Visual:** A glowing quiz card on dark background with a large pause icon and three numbered question slots. A cartoon brain wearing a thinking pose sits beside it.
- **Text overlay:** "QUICK BRAIN CHECK! — PAUSE & ANSWER\n1) How many neurons?  2) Name the gap?  3) Which part builds long-term memories?"
- **Style:** Bright quiz-show energy, neon yellow question numbers, bold PAUSE prompt. Playful.

---
## Slide 14 — Answers
- **Visual:** The same quiz card now revealing three glowing green checkmark answers, with a celebratory burst of light particles.
- **Text overlay:** "ANSWERS:\n1) ~86 BILLION NEURONS  2) SYNAPSE  3) HIPPOCAMPUS"
- **Style:** Green correct-answer glow, confetti sparks, confident bold reveal typography.

---
## Slide 15 — Recap & Wrap-Up
- **Visual:** A montage ring of mini-icons from the episode (neuron, synapse, LTP arrows, hippocampus, sleeping child, bicycle) circling a glowing brain. Subscribe button and bell icon pulse at the bottom.
- **Text overlay:** "RECAP: NEURONS • SYNAPSES • LTP • HIPPOCAMPUS • SLEEP\nSUBSCRIBE & SHARE!"
- **Style:** Energetic finale, neon red Subscribe button with bell, Katixo Shiksha logo centered, celebratory glow.

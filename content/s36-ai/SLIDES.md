# S36 — How Does AI Actually Think? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A glowing robot head made of circuit lines, with a question mark forming inside the brain area. Split screen showing a phone with a cat photo recognized as "CAT".
- **Text overlay:** "How Does AI Actually THINK?"
- **Style:** Neon cyan and magenta glow on deep black background, big bold title font, subtle circuit grid texture.

---
## Slide 2 — What is AI really
- **Visual:** A cracked-open robot showing NO tiny human inside, instead lines of code and a flow of data arrows feeding in.
- **Text overlay:** "AI = a program that LEARNS from DATA"
- **Style:** Dark blue gradient, neon green code streams, friendly icon callouts.

---
## Slide 3 — Training with examples
- **Visual:** A grid of thousands of cat photos flowing into a funnel labeled "TRAINING", machine getting brighter as it learns.
- **Text overlay:** "Show it millions of examples → it finds patterns"
- **Style:** Photo mosaic transitioning into glowing neural lines, orange accent funnel.

---
## Slide 4 — Neural Network
- **Visual:** A layered network diagram, circles (neurons) connected by lines, compared side-by-side with a human brain neuron illustration.
- **Text overlay:** "Neural Network — inspired by your BRAIN"
- **Style:** Glowing nodes in cyan, layers labeled Input → Hidden → Output, brain on left in soft purple.

---
## Slide 5 — Weights & backpropagation
- **Visual:** Connection lines with number tags, a feedback loop arrow curving back labeled "fix the mistake".
- **Text overlay:** "Billions of WEIGHTS, tuned by every mistake"
- **Style:** Neon numbers floating on connections, red error arrow looping back, dark grid.

---
## Slide 6 — How ChatGPT works
- **Visual:** A chat bubble with words appearing one at a time, each next word highlighted with a probability percentage above it.
- **Text overlay:** "LLM = predicts the NEXT word, again & again"
- **Style:** Terminal-style chat window, neon green text, percentages glowing yellow.

---
## Slide 7 — Hallucination warning
- **Visual:** A confident robot giving a wrong answer with a big red WARNING triangle, broken fact icon.
- **Text overlay:** "AI can be confidently WRONG — always double-check"
- **Style:** Caution red and black, glitch effect on text, dramatic.

---
## Slide 8 — Scale & power
- **Visual:** Rows of GPU chips glowing in a giant data center, with big numbers floating: 175 billion parameters.
- **Text overlay:** "Trained on 1000s of chips — 175B+ parameters"
- **Style:** Futuristic server room, blue LED rows, huge bold neon numbers.

---
## Slide 9 — AI everywhere
- **Visual:** Icon collage: YouTube, Google Maps, X-ray scan, self-driving car, face unlock phone, all connected by glowing lines.
- **Text overlay:** "AI is already ALL around you"
- **Style:** Hexagon icon grid, multicolor neon edges, connected web.

---
## Slide 10 — Smarter than humans?
- **Visual:** A robot beside a small child; robot does one task fast, child shows a lightbulb of common sense.
- **Text overlay:** "Fast at ONE thing — but no common sense (yet)"
- **Style:** Warm vs cool color contrast, friendly tone, lightbulb glow.

---
## Slide 11 — Quiz
- **Visual:** Big "QUICK BRAIN CHECK!" header with a pause icon and three numbered question cards.
- **Text overlay:** "1) AI's brain? 2) What do LLMs predict? 3) Confident wrong answer = ?"
- **Style:** Quiz-show vibe, neon question marks, bold numbered cards, pause symbol bottom right.

## Slide 12 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three answer cards flipping over with checkmarks.
- **Text overlay:** "1) Neural Network 2) The next word 3) Hallucination"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, celebratory neon confetti, same card layout as quiz.

## Slide 13 — Recap + Next episode

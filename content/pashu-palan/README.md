# Katixo Pashu Palan

This folder is for the separate farming and dairy-focused faceless channel.

Audience: farmers, dairy entrepreneurs, rural business learners, milk buyers, and families who want practical dairy knowledge.

Recommended playlists:

- `milk-purity/` — milk quality, clean milk handling, lactometer, fat and SNF basics
- `dairy-farming/` — cow and buffalo care, shed, fodder, daily routine
- `ghee-making/` — home ghee, bilona ghee, cream ghee, packaging basics
- `goat-farming/` — breeds, shed, feeding, beginner mistakes
- `poultry-farming/` — broiler and layer basics, farm setup, feed awareness
- `rural-business/` — paneer, curd, ghee, milk collection, small dairy business

Content rule:

- Keep videos practical, educational, and non-medical.
- For animal health problems, advise viewers to contact a qualified veterinary doctor.
- For milk purity, explain that home checks are only screening methods and lab testing is the reliable proof.

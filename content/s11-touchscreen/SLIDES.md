# S11 — How Do Touchscreens Feel Your Finger? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** Close-up of a finger about to touch a glowing dark smartphone screen, electric sparks jumping from fingertip to glass.
- **Text overlay:** "How Does a Touchscreen FEEL Your Finger?"
- **Style:** Neon cyan title text, dramatic dark background, glowing fingertip as focal point.

---
## Slide 2 — Capacitive Screen Basics
- **Visual:** Exploded cross-section of a phone screen showing the transparent conductive layer beneath the glass.
- **Text overlay:** "Capacitive Screen = stores electric charge"
- **Style:** Layered diagram, labels in neon green, indium tin oxide layer highlighted.

---
## Slide 3 — The Invisible Charge Sheet
- **Visual:** A phone screen covered with a glowing blue grid of electric field lines, calm and even.
- **Text overlay:** "An invisible electric field covers the whole screen"
- **Style:** Glowing field overlay, pulsing neon blue, dark phone body.

---
## Slide 4 — Your Body Is a Conductor
- **Visual:** Silhouette of a teen with water-drop and salt icons, electricity arc flowing from body to phone.
- **Text overlay:** "Your body is 60% water = conducts electricity!"
- **Style:** Human silhouette in neon outline, charge arc animation freeze-frame.

---
## Slide 5 — The Grid Finds You
- **Visual:** Graph-paper style grid on screen with horizontal and vertical lines, a bright dot at one crossing point.
- **Text overlay:** "Grid lines pinpoint your finger like a map"
- **Style:** Crosshair highlight, neon yellow crossing point, coordinate labels.

---
## Slide 6 — The Touch Controller Chip
- **Visual:** A tiny glowing microchip reading signals, arrows flowing to a larger processor.
- **Text overlay:** "Touch controller → finds the point → tells processor"
- **Style:** Circuit-board aesthetic, glowing data arrows, milliseconds counter.

---
## Slide 7 — Why Gloves Fail
- **Visual:** Hand in a woollen glove tapping a screen with a red X, vs bare finger with a green tick.
- **Text overlay:** "Wool = insulator = no charge = no touch"
- **Style:** Split comparison, red and green neon accents, frosty winter vibe.

---
## Slide 8 — Multi-Touch Magic
- **Visual:** Two fingers pinching to zoom a photo, multiple glowing points on the grid.
- **Text overlay:** "Charge changes in many spots = multi-touch!"
- **Style:** Two glowing touch points, zoom gesture arrows, vibrant neon.

---
## Slide 9 — Water Confuses the Screen
- **Visual:** Water droplets scattered on a screen with random glowing touch points firing.
- **Text overlay:** "Water conducts too → screen gets confused"
- **Style:** Wet screen look, chaotic random sparks, neon magenta accents.

---
## Slide 10 — Full Recap Flow
- **Visual:** A flow diagram: charge sheet → finger → coupling → grid → chip → action.
- **Text overlay:** "Charge → Finger → Grid → Chip → Tap!"
- **Style:** Clean horizontal flowchart, neon arrows connecting each step.

## Slide 11 — Quick Brain Check

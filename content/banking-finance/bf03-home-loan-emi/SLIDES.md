# BF03 — Home Loan EMI Explained — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_012.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Clean fintech infographic, dark navy, teal/green accents, rupee icons, simple charts

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A happy family in front of a new house, with an EMI receipt where most of the bar is shaded "interest".
- **Text overlay:** "Shuruaati EMI Mostly Interest Kyun Hoti Hai?"
- **Style:** Bold title, dark navy, teal house icon, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.

---
## Slide 2 — What is EMI
- **Visual:** One EMI coin splitting into two parts — "Principal" and "Interest".
- **Text overlay:** "EMI = Equated Monthly Instalment | Andar 2 hisse: Principal + Interest"
- **Style:** Split-coin graphic, two labelled segments.

---
## Slide 3 — Three factors
- **Visual:** Three dials: Principal (₹), Rate (%), Tenure (years).
- **Text overlay:** "EMI = Principal × Rate × Tenure | Byaj hamesha BAKI loan par"
- **Style:** Three gauges, outstanding-principal note highlighted in teal.

---
## Slide 4 — Worked example setup
- **Visual:** A loan summary card.
- **Text overlay:** "₹30L Loan | 9% | 20 saal | EMI ≈ ₹26,992/month"
- **Style:** Clean spec card, rupee note, calendar icon.

---
## Slide 5 — Monthly rate
- **Visual:** 9% per year arrow dividing by 12 into 0.75% per month, applied to remaining loan.
- **Text overlay:** "9%/saal ÷ 12 = 0.75%/month | Lagta hai baki loan par"
- **Style:** Division graphic, outstanding-balance bar.

---
## Slide 6 — First EMI breakdown
- **Visual:** A bar of ₹26,992 split: ₹22,500 interest (red, big) + ₹4,492 principal (green, small).
- **Text overlay:** "1st EMI: ₹22,500 interest + ₹4,492 principal = ~83% byaj"
- **Style:** Big stacked bar, red dominating, "83%" callout.

---
## Slide 7 — How it shifts (amortisation)
- **Visual:** Line/area chart over 20 years: interest portion shrinking, principal portion growing.
- **Text overlay:** "Saal-dar-saal: Interest ghatta hai, Principal badhta hai"
- **Style:** Amortisation chart, two coloured areas crossing over.

---
## Slide 8 — Total cost shock
- **Visual:** Two stacked rupee columns — ₹30L principal + ₹34.78L interest = ₹64.78L total.
- **Text overlay:** "20 saal mein total ≈ ₹64.78L | Byaj akela ≈ ₹34.78L!"
- **Style:** Big comparison bars, shock-red interest column.

---
## Slide 9 — Save with prepayment
- **Visual:** An extra rupee chunk slicing the outstanding-principal bar shorter.
- **Text overlay:** "Prepayment = principal ghatao = kam byaj | Floating par penalty nahi"
- **Style:** Scissors cutting the loan bar, savings glow.

---
## Slide 10 — Tenure & rate tips
- **Visual:** Short tenure (bigger EMI, less total) vs long tenure (small EMI, huge total); a balance-transfer arrow between two banks.
- **Text overlay:** "Chhota tenure = lakhon bachat | Rate compare | Balance transfer"
- **Style:** Two-path comparison + bank-to-bank arrow.

---
## Slide 11 — Recap (3 points)
- **Visual:** Three numbered teal cards.
- **Text overlay:** "1. EMI = principal+interest  2. Shuru mein 83% byaj  3. Prepay = bachat"
- **Style:** Clean recap grid, check-circle icons.

---
## Slide 12 — CTA
- **Visual:** Katixo KhojLab logo, subscribe button, calculator + house icons.
- **Text overlay:** "Subscribe Katixo KhojLab! | Ye general educational info hai, financial advice nahi."
- **Style:** Centered CTA, teal subscribe button, small disclaimer at bottom.

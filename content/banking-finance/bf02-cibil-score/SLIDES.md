# BF02 — CIBIL Score Kya Hai — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_012.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Clean fintech infographic, dark navy, teal/green accents, rupee icons, simple charts

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A bank desk where a manager points at a big 3-digit number on screen while a person waits anxiously.
- **Text overlay:** "CIBIL Score: Yeh 3 Digit Loan Dilata Bhi Hai, Rokta Bhi"
- **Style:** Bold title, dark navy, teal score gauge, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.

---
## Slide 2 — What is CIBIL
- **Visual:** CIBIL/TransUnion shield connected to RBI and several banks feeding data into it.
- **Text overlay:** "CIBIL = Credit Bureau (RBI ke under) | Aapka Credit Track Record"
- **Style:** Data-flow diagram, banks feeding arrows into a central bureau icon.

---
## Slide 3 — The 300–900 scale
- **Visual:** Horizontal gauge from 300 (red) to 900 (green) with zones marked.
- **Text overlay:** "300 — 900 | 750+ = Bahut Achha | 600 se neeche = Risk"
- **Style:** Color-graded meter, needle pointing near 780, NA/NH note at side.

---
## Slide 4 — What builds the score
- **Visual:** Pie/donut chart with four slices.
- **Text overlay:** "Payment History 35% | Utilisation | Credit Age | Mix & Enquiries"
- **Style:** Donut chart, largest slice (payment history) highlighted in teal.

---
## Slide 5 — Credit utilisation example
- **Visual:** A credit card with a ₹1,00,000 limit bar; 80% filled in red vs 30% in green.
- **Text overlay:** "Limit ₹1L | 80% use = bura | 30% se neeche rakho"
- **Style:** Two comparison bars, red vs green, rule badge "<30%".

---
## Slide 6 — Why it saves money
- **Visual:** Two home-loan paths: 8.5% (good score) vs 10.5% (weak score) over 20 years, big rupee gap.
- **Text overlay:** "₹20L Home Loan | 8.5% vs 10.5% = Lakhon ka farak"
- **Style:** Split comparison, two interest meters, rupee savings highlight.

---
## Slide 7 — Tip 1: Pay on time
- **Visual:** Calendar with EMI due date circled, auto-pay toggle ON, green tick.
- **Text overlay:** "Har EMI/Bill time par bharo | Auto-pay laga do"
- **Style:** Calendar + toggle graphic, "35% factor" badge.

---
## Slide 8 — Tips 2–4
- **Visual:** Three icons — low utilisation gauge, an old card kept (not cut), and a single apply vs many applies.
- **Text overlay:** "Utilisation <30% | Purane card mat bandh karo | Ek saath apply mat karo"
- **Style:** 3-column tip grid, hard-enquiry caution note.

---
## Slide 9 — Myths busted
- **Visual:** Three myth cards each stamped with a red "MYTH" and a green truth below.
- **Text overlay:** "Khud check karna score nahi girata (soft enquiry)"
- **Style:** Myth vs fact cards, soft vs hard enquiry contrast.

---
## Slide 10 — Check your report yearly
- **Visual:** A report sheet with a magnifier spotting a wrong entry, a "FREE once a year" badge.
- **Text overlay:** "Saal mein 1 baar FREE report check karo | Galti? Dispute raise"
- **Style:** Report document graphic, magnifier, dispute button.

---
## Slide 11 — Recap (3 points)
- **Visual:** Three numbered teal cards.
- **Text overlay:** "1. 300–900 history score  2. On-time pay sabse bada  3. Achha score = paisa bachat"
- **Style:** Clean recap grid, check-circle icons.

---
## Slide 12 — CTA
- **Visual:** Katixo KhojLab logo, subscribe button, rising score arrow.
- **Text overlay:** "Subscribe Katixo KhojLab! | Ye general educational info hai, financial advice nahi."

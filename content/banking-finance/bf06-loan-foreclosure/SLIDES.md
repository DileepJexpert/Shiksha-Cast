# BF06 — What Is Loan Foreclosure / Pre-payment — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_012.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Clean fintech infographic, dark navy, teal/green accents, rupee icons, simple charts

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person holding a bonus envelope, thought bubble showing a home loan with a "close it early?" question mark.
- **Text overlay:** "Extra Paisa Aaya — Loan Jaldi Khatam Karein?"
- **Style:** Bold title, dark navy, teal accents, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.

---
## Slide 2 — Two Terms Defined
- **Visual:** Split card: left "Pre-payment / Part-payment = thoda-thoda extra", right "Foreclosure = pura ek saath".
- **Text overlay:** "Pre-payment vs Foreclosure"
- **Style:** Clean two-column comparison, icons of coins (small drops vs one big lump).

---
## Slide 3 — Interest on Outstanding
- **Visual:** A shrinking principal bar; as it shrinks, the interest slice shrinks too, green savings highlighted.
- **Text overlay:** "Principal Ghata = Interest Ghata"
- **Style:** Bar shrink animation still, teal/green savings callout.

---
## Slide 4 — Amortization (Early = Better)
- **Visual:** Stacked bar chart across years: early years mostly interest (red), later years mostly principal (green).
- **Text overlay:** "Shuru Ke Saal = Zyada Interest = Jaldi Bharo"
- **Style:** Amortization chart, clear red/green split, arrow pointing to early years.

---
## Slide 5 — Worked Example: Part-payment
- **Visual:** Loan box "₹20L @ 9% / 20yr, EMI ≈ ₹17,995", one extra EMI/year arrow → timeline shrinks 20yr → ~17yr.
- **Text overlay:** "1 Extra EMI/Saal → 20 Saal Se 17 Saal"
- **Style:** Timeline graphic, savings highlighted in green.

---
## Slide 6 — Worked Example: Foreclosure
- **Visual:** Year-8 outstanding ₹14L lump paid → future interest crossed out; small note "emergency fund bachao".
- **Text overlay:** "Pura ₹14L Chukao → Aage Ka Interest Bach Gaya"
- **Style:** Big strike-through on future interest, caution note in amber.

---
## Slide 7 — RBI Rule: No Penalty on Floating Home Loan
- **Visual:** RBI shield icon; green check on "Floating Home Loan = 0 penalty", amber tag on "Fixed/Personal = 2–5%".
- **Text overlay:** "Floating Home Loan = No Penalty (RBI)"
- **Style:** Official trust tone, navy + green, small print for fixed-rate exception.

---
## Slide 8 — 3 Practical Tips
- **Visual:** Three numbered cards: (1) Confirm charges + reduce tenure, (2) Keep emergency fund, (3) Do the math vs investing.
- **Text overlay:** "3 Smart Tips Pre-payment Ke Liye"
- **Style:** Tip cards row, teal badges, checkmarks.

---
## Slide 9 — Common Mistake: No Emergency Fund
- **Visual:** A person who emptied savings into a loan, then forced to take a costly new loan; warning loop.
- **Text overlay:** "Pehle Emergency Fund, Phir Loan"
- **Style:** Caution red-amber, 3–6 months fund jar icon.

---
## Slide 10 — Priority: Clear Costliest Loan First
- **Visual:** Ranked bars of interest rates: credit card 40% / personal 14% / home 9%, arrow to clear top first.
- **Text overlay:** "Sabse Mehnga Karza Pehle Khatam Karo"
- **Style:** Ranked bar chart, red at top, green at bottom.

---
## Slide 11 — Quick Recap (3 Points)
- **Visual:** Three recap bars: 1) jaldi = zyada bachat, 2) floating = no penalty, 3) emergency fund + record.
- **Text overlay:** "Recap: Jaldi Bharo · No Penalty · Fund Rakho"
- **Style:** Clean checklist, teal ticks.

---
## Slide 12 — CTA / Disclaimer
- **Visual:** Katixo KhojLab logo, subscribe button, mascot cutting a loan chain with scissors (debt-free).
- **Text overlay:** "Subscribe Karein — Katixo KhojLab" + small "Ye general educational info hai, financial advice nahi."
- **Style:** Bright CTA, red subscribe button, small grey disclaimer at bottom.

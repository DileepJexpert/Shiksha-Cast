# BF04 — NEFT vs RTGS vs IMPS vs UPI — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_012.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Clean fintech infographic, dark navy, teal/green accents, rupee icons, simple charts

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A phone payment screen with four buttons — NEFT, RTGS, IMPS, UPI — a confused emoji hovering.
- **Text overlay:** "NEFT vs RTGS vs IMPS vs UPI — Farak Kya Hai?"
- **Style:** Bold title, dark navy, four teal chips, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.

---
## Slide 2 — Who runs them
- **Visual:** Two columns — RBI shield over NEFT & RTGS, NPCI shield over IMPS & UPI.
- **Text overlay:** "NEFT & RTGS = RBI | IMPS & UPI = NPCI | Sab safe & regulated"
- **Style:** Two grouped boxes under regulator shields.

---
## Slide 3 — NEFT
- **Visual:** Clock with half-hour batch icon, "24x7" badge, no-limit note.
- **Text overlay:** "NEFT: 24x7 par batch mein settle | No RBI limit"
- **Style:** Card layout, batch/clock graphic, teal accent.

---
## Slide 4 — RTGS
- **Visual:** A big rupee bag with a "real-time" lightning bolt, "min ₹2 Lakh" badge.
- **Text overlay:** "RTGS: Real-time, badi rakam | Minimum ₹2 Lakh"
- **Style:** Card layout, large-amount visual, lightning icon.

---
## Slide 5 — IMPS
- **Visual:** Instant transfer arrow between two account icons, "up to ~₹5 Lakh" badge.
- **Text overlay:** "IMPS: Instant, 24x7 | Account no. se, ~₹5 Lakh tak"
- **Style:** Card layout, instant-bolt, account-to-account arrow.

---
## Slide 6 — UPI
- **Visual:** QR code + UPI ID with no account-number clutter, "~₹1 Lakh/day" badge.
- **Text overlay:** "UPI: Sirf ID/QR | Rozmarra ke payment | ~₹1 Lakh/day"
- **Style:** Card layout, QR graphic, simplicity highlight.

---
## Slide 7 — Comparison table
- **Visual:** 4-column comparison table: Speed, Min/Max amount, Best for, Regulator.
- **Text overlay:** "Speed | Amount | Best for | Regulator — sab ek nazar mein"
- **Style:** Clean grid table, alternating navy rows, teal headers.

---
## Slide 8 — Which one when (example)
- **Visual:** Three scenarios — ₹200 shopkeeper (UPI), ₹50k to friend (IMPS/UPI), ₹25L house (RTGS).
- **Text overlay:** "₹200 → UPI | ₹50k → IMPS/UPI | ₹25L → RTGS"
- **Style:** Three scenario cards with matching tool tags.

## Slide 5 — What Is SIP
- **Visual:** A calendar with a fixed date marked; auto-debit arrow from bank to fund each month (₹500/₹1,000).
- **Text overlay:** "SIP = Har Mahina Auto Nivesh"
- **Style:** Recurring loop graphic, auto-pay clock, friendly tone.

## Slide 6 — Rupee Cost Averaging
- **Visual:** Wavy market line; at low NAV more units bought (more coins), at high NAV fewer; averaged out.

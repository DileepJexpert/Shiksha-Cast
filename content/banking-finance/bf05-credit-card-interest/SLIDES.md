# BF05 — How Credit Card Interest Really Works — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_012.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Clean fintech infographic, dark navy, teal/green accents, rupee icons, simple charts

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A worried person staring at a phone showing a huge credit card bill, red shock lines around the number.
- **Text overlay:** "Credit Card Interest: Itna Bill Kaise Aaya?!"
- **Style:** Bold title centered, dark navy background, glowing teal accent on the card icon, Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.

---
## Slide 2 — Good News: Interest-Free Period
- **Visual:** A calendar with 18–50 days highlighted in green, a "₹0 interest" stamp on it.
- **Text overlay:** "Pura Bill Bharo = 0% Interest (Grace Period)"
- **Style:** Friendly green tones, smiling card mascot, clean sans-serif.

---
## Slide 3 — Monthly Rate vs APR
- **Visual:** A small "3% / month" tag morphing into a big red "≈36–45% / year" gauge.
- **Text overlay:** "Monthly Rate Chhota, Saal Bhar Mein Bada"
- **Style:** Speedometer-style gauge, red zone on the high end, RBI-disclosure note tag.

---
## Slide 4 — The Big Myth
- **Visual:** A myth-vs-reality split panel; left "sirf bacha hua amount" crossed out, right "PURA outstanding" highlighted red.
- **Text overlay:** "Myth: Sirf Bachi Rakam Pe Interest — GALAT"
- **Style:** Split-screen, red X on the myth side, teal check on reality.

---
## Slide 5 — Worked Example (₹30,000)
- **Visual:** Step-by-step number flow: ₹30,000 → minimum due ₹1,500 → outstanding ₹28,500 → interest ≈ ₹998.
- **Text overlay:** "₹30,000 Shopping → Min Due → +₹998 Interest"
- **Style:** Arrow flow chart, rupee coins, the rising interest in red.

---
## Slide 6 — Cash Withdrawal Trap
- **Visual:** A credit card stuck in an ATM with a red "no grace period" flag and a fee tag (2.5–3%).
- **Text overlay:** "Card Se Cash = Din 1 Se Interest + Fee"
- **Style:** Warning yellow-red accents, ATM illustration, fee callout bubble.

---
## Slide 7 — Late Payment Double Hit
- **Visual:** Two falling hammers labelled "Late Fee ₹500–1,300" and "CIBIL Score Down", a declining score meter.
- **Text overlay:** "Late Payment = Fee + Score Girta Hai"
- **Style:** Impact graphic, downward red arrow on CIBIL gauge.

---
## Slide 8 — 3 Practical Tips
- **Visual:** Three numbered cards: (1) Pay total due, (2) Auto-pay/reminder, (3) Use <30% limit.
- **Text overlay:** "3 Aadtein Jo Aapko Bachayengi"
- **Style:** Three clean tip cards in a row, teal numbered badges, checkmark icons.

---
## Slide 9 — EMI Conversion & Balance Transfer
- **Visual:** A big 40% interest balloon shrinking into a small 12–14% EMI box; a second card with "low/zero offer".
- **Text overlay:** "Bada Bill? EMI Ya Balance Transfer Socho"
- **Style:** Before/after comparison, green savings highlight, small "read processing fee" footnote.

---
## Slide 10 — Common Mistake: Card-to-Card Cycle
- **Visual:** Money looping endlessly between two cards in a circular arrow, labelled "Debt Jaal".
- **Text overlay:** "Ek Card Ka Bill Doosre Se? Jaal Hai"
- **Style:** Circular loop graphic, muted warning red, simple flat icons.

---
## Slide 11 — Quick Recap (3 Points)
- **Visual:** Three stacked recap bars: pay full = 0%, min due = trap, avoid cash/late.
- **Text overlay:** "Recap: 1) Pura Bharo 2) Min Due Jaal 3) Cash/Late Se Bacho"
- **Style:** Clean checklist, teal ticks, dark navy background.

---
## Slide 12 — CTA / Disclaimer
- **Visual:** Katixo KhojLab logo big, subscribe button, smiling mascot giving thumbs up next to a healthy credit card.
- **Text overlay:** "Subscribe Karein — Katixo KhojLab" + small "Ye general educational info hai, financial advice nahi."
- **Style:** Bright CTA, red subscribe button, disclaimer in small grey text at bottom.

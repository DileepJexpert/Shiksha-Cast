# BF01 — UPI Kaise Kaam Karta Hai — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_012.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Clean fintech infographic, dark navy, teal/green accents, rupee icons, simple charts

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** Hand holding a phone scanning a chai-stall QR code, coins flying out of the screen as a glowing arrow.
- **Text overlay:** "UPI: 2 Second Mein Paisa Kaise Pahunchta Hai?"
- **Style:** Big bold title, dark navy background, teal QR glow, small Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.

---
## Slide 2 — What is UPI
- **Visual:** Central UPI logo with lines connecting to RBI and NPCI shields and several bank icons.
- **Text overlay:** "UPI = Unified Payments Interface | Banaya: NPCI (2016)"
- **Style:** Network diagram, teal connectors, clean labels.

---
## Slide 3 — UPI ID and PIN
- **Visual:** A phone card showing a VPA "yourname@oksbi" and a masked 6-digit PIN box with a lock icon.
- **Text overlay:** "UPI ID (VPA) + Secret UPI PIN = Aapki Security"
- **Style:** Two stacked cards, padlock accent in green.

---
## Slide 4 — How money travels in 1 second
- **Visual:** Flow: Your App → NPCI (traffic-police icon) → Sender Bank + Receiver Bank → green tick.
- **Text overlay:** "App → NPCI → Dono Banks → Paisa Transfer (Seconds Mein, 24x7)"
- **Style:** Horizontal flowchart with animated-style arrows, stopwatch icon.

---
## Slide 5 — Worked example
- **Visual:** Rahul (SBI) sending ₹500 to Priya (HDFC); ₹500 debit and credit shown with SMS bubbles.
- **Text overlay:** "Rahul (SBI) → ₹500 → Priya (HDFC) | Dono ko SMS"
- **Style:** Split screen, two bank cards, rupee note graphic.

---
## Slide 6 — QR code and Pay vs Collect
- **Visual:** QR code revealing a hidden UPI ID; two buttons "Pay" (money out) and "Collect/Request" (money in).
- **Text overlay:** "QR = ID chhupi hoti hai | Pay = bhejna, Request = koi maang raha"
- **Style:** QR with magnifier, red arrow for Pay, caution highlight on Request.

---
## Slide 7 — Limits and charges
- **Visual:** Speedometer-style gauge showing daily limit; a big "₹0 Charges — FREE" badge.
- **Text overlay:** "Daily Limit ~₹1 Lakh | Aam log ke liye FREE"
- **Style:** Gauge graphic, green free badge, small India digital-payments stat ribbon.

---
## Slide 8 — Safety rule #1
- **Visual:** Crossed-out PIN pad with text "Paisa lene ke liye PIN nahi"; a fraudster icon with red cross.
- **Text overlay:** "Receive ke liye PIN/Scan KABHI nahi | Warna = Fraud"
- **Style:** Bold red warning band, shield icon.

---
## Slide 9 — Common mistakes
- **Visual:** Three icons — wrong ID, public WiFi, fake cashback link — each with a red X.
- **Text overlay:** "Galtiyan: Galat ID | Public WiFi | Lalch wale link"
- **Style:** 3-column grid, red X marks, simple flat icons.

---

- **Text overlay:** "1. NPCI chalata hai  2. ID+PIN se transfer  3. Lene ke liye PIN nahi"

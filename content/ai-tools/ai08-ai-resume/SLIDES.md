# AI08 — AI Se Resume Aur Cover Letter Banayein — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_012.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Modern tech, dark UI, neon accents, screenshots/icons, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A blank confused resume sheet on the left transforming into a clean, glowing professional resume + cover letter on the right, sparkle particles around it.
- **Text overlay:** "AI Se Resume Aur Cover Letter Kaise Banayein?"
- **Style:** Big bold title, dark UI background, neon blue/cyan glow, small Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.

---
## Slide 2 — What AI does here
- **Visual:** A person handing details (education, skills, experience cards) into an AI icon, which outputs a neatly formatted resume.
- **Text overlay:** "Jaankari aap ki, Sajaana AI ka"
- **Style:** Left-to-right flow, neon arrow, clean icons.

---
## Slide 3 — Useful tools
- **Visual:** Logo cards in a grid: ChatGPT (content), Canva (templates), Google Docs (templates), with a "content + template = best" note.
- **Text overlay:** "Content: ChatGPT | Design: Canva / Google Docs"
- **Style:** Card grid, neon outline on each card, "FREE" tag badges.

---
## Slide 4 — Step by step (resume)
- **Visual:** Three numbered screenshots: 1) give details in ChatGPT, 2) clear instruction for a 1-page resume, 3) paste into a Canva template and download PDF.
- **Text overlay:** "Details do → Resume banwao → Template + PDF"
- **Style:** Numbered steps, cyan highlights on buttons.

---
## Slide 5 — Cover letter
- **Visual:** A short letter card with a highlighted line "why I'm right for this job", and a job-description card feeding a keyword into it.
- **Text overlay:** "Cover Letter = Chhoti, job se match karti chitthi"
- **Style:** Letter mockup, neon highlight on key line.

---
## Slide 6 — Worked example (fresher receptionist)
- **Visual:** A ChatGPT prompt card on top; below, two output cards side by side: a 1-page resume and a short cover letter.
- **Text overlay:** "Example: 12th-pass Fresher → Receptionist Job"
- **Style:** Prompt card on top, two result cards below, green tick on completion.

---
## Slide 7 — Tips
- **Visual:** Three tip cards with icons: a "match keywords to job" target, a single-page document, bullet-point lines.
- **Text overlay:** "Job ke hisaab se | Ek page | Points me likho"
- **Style:** Three horizontal tip cards, icon + short line each.

---
## Slide 8 — Cautions
- **Visual:** Amber warning panel with three icons: a crossed-out fake degree/skill, a contact-details checkmark, an eye reviewing the final copy.
- **Text overlay:** "Jhooth hatao | Contact check | Khud padho"
- **Style:** Red/amber warning accents on dark UI.

---
## Slide 9 — Who it helps
- **Visual:** Four mini personas: fresher, experienced professional, student/intern, anyone needing a quick email/letter.
- **Text overlay:** "Fresher | Experienced | Student | Email/Letter"
- **Style:** Four-panel collage, neon dividers.

---
## Slide 10 — Reality check
- **Visual:** A confident person at an interview pointing to their resume, speech bubble showing they know every line; a small "real skills" badge.
- **Text overlay:** "Resume wahi acha jo aap jaante ho"
- **Style:** Friendly illustration, calm cyan tone.

## Slide 11 — Recap (3 points)

- **Text overlay:** "1. Content + template  2. Job ke hisaab se  3. Sach & check"

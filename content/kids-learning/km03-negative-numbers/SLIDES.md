# KM03 — Positive aur Negative Numbers, Zero Ke Aage Peeche — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Bright, friendly, colorful, big numbers, cartoon icons
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A snowy mountain with a thermometer showing "-5°C", a shivering cartoon kid in a cap, snowflakes falling. A big "0" in the center with arrows pointing left (-) and right (+). Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.
- **Text overlay:** "Positive aur Negative Numbers!"  (big bubbly letters)
- **Style:** Cool icy blue background, playful and energetic.

## Slide 2 — Zero is King
- **Visual:** A cartoon "0" wearing a crown in the center. To the right glowing green +1, +2, +3; to the left glowing red -1, -2, -3.
- **Text overlay:** "Daaye = Positive, Baaye = Negative"
- **Style:** Symmetrical layout, green right side, red left side.

## Slide 3 — The Number Line
- **Visual:** A long horizontal number line from -5 to +5 with 0 in the middle, a cartoon frog hopping rightward getting bigger and leftward getting smaller.
- **Text overlay:** "Number Line = tumhari best dost"
- **Style:** Clean number line, friendly hopping mascot, arrows on both ends.

## Slide 4 — Which is Bigger
- **Visual:** Number line with -5 and -2 marked; a thumbs-up on -2 (right side), thumbs-down note on -5. Small label "right side = bigger".
- **Text overlay:** "Daaye wala hamesha bada! (-2 > -5)"
- **Style:** Highlight glow on the right-side number, blackboard tone.

## Slide 5 — Worked Example 1 (Compare -2 vs -7)
- **Visual:** Number line showing -2 closer to 0 and -7 far left; two thermometers -2°C (less cold) and -7°C (more cold). -2 circled with a star.
- **Text overlay:** "-2 > -7 (-2 kam thanda!)"
- **Style:** Icy comparison scene, "Example 1" badge in corner.

## Slide 6 — Worked Example 2 (3 - 5)
- **Visual:** A mascot standing on number line: 3 steps right to +3 (green arrow), then 5 steps left (red arrow) landing on -2.
- **Text overlay:** "3 - 5 = -2 (peeche chalo!)"
- **Style:** Footstep trail on number line, "Example 2" badge.

## Slide 7 — Real Life (Elevator / Lift)
- **Visual:** A building cross-section with a lift: floors +3, +2, +1, Ground (0), -1, -2 (basement parking with cars). A kid in the lift at -2.
- **Text overlay:** "Basement = Negative floors!"
- **Style:** Cutaway building, friendly lift cartoon, clear floor labels.

## Slide 8 — Worked Example 3 (-3 + 5 temperature)
- **Visual:** Thermometer rising from -3°C, sun coming out, number line showing 5 hops right from -3 to +2. "+2" circled.
- **Text overlay:** "-3 + 5 = 2 (thand se garmi!)"
- **Style:** Sunrise warm tones over icy base, "Example 3" badge.

## Slide 9 — Opposite Numbers
- **Visual:** Number line with 4 and -4 both four steps from 0, a double-headed arrow showing equal distance, and "4 + (-4) = 0" with a balance scale.
- **Text overlay:** "4 aur -4 = opposite | jodo = 0"
- **Style:** Symmetrical mirror look, balanced scale visual.

## Slide 10 — Chalo Try Karein! (Practice)
- **Visual:** Friendly mascot holding a PAUSE sign. Three question cards: "-4 ya -1 bada?", "-2 + 6 = ?", "4 ka opposite?".
- **Text overlay:** "Chalo Try Karein! (Pause karo)"
- **Style:** Quiz-show feel, bright question cards, big PAUSE icon.

## Slide 11 — Answers
- **Visual:** Same three cards revealed: "-1 bada", "4", "-4", each with a green tick, a small number line, and a celebrating mascot.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: -1, 4, -4 — Shabaash!"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, celebratory sparkles.

## Slide 12 — Quick Recap
- **Visual:** Checklist board: 0 in middle, right=positive/left=negative, right side bigger, add=go right/subtract=go left, opposites add to 0.
- **Text overlay:** "Recap: Number line yaad rakho!"
- **Style:** Notebook / blackboard look, neat checklist.

## Slide 13 — CTA / Subscribe
- **Visual:** Happy mascot giving a thumbs up beside a big red SUBSCRIBE button and bell icon, plus and minus number confetti raining down. Katixo KhojLab logo center.
- **Text overlay:** "Shabaash! Katixo KhojLab Subscribe karo!"
- **Style:** Bright celebration, YouTube subscribe colors, joyful.

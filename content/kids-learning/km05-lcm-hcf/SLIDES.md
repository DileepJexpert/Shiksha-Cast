# KM05 — LCM aur HCF, Ekdum Aasaan Tarike Se — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Bright, friendly, colorful, big numbers, cartoon icons
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** Two cartoon kids running on a circular track at different speeds, plus two bells ringing with sound waves. Confetti and stars. Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.
- **Text overlay:** "LCM aur HCF!"  (big bubbly letters)
- **Style:** Bright blue and orange background, playful and energetic.

## Slide 2 — Factors aur Multiples
- **Visual:** On the left "12" with its factor blocks 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12 falling out neatly. On the right "4" with a table strip 4, 8, 12, 16, 20.
- **Text overlay:** "Factors = bhaajak | Multiples = table"
- **Style:** Split screen, factors in green blocks, multiples in blue strip.

## Slide 3 — HCF Kya Hai
- **Visual:** Two number circles overlapping (Venn style), the overlap glowing with the word "Common". A big arrow points to the largest common number. Label "Highest = sabse bada".
- **Text overlay:** "HCF = Highest Common Factor"
- **Style:** Clean white background, glowing overlap, bold labels.

## Slide 4 — HCF Worked Example (12 & 18)
- **Visual:** 12's factors row (1,2,3,4,6,12) and 18's factors row (1,2,3,6,9,18); the commons 1,2,3,6 highlighted, and 6 circled big with a star.
- **Text overlay:** "12 aur 18 ka HCF = 6"
- **Style:** Example badge in corner, yellow highlight on commons.

## Slide 5 — LCM Kya Hai
- **Visual:** Two table strips growing rightward, meeting at a glowing smallest common number. Label "Lowest = sabse chhota".
- **Text overlay:** "LCM = Lowest Common Multiple"
- **Style:** Two colored tracks merging, friendly arrow pointing to first meet point.

## Slide 6 — LCM Worked Example (4 & 6)
- **Visual:** 4's table (4,8,12,16,20,24) and 6's table (6,12,18,24,30); commons 12 and 24 highlighted, 12 circled big with a star.
- **Text overlay:** "4 aur 6 ka LCM = 12"
- **Style:** Example badge in corner, green highlight on smallest common.

## Slide 7 — LCM Real Life (Bells)
- **Visual:** Two bells, one labeled "har 4 min", other "har 6 min", a clock showing 12 minutes with both bells ringing together and sound bursts.
- **Text overlay:** "Dono ghantiyaan = 12 min baad ek saath!"
- **Style:** Warm classroom scene, motion lines on bells.

## Slide 8 — HCF Real Life (Bags)
- **Visual:** 12 toffees and 18 chocolates being sorted into 6 identical bags, each bag showing 2 toffees + 3 chocolates.
- **Text overlay:** "12 toffees + 18 chocolates = 6 bags"
- **Style:** Cheerful shop scene, neat equal bags lined up.

## Slide 9 — Bigger Example (8 & 12)
- **Visual:** Factors of 8 and 12 with common 4 circled (HCF), tables of 8 and 12 with common 24 circled (LCM). Bottom strip: "4 × 24 = 96 = 8 × 12".
- **Text overlay:** "HCF × LCM = dono numbers ka product!"
- **Style:** Two-panel layout, magic-trick sparkle on the product line.

## Slide 10 — Chalo Try Karein! (Practice)
- **Visual:** Friendly mascot holding a PAUSE sign. Three question cards: "6 & 9 ka HCF?", "3 & 5 ka LCM?", "10 & 15 ka HCF?".
- **Text overlay:** "Chalo Try Karein! (Pause karo)"
- **Style:** Quiz-show feel, bright question cards, big PAUSE icon.

## Slide 11 — Answers
- **Visual:** Same three cards revealed: "HCF = 3", "LCM = 15", "HCF = 5", each with a green tick and a celebrating mascot.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: 3, 15, 5 — Shabaash!"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, celebratory sparkles.

## Slide 12 — Quick Recap
- **Visual:** Checklist board: HCF = biggest common factor (sharing equally), LCM = smallest common multiple (meeting time), "factors for HCF, tables for LCM".
- **Text overlay:** "Recap: HCF factors, LCM tables!"
- **Style:** Notebook / blackboard look, neat checklist.

## Slide 13 — CTA / Subscribe
- **Visual:** Happy mascot giving a thumbs up beside a big red SUBSCRIBE button and bell icon, number confetti raining down. Katixo KhojLab logo center.
- **Text overlay:** "Shabaash! Katixo KhojLab Subscribe karo!"
- **Style:** Bright celebration, YouTube subscribe colors, joyful.

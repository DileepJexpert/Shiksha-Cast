# KM01 — Fractions (Bhinn) Kya Hote Hain — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_012.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Bright, friendly, colorful, big numbers, simple cartoon icons (pizza/chocolate for fractions etc.)
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** Big smiling cartoon pizza in the center with friendly eyes, a few kids reaching for slices, confetti and stars around. Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.
- **Text overlay:** "Fractions = Bhinn!"  (big bubbly letters)
- **Style:** Bright orange and yellow background, playful, energetic.

## Slide 2 — What is a Fraction
- **Visual:** A full chocolate bar on the left, then an arrow, then the same bar broken into 4 equal pieces on the right. A green tick on equal pieces, a red cross on unequal pieces.
- **Text overlay:** "Barabar hisse = Fraction"
- **Style:** Brown chocolate tones on a soft cream background.

## Slide 3 — How to Write a Fraction
- **Visual:** A giant fraction "3/8" in the middle, with a label arrow to top number saying "Numerator" and bottom number saying "Denominator". A pizza cut into 8 slices beside it with 3 slices highlighted.
- **Text overlay:** "Upar = Numerator, Neeche = Denominator"
- **Style:** Clean white background, bold black fraction line, colorful labels.

## Slide 4 — Worked Example 1 (Pizza 1/4)
- **Visual:** Pizza cut into 4 equal slices, 1 slice lifted out and glowing. Big "1/4" beside it.
- **Text overlay:** "1 slice liya = 1/4 (ek-chauthai)"
- **Style:** Warm pizza colors, step number "Example 1" badge in corner.

## Slide 5 — Worked Example 2 (Chocolate 6 pieces)
- **Visual:** Chocolate bar of 6 squares; 2 squares shown going to a little sister, 4 squares staying. Labels "2/6" and "4/6", and "6/6 = poora!".
- **Text overlay:** "2/6 + 4/6 = 6/6 = Poora!"
- **Style:** Chocolate brown, cheerful sibling cartoons.

## Slide 6 — Worked Example 3 (Roti halves & thirds)
- **Visual:** One roti split into 2 equal halves labeled "1/2 = aadha", and another roti split into 3 equal parts labeled "1/3 = ek-tihai".
- **Text overlay:** "Zyada hisse = chhota tukda"
- **Style:** Soft golden-brown roti on a plate, kitchen vibe.

## Slide 7 — Equal Fractions
- **Visual:** Two identical pizzas side by side; left one cut in 2 with 1 shaded, right one cut in 4 with 2 shaded — both shaded areas same size, big "=" between them.
- **Text overlay:** "1/2 = 2/4 (Equal Fractions!)"
- **Style:** Side-by-side comparison, glowing equals sign.

## Slide 8 — Chalo Try Karein! (Practice)
- **Visual:** A friendly cartoon mascot holding a pause sign. Three numbered question cards: pizza (8 slices, 5 eaten), chocolate (10 pieces, 3 given), marbles (6 total, 2 red).
- **Text overlay:** "Chalo Try Karein! (Pause karo)"
- **Style:** Quiz-show feel, bright question cards, big PAUSE icon.

## Slide 9 — Answers
- **Visual:** Same three question cards now with answers revealed: "5/8", "3/10", "2/6", each with a green tick and a small celebrating mascot.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: 5/8, 3/10, 2/6 — Shabaash!"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, celebratory sparkles.

## Slide 10 — Fractions in Real Life
- **Visual:** A collage: half glass of milk, a clock showing half hour, a cake being shared, a pizza box. Each tagged with its fraction.
- **Text overlay:** "Fractions har jagah hain!"
- **Style:** Everyday home scene, warm and relatable.

## Slide 11 — Quick Recap
- **Visual:** A checklist board with 5 ticked points and tiny icons: fraction = equal part, denominator (bottom), numerator (top), big denominator = small piece, 1/2 = 2/4.
- **Text overlay:** "Recap: 5 baatein yaad rakho!"
- **Style:** Notebook / blackboard look, neat checklist.

## Slide 12 — CTA / Subscribe
- **Visual:** Happy mascot giving a thumbs up beside a big red SUBSCRIBE button and a bell icon, pizza and chocolate confetti raining down. Katixo KhojLab logo center.
- **Text overlay:** "Shabaash! Katixo KhojLab Subscribe karo!"
- **Style:** Bright celebration, YouTube subscribe colors, joyful.

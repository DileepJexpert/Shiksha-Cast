# KM04 — Multiplication Tables Ki Tricks, Ab Yaad Karna Aasaan — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Bright, friendly, colorful, big numbers, cartoon icons
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A cheerful cartoon kid wearing a magician hat pulling number cards (2, 5, 9, 10) out of a hat, sparkles and stars all around. A big "×" symbol glowing. Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.
- **Text overlay:** "Tables Ki Magic Tricks!"  (big bubbly letters)
- **Style:** Bright magenta and gold background, magical and energetic.

## Slide 2 — What is Multiplication
- **Visual:** Three plates, each with 4 samosas, and the equation "4 + 4 + 4 = 12" turning into "4 × 3 = 12" with an arrow.
- **Text overlay:** "Multiplication = tez jodna"
- **Style:** Warm food illustration, equal groups clearly shown.

## Slide 3 — Trick: Table of 2 (Double)
- **Visual:** A number "7" being mirrored/cloned into two 7s with "7 + 7 = 14", and "2 × 7 = 14" beside it.
- **Text overlay:** "×2 = Double karo!"
- **Style:** Mirror/twin effect, bright blue, "Trick 1" badge.

## Slide 4 — Trick: Table of 10 and 5
- **Visual:** "5" with a zero sliding behind it to make "50" (10×5). Below, a strip of 5's table 5,10,15,20,25 with last digits 5 and 0 highlighted.
- **Text overlay:** "×10 = peeche zero | ×5 = 5 ya 0 par"
- **Style:** Zero-sliding animation feel, green and yellow, "Trick 2" badge.

## Slide 5 — Trick: Table of 9 (Fingers)
- **Visual:** Two open cartoon hands, the 3rd finger from left folded down; left fingers labeled "2", right fingers labeled "7", big answer "27" for 9×3.
- **Text overlay:** "×9 finger trick: 9 × 3 = 27"
- **Style:** Clear hand illustration, folded finger highlighted red, "Trick 3" badge.

## Slide 6 — Trick: Table of 9 (Digit Sum)
- **Visual:** Cards "18", "36", "63" each with arrows summing digits to 9 (1+8=9, 3+6=9, 6+3=9), with a surprised mascot.
- **Text overlay:** "×9 ke ank jodo = hamesha 9!"
- **Style:** Magic reveal cards, sparkles, "Trick 4" badge.

## Slide 7 — Trick: Order Doesn't Matter
- **Visual:** "3 × 8" and "8 × 3" both pointing to the same big "24" with a two-way arrow between them.
- **Text overlay:** "3 × 8 = 8 × 3 = 24 (order same!)"
- **Style:** Balanced two-way layout, glowing equals, "Trick 5" badge.

## Slide 8 — Worked Example (6×5 and 9×6)
- **Visual:** "6 × 5 = 30" using the 5-trick, and "9 × 6 = 54" using folded 6th finger (left 5, right 4), with digit-check "5 + 4 = 9".
- **Text overlay:** "6 × 5 = 30 | 9 × 6 = 54"
- **Style:** Two-panel worked solution, green ticks, example badge.

## Slide 9 — Trick: Table of 4 (Double-Double)
- **Visual:** "7" → double → "14" → double → "28", shown as two hop arrows. "4 × 7 = 28".
- **Text overlay:** "×4 = Double ka Double!"
- **Style:** Two-step hop diagram, orange tones, "Trick 6" badge.

## Slide 10 — Chalo Try Karein! (Practice)
- **Visual:** Friendly mascot holding a PAUSE sign. Three question cards: "10 × 7 = ?", "9 × 4 = ? (fingers)", "4 × 8 = ? (double-double)".
- **Text overlay:** "Chalo Try Karein! (Pause karo)"
- **Style:** Quiz-show feel, bright question cards, big PAUSE icon.

## Slide 11 — Answers
- **Visual:** Same three cards revealed: "70", "36", "32", each with a green tick and a celebrating mascot.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: 70, 36, 32 — Shabaash!"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, celebratory sparkles.

## Slide 12 — Quick Recap
- **Visual:** Checklist board of tricks: ×2 double, ×10 add zero, ×5 ends 5/0, ×9 fingers + digit sum, ×4 double-double, order doesn't matter.
- **Text overlay:** "Recap: 6 tricks yaad rakho!"
- **Style:** Notebook / blackboard look, neat checklist with mini icons.

## Slide 13 — CTA / Subscribe

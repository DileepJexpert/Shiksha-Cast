# KM02 — Decimals aur Place Value, Dot Ka Jadoo — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Bright, friendly, colorful, big numbers, cartoon icons
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A giant glowing decimal point (dot) as a cute cartoon character with eyes, standing between a "12" and a "5", next to a chocolate price tag "₹12.5". Confetti and stars. Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.
- **Text overlay:** "Decimals = Dot Ka Jadoo!"  (big bubbly letters)
- **Style:** Bright purple and yellow background, playful and energetic.

## Slide 2 — Place Value Basics
- **Visual:** Number "345" on three colored columns labeled Hundreds (3), Tens (4), Ones (5), each column showing its value 300, 40, 5.
- **Text overlay:** "Har digit ki apni jagah = Place Value"
- **Style:** Three tall blocks, each a different color, clean white background.

## Slide 3 — Meet the Decimal Point
- **Visual:** A pizza cut into 10 equal slices with 3 highlighted, big "0.3" beside it. A vertical line through a number showing "whole | parts" with the dot in the middle.
- **Text overlay:** "Dot ke baaye = poore, daaye = hisse"
- **Style:** Warm pizza colors, glowing dot in the center.

## Slide 4 — Right Side Place Value
- **Visual:** A place-value chart extending right of the dot: tenths (1 of 10 grid) and hundredths (1 of 100 grid). Examples "0.7" and "0.25" labeled.
- **Text overlay:** "Tenths = dasvaan, Hundredths = sauvaan"
- **Style:** Grid visuals shrinking from left to right, blue and green tones.

## Slide 5 — Worked Example 1 (4.6)
- **Visual:** Four whole chocolate bars + one bar cut in 10 with 6 pieces highlighted. Big "4.6" with arrows: 4 = wholes, 6 = tenths.
- **Text overlay:** "4.6 = chaar poore + chhah dasvaan"
- **Style:** Chocolate brown, "Example 1" badge in corner.

## Slide 6 — Worked Example 2 (12.35)
- **Visual:** Place-value chart with columns Tens(1), Ones(2), dot, Tenths(3), Hundredths(5). Each column glowing.
- **Text overlay:** "12.35 = baarah point three five"
- **Style:** Colorful chart, "Example 2" badge, bold dot separator.

## Slide 7 — Decimals in Real Life (Money & Weight)
- **Visual:** A pencil with price tag "₹5.50", and a weighing scale showing "28.5 kg" with a smiling kid.
- **Text overlay:** "Paise aur weight me decimals!"
- **Style:** Everyday shop and home scene, warm and relatable.

## Slide 8 — Worked Example 3 (Compare 0.5 vs 0.05)
- **Visual:** Two pizzas: left cut in 10 with 1 big slice highlighted (0.5 area is 5 slices), right cut in 100 with 5 tiny slices highlighted. Big ">" sign, "0.5" circled.
- **Text overlay:** "0.5 > 0.05 (dasvaan bada hai!)"
- **Style:** Side-by-side comparison, glowing greater-than sign.

## Slide 9 — Hidden Dot Trick
- **Visual:** "7" transforming into "7.0", and "0.5" next to "0.50" with a big equals sign and a sparkle/magic wand.
- **Text overlay:** "0.5 = 0.50 (keemat same!)"
- **Style:** Magic-trick sparkle, friendly mascot pointing.

## Slide 10 — Chalo Try Karein! (Practice)
- **Visual:** Friendly mascot holding a PAUSE sign. Three question cards: "8.4 me 4 kis jagah?", "0.7 ya 0.07 bada?", "6.25 ko padho?".
- **Text overlay:** "Chalo Try Karein! (Pause karo)"
- **Style:** Quiz-show feel, bright question cards, big PAUSE icon.

## Slide 11 — Answers
- **Visual:** Same three cards revealed: "Tenths", "0.7 bada", "six point two five", each with a green tick and a celebrating mascot.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: Tenths, 0.7, six point two five!"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, celebratory sparkles.

## Slide 12 — Quick Recap
- **Visual:** Checklist board: dot separates whole & parts, left = ones/tens/hundreds, right = tenths/hundredths, compare using place value, 0.5 = 0.50.
- **Text overlay:** "Recap: Place value yaad rakho!"
- **Style:** Notebook / blackboard look, neat checklist.

## Slide 13 — CTA / Subscribe
- **Visual:** Happy mascot giving a thumbs up beside a big red SUBSCRIBE button and bell icon, decimal-number confetti raining down. Katixo KhojLab logo center.
- **Text overlay:** "Shabaash! Katixo KhojLab Subscribe karo!"
- **Style:** Bright celebration, YouTube subscribe colors, joyful.

# KM08 — X-axis aur Y-axis (Coordinates) Kids ke Liye — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Bright, friendly, colorful, big numbers, cartoon icons
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A pirate treasure map with a grid, a big red X marking the spot and footsteps "3 right, 2 up". Confetti and stars. Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.
- **Text overlay:** "Coordinates ka Khel!"  (big bubbly letters)
- **Style:** Adventurous map look, bright teal and gold, playful.

## Slide 2 — Graph Paper, X aur Y
- **Visual:** A clean grid with a horizontal arrow labeled "X-axis (aadi)" and a vertical arrow labeled "Y-axis (khadi)" crossing in the middle.
- **Text overlay:** "X = aadi | Y = khadi"
- **Style:** Light grid, X-axis in blue, Y-axis in red, bold labels.

## Slide 3 — Origin
- **Visual:** Grid with the crossing point glowing, labeled "Origin (0,0)" with a star, both axes numbered 0,1,2,3... from there.
- **Text overlay:** "Origin = (0, 0) — yahin se shuru!"
- **Style:** Glowing center point, numbered axes, friendly arrows.

## Slide 4 — Coordinates Likhne ka Tarika
- **Visual:** A big "(3 , 2)" with arrows: first number → "right (X)", second number → "up (Y)". A walking kid icon: 3 steps right, 2 steps up.
- **Text overlay:** "(X, Y) = pehle right, phir upar"
- **Style:** Step arrows on grid, color-coded X blue / Y red.

## Slide 5 — Worked Example (4,3)
- **Visual:** Grid showing path from origin: 4 steps right along X, then 3 steps up, dot placed at (4,3) with a star.
- **Text overlay:** "(4, 3) — 4 right, 3 upar"
- **Style:** Example badge, dotted path, glowing final point.

## Slide 6 — Worked Example (2,5) + order warning
- **Visual:** Grid with point (2,5) plotted, and a faint (5,2) shown elsewhere to contrast, a "≠" between them.
- **Text overlay:** "(2,5) ≠ (5,2)!"
- **Style:** Two distinct dots, warning sparkle on the not-equal sign.

## Slide 7 — Ordered Pair (Order Matters)
- **Visual:** Grid with (3,1) and (1,3) both plotted, clearly in different spots, each labeled. Big note "ORDER MATTERS".
- **Text overlay:** "Pehla = X, doosra = Y — kabhi mat badlo!"
- **Style:** Two colored dots, bold rule banner.

## Slide 8 — Real Life (Chess / Map / Games)
- **Visual:** Three mini panels: a chessboard square, a city map pin, a video-game character on a grid — all using coordinates.
- **Text overlay:** "Chess, maps, games — sab coordinates!"
- **Style:** Three cheerful panels, icons for each, fun vibe.

## Slide 9 — Plotting Shapes
- **Visual:** Grid with three points (1,1), (4,1), (4,4) plotted and connected with lines starting to form a shape.
- **Text overlay:** "Points jodo = shapes bante hain!"
- **Style:** Connected dots, light shading, artistic feel.

## Slide 10 — Chalo Try Karein! (Practice)
- **Visual:** Friendly mascot holding a PAUSE sign. Three question cards: "(3,2) — pehle kitne right?", "Origin kaunsa point?", "(2,4) aur (4,2) same? Haan/Naa".
- **Text overlay:** "Chalo Try Karein! (Pause karo)"
- **Style:** Quiz-show feel, bright question cards, big PAUSE icon.

## Slide 11 — Answers
- **Visual:** Same three cards revealed: "3 right", "(0,0)", "Naa (alag jagah)", each with a green tick and a celebrating mascot.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: 3, (0,0), Naa — Shabaash!"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, celebratory sparkles.

## Slide 12 — Quick Recap
- **Visual:** Checklist board: X-axis = aadi, Y-axis = khadi, Origin = (0,0), (X,Y) = pehle right phir upar, order matters.
- **Text overlay:** "Recap: X aadi, Y khadi, (X,Y)!"
- **Style:** Notebook / blackboard look, neat checklist.

## Slide 13 — CTA / Subscribe
- **Visual:** Happy mascot giving a thumbs up beside a big red SUBSCRIBE button and bell icon, grid-and-star confetti raining down. Katixo KhojLab logo center.
- **Text overlay:** "Shabaash! Katixo KhojLab Subscribe karo!"
- **Style:** Bright celebration, YouTube subscribe colors, joyful.

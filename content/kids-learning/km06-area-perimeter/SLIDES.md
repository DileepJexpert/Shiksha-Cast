# KM06 — Area aur Perimeter Kya Hota Hai — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Bright, friendly, colorful, big numbers, cartoon icons
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A cartoon room with a kid walking along the walls (dotted path = perimeter) and the floor filled with square tiles (= area). Confetti and stars. Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.
- **Text overlay:** "Area aur Perimeter!"  (big bubbly letters)
- **Style:** Bright teal and orange background, playful and energetic.

## Slide 2 — Perimeter Kya Hai
- **Visual:** A garden shape with a kid walking all the way around the edge, glowing dotted boundary line, footprints along the border.
- **Text overlay:** "Perimeter = kinaara (boundary)"
- **Style:** Green grass scene, glowing edge line, friendly arrow tracing the border.

## Slide 3 — Area Kya Hai
- **Visual:** A floor being filled with small colorful square tiles one by one, counting them up to fill the whole space.
- **Text overlay:** "Area = andar ki jagah (sq units)"
- **Style:** Split mood vs Slide 2: inside filled with grid tiles, bright fill colors.

## Slide 4 — Rectangle Formulas
- **Visual:** A labeled rectangle with length and breadth marked. Two formula cards: Perimeter = 2 × (l + b), Area = l × b.
- **Text overlay:** "Rectangle: P = 2(l+b), A = l×b"
- **Style:** Clean white background, bold formula cards, length in blue, breadth in red.

## Slide 5 — Rectangle Worked Example (8 × 5)
- **Visual:** Rectangle labeled 8 cm and 5 cm. Step lines: P = 2×(8+5) = 26 cm; A = 8×5 = 40 sq cm. Star on each answer.
- **Text overlay:** "P = 26 cm | A = 40 sq cm"
- **Style:** Example badge in corner, yellow highlight on final answers.

## Slide 6 — Square Formulas + Example
- **Visual:** A square with all 4 sides marked equal (6 cm). Formula cards: Perimeter = 4 × side, Area = side × side. Worked: P = 24 cm, A = 36 sq cm.
- **Text overlay:** "Square: P = 4×side, A = side×side"
- **Style:** Equal-side tick marks on all four sides, purple accent.

## Slide 7 — Perimeter Real Life (Fence)
- **Visual:** A 10 m × 4 m garden with a wooden fence being built all around the edge, measuring tape along the boundary.
- **Text overlay:** "Fence = Perimeter = 28 m"
- **Style:** Warm outdoor scene, fence posts along the border, tape measure.

## Slide 8 — Area Real Life (Grass)
- **Visual:** Same 10 m × 4 m garden now being filled fully with green grass turf squares covering the inside.
- **Text overlay:** "Grass = Area = 40 sq m"
- **Style:** Lush green fill inside, contrast with bare border, cheerful.

## Slide 9 — Bigger Example (12 × 3)
- **Visual:** Rectangle 12 cm × 3 cm. Two panels: Perimeter = 30 cm (straight units), Area = 36 sq cm (square units). A note bubble: "Area = sq units, Perimeter = units".
- **Text overlay:** "P = 30 cm | A = 36 sq cm"
- **Style:** Two-panel layout, sparkle on the units note.

## Slide 10 — Chalo Try Karein! (Practice)
- **Visual:** Friendly mascot holding a PAUSE sign. Three question cards: "Rect 7×2 ka Perimeter?", "Rect 7×2 ka Area?", "Square side 5 ka Perimeter?".
- **Text overlay:** "Chalo Try Karein! (Pause karo)"
- **Style:** Quiz-show feel, bright question cards, big PAUSE icon.

## Slide 11 — Answers
- **Visual:** Same three cards revealed: "P = 18 cm", "A = 14 sq cm", "P = 20 cm", each with a green tick and a celebrating mascot.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: 18 cm, 14 sq cm, 20 cm — Shabaash!"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, celebratory sparkles.

## Slide 12 — Quick Recap
- **Visual:** Checklist board: Perimeter = boundary (fence), Area = inside space (grass/tiles), Rectangle P=2(l+b) A=l×b, Square P=4×side A=side×side.
- **Text overlay:** "Recap: Perimeter kinaara, Area andar!"
- **Style:** Notebook / blackboard look, neat checklist.

## Slide 13 — CTA / Subscribe
- **Visual:** Happy mascot giving a thumbs up beside a big red SUBSCRIBE button and bell icon, number confetti raining down. Katixo KhojLab logo center.
- **Text overlay:** "Shabaash! Katixo KhojLab Subscribe karo!"
- **Style:** Bright celebration, YouTube subscribe colors, joyful.

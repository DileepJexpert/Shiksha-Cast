# KM07 — Ghadi aur Time Kaise Padhein — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Bright, friendly, colorful, big numbers, cartoon icons
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A big smiling cartoon wall clock with eyes, surrounded by icons of school, food, and play. Confetti and stars. Katixo KhojLab logo top-left.
- **Text overlay:** "Ghadi aur Time!"  (big bubbly letters)
- **Style:** Bright sky-blue and yellow background, playful and energetic.

## Slide 2 — Clock ke Hands
- **Visual:** A clean clock face 1–12, with the SHORT hand labeled "hour" (red) and the LONG hand labeled "minute" (blue), arrows pointing to each.
- **Text overlay:** "Chhota = hour | Bada = minute"
- **Style:** Clean white clock, color-coded hands, clear bold labels.

## Slide 3 — Time ki Counting
- **Visual:** Three stacked bars: 1 din = 24 hours, 1 hour = 60 minutes, 1 minute = 60 seconds. Small "5" badge between two clock numbers.
- **Text overlay:** "24 hours, 60 min, 60 sec"
- **Style:** Infographic bars, friendly icons (sun, clock, stopwatch).

## Slide 4 — Minute ka Hisaab (×5)
- **Visual:** Clock with each number multiplied by 5 shown faintly outside (12=0, 1=5, 2=10, 3=15 ... 6=30). Big minute hand pointing to 6 = 30.
- **Text overlay:** "Number × 5 = minutes"
- **Style:** Highlight the ×5 ring in orange, big glowing "30" example.

## Slide 5 — Worked Example (3 o'clock)
- **Visual:** Clock with short hand on 3, long hand on 12. Caption bubble "hour = 3, minute = 0".
- **Text overlay:** "Time = 3 o'clock (3 baje)"
- **Style:** Example badge in corner, green tick, simple and clear.

## Slide 6 — Worked Example (Half past 4)
- **Visual:** Clock with short hand between 4 and 5, long hand on 6. Caption "hour = 4, minute = 6×5 = 30".
- **Text overlay:** "4:30 — saadhe 4 (half past)"
- **Style:** Example badge, highlight the 6→30 with sparkle.

## Slide 7 — Worked Example (Quarter past 9)
- **Visual:** Clock with short hand near 9, long hand on 3. Caption "hour = 9, minute = 3×5 = 15".
- **Text overlay:** "9:15 — sawa 9 (quarter past)"
- **Style:** Example badge, highlight the 3→15, cheerful.

## Slide 8 — Real Life (School Time)
- **Visual:** A kid waking at 7:30 clock and a school bell at 8:00 clock, an arrow between showing "30 min". Also a small play scene 4:45 → 5:00.
- **Text overlay:** "School 8 baje — 30 min bache!"
- **Style:** Warm morning scene, two clocks with a countdown arrow.

## Slide 9 — AM aur PM
- **Visual:** Split screen: left a sun with "AM = subah" and breakfast at 8 AM; right a moon with "PM = shaam/raat" and dinner at 8 PM.
- **Text overlay:** "AM = subah | PM = shaam/raat"
- **Style:** Day/night split, warm vs cool colors, clear icons.

## Slide 10 — Chalo Try Karein! (Practice)
- **Visual:** Friendly mascot holding a PAUSE sign. Three question cards: "Short=2, Long=12 — time?", "Bada kaanta 6 par = kitne min?", "Naashta 8 baje — AM ya PM?".
- **Text overlay:** "Chalo Try Karein! (Pause karo)"
- **Style:** Quiz-show feel, bright question cards, big PAUSE icon.

## Slide 11 — Answers
- **Visual:** Same three cards revealed: "2 o'clock", "30 minutes", "AM", each with a green tick and a celebrating mascot.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: 2 baje, 30 min, AM — Shabaash!"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, celebratory sparkles.

## Slide 12 — Quick Recap
- **Visual:** Checklist board: chhota = hour, bada = minute, number×5 = minutes, 12=full hour, 3=15, 6=30, 9=45, AM=subah, PM=shaam.
- **Text overlay:** "Recap: chhota hour, bada minute, ×5!"
- **Style:** Notebook / blackboard look, neat checklist.

## Slide 13 — CTA / Subscribe
- **Visual:** Happy mascot giving a thumbs up beside a big red SUBSCRIBE button and bell icon, clock confetti raining down. Katixo KhojLab logo center.
- **Text overlay:** "Shabaash! Katixo KhojLab Subscribe karo!"
- **Style:** Bright celebration, YouTube subscribe colors, joyful.

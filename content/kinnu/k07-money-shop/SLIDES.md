# Kinnu's Money Shop | Counting Coins — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Brand:** Kinnu (A Katixo channel)
**Character:** Kinnu = a cute GIRL stickman (big round head, big happy eyes, little hair bow), bright classroom/adventure scenes, thick outlines, flat bright colors.
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK
- **Visual:** Kinnu standing behind a colourful little shop counter with shelves of toffees, pencils and balloons. She holds up one toffee with a giant "₹100" price tag; a customer stickman looks shocked with wide eyes.
- **On-screen text:** "Ek toffee = ₹100?! 😲"
- **Style:** Bright candy-shop colors, big cartoon price tag, thick black outlines, funny shocked expressions.

## Slide 2 — MEET THE COINS
- **Visual:** Four big shiny coins lined up in a row, clearly labelled ₹1, ₹2, ₹5, ₹10. Kinnu pointing at each coin with a happy grin.
- **On-screen text:** "₹1   ₹2   ₹5   ₹10"
- **Style:** Golden/silver flat coins with bold numbers, sparkle shine, clean row layout, cheerful Kinnu.

## Slide 3 — ₹2 + ₹1 = ₹3 (Toffee)
- **Visual:** A toffee with a "₹3" tag on the counter. Kinnu placing one ₹2 coin and one ₹1 coin side by side; a small "= ₹3" appears.
- **On-screen text:** "₹2 + ₹1 = ₹3"
- **Style:** Step-by-step coin layout, plus and equals signs, focused happy Kinnu counting.

## Slide 4 — ₹5 = ₹5 (Pencil)
- **Visual:** A pencil with a "₹5" tag. Kinnu proudly holding up a single ₹5 coin. Big tick mark beside it.
- **On-screen text:** "₹5 coin = ₹5"
- **Style:** Simple single-coin focus, green tick, delighted Kinnu, clean uncluttered scene.

## Slide 5 — ₹5 + ₹5 = ₹10 (Copy)
- **Visual:** A notebook/copy with a "₹10" tag. Kinnu placing two ₹5 coins together; a "= ₹10" appears. Small note: "no ₹10 coin? no problem!"
- **On-screen text:** "₹5 + ₹5 = ₹10"
- **Style:** Two equal coins joining, friendly arrow, "aha!" Kinnu showing different coins make same value.

## Slide 6 — ₹5 + ₹2 = ₹7 (Chocolate)
- **Visual:** A chocolate bar with a "₹7" tag. Kinnu lining up a ₹5 coin and a ₹2 coin with a "= ₹7".
- **On-screen text:** "₹5 + ₹2 = ₹7"
- **Style:** Clear coin-addition layout, yummy chocolate, satisfied counting Kinnu.

## Slide 7 — QUIZ / TUM KARO
- **Visual:** A balloon with a "₹6" tag floating above. Three coins available: ₹5, ₹2, ₹1, with question marks. Kinnu pointing at the viewer, inviting them to choose.
- **On-screen text:** "Balloon = ₹6 — kaunse coins?"
- **Style:** Quiz-show spotlight, floating balloon, fun question marks, encouraging pointing Kinnu.

## Slide 8 — RECAP + SUBSCRIBE
- **Visual:** Kinnu giving a thumbs-up at her shop counter with all four coins (₹1, ₹2, ₹5, ₹10) displayed and the toffee now tagged a correct "₹3". Big Like and Subscribe buttons, Kinnu logo.
- **On-screen text:** "Learn with Kinnu! Like & Subscribe • A Katixo channel"
- **Style:** Celebratory confetti, red Subscribe button, thumbs-up Kinnu, warm bright finale.

# Kinnu and the Slippery Floor | Friction Explained — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Brand:** Kinnu (A Katixo channel)
**Character:** Kinnu = a cute GIRL stickman character (big round head, big happy eyes, a little hair bow), bright classroom/adventure scenes, thick outlines, flat bright colors, 3D-looking objects.
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK
- **Visual:** Shiny wet classroom floor with a "Wet Floor" sign. Kinnu mid-fall, legs up in the air, slipping comically, motion swoosh lines, a big surprised face with spirals. A "?" and a cartoon "OOPS!" star burst near her.
- **On-screen text:** "Kinnu phisal gayi! — Friction kya hai?"
- **Style:** Funny slapstick fall, glossy reflective wet tiles, bright and playful.

## Slide 2 — THE PROBLEM
- **Visual:** Kinnu wobbling on smooth wet glossy tiles, arms flailing for balance, water droplets sparkling on the floor. Confused thought bubble showing the floor with sparkly shine (too smooth).
- **On-screen text:** "Gili-chikni floor = phisalna!"
- **Style:** Emphasize the shiny smooth surface, wobbly cute pose.

## Slide 3 — TEACHER EXPLAINS FRICTION
- **Visual:** Friendly teacher character beside Kinnu (now sitting up). A big diagram: two blocks rubbing together with arrows and tiny "grip" hands between them, labeled "FRICTION". Kinnu has a lightbulb above her head.
- **On-screen text:** "Friction = pakad / grip!"
- **Style:** Classroom learning vibe, clear force arrows, simple diagram.

## Slide 4 — EXPERIMENT (smooth vs carpet)
- **Visual:** Split scene. LEFT: toy car zooming far on a smooth shiny tile floor with long speed lines ("sarrr!"). RIGHT: same toy car stopping quickly on a fuzzy carpet with short lines and a "stop" burst. Kinnu in the middle pointing at both.
- **On-screen text:** "Carpet = zyada friction, gaadi rukti hai"
- **Style:** Clear before/after comparison, contrast smooth vs rough textures.

## Slide 5 — SHOES HAVE GRIP
- **Visual:** Close-up of the bottom of a shoe showing rough wavy grooves/tread, with sparkle "grip" marks. Small insets of sports shoes and a car tyre, both with treads. Kinnu pointing and smiling.
- **On-screen text:** "Rough grooves = grip!"
- **Style:** Detailed tread pattern, friendly labels, bright.

## Slide 6 — KINNU WINS (rough chappal)
- **Visual:** Kinnu now wearing chunky rough-soled chappals/shoes, walking confidently and steadily across the same wet floor with a big proud smile, little "grip" sparkle marks under her feet, NO falling.
- **On-screen text:** "Rough = grip = no phisalna!"
- **Style:** Confident victorious walk, same wet floor as slide 1 but safe now.

## Slide 7 — QUIZ / TUM KARO
- **Visual:** Two question panels. Panel A: a skater gliding fast on smooth ice with speed lines. Panel B: a hand firmly holding a pencil with little grip marks. Kinnu on the side pointing, inviting viewer to think. Question marks above.
- **On-screen text:** "Tum karo! Barf = kam friction?"
- **Style:** Two clear example panels, thinking/quiz mood.

## Slide 8 — RECAP + SUBSCRIBE
- **Visual:** Kinnu standing happily next to a simple recap chart: "Rough = zyada friction" with a grippy shoe, and "Smooth/gila = kam friction" with shiny tile. Big like and subscribe buttons floating, "Kinnu" logo and "A Katixo channel" tag.
- **On-screen text:** "Friction done! Like & Subscribe — A Katixo channel"
- **Style:** Clean recap, warm wrap-up, clear branding.

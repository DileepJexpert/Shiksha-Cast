# Kinnu's Time Machine | Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Brand:** Kinnu (A Katixo channel)
**Character:** Kinnu = a cute GIRL stickman (big round head, big happy eyes, little hair bow), bright classroom/adventure scenes, thick outlines, flat bright colors.
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK
- **Visual:** Kinnu standing proudly next to a colourful cardboard Time Machine (round door, blinking lights, big lever). She looks confused with a question mark above her head; three identical "Monday" calendar pages float around her.
- **On-screen text:** "Sab din ek jaise?! 🤔"
- **Style:** Bright purple-and-yellow machine, thick black outlines, flat colors, playful sparkles, confused-but-cute Kinnu face.

## Slide 2 — YESTERDAY
- **Visual:** Time Machine glowing, arrow pointing BACKWARD. Kinnu in a park playing with a ball, eating ice-cream, then sleeping in bed (small thought-bubble memories). A calendar page labelled "Yesterday" flips back.
- **On-screen text:** "Yesterday = Kal (beeta din)"
- **Style:** Warm sunset orange tones for the past, faded soft edges to show it's a memory, smiling Kinnu.

## Slide 3 — TODAY
- **Visual:** Kinnu fresh and bright, holding a school bag, waving to friends in a sunny classroom. Big shining "Today" calendar page in the center.
- **On-screen text:** "Today = Aaj (abhi)"
- **Style:** Crisp bright daylight blue-and-green, sharp clear outlines (in focus, "now"), happy energetic Kinnu.

## Slide 4 — TOMORROW
- **Visual:** Time Machine zooming FORWARD with arrow. Dreamy thought bubbles of Kinnu baking a cake and visiting grandma. A "Tomorrow" calendar page glows ahead with a little star.
- **On-screen text:** "Tomorrow = Kal (aane wala din)"
- **Style:** Cool dreamy pink-and-cyan future glow, sparkly stars, excited Kinnu looking forward.

## Slide 5 — RECAP TIMELINE
- **Visual:** A simple left-to-right timeline arrow. Three boxes: Yesterday (faded), Today (bright, Kinnu standing on it), Tomorrow (glowing). Kinnu pointing happily at all three.
- **On-screen text:** "Kal ← Aaj → Kal"
- **Style:** Clean horizontal timeline, three distinct colors, big friendly arrow, "aha!" Kinnu expression.

## Slide 6 — EXAMPLE (one drawing, three days)
- **Visual:** Three panels of the same drawing: yesterday Kinnu sketches it, today she colours it, tomorrow she shows it to the teacher. Same picture progressing across three day-labels.
- **On-screen text:** "Ek kaam, teen din!"
- **Style:** Comic-strip 3-panel layout, day labels on top, cheerful Kinnu in each panel.

## Slide 7 — QUIZ / TUM KARO
- **Visual:** Big calendar showing "Wednesday / Budhwar" in the middle, with empty boxes on either side and big question marks. Kinnu pointing at the viewer encouragingly.
- **On-screen text:** "Aaj Budhwar — Yesterday? Tomorrow?"
- **Style:** Quiz-show vibe, spotlight on calendar, friendly pointing Kinnu, fun question marks.

## Slide 8 — RECAP + SUBSCRIBE
- **Visual:** Kinnu giving a thumbs-up beside the Time Machine, with the three labelled day-cards (Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow) lined up. Big Like and Subscribe buttons, Kinnu logo.
- **On-screen text:** "Learn with Kinnu! Like & Subscribe • A Katixo channel"
- **Style:** Celebratory confetti, red Subscribe button, thumbs-up Kinnu, warm bright finale.

# Kinnu Crosses the Star Bridge | Counting 1 to 10 — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Brand:** Kinnu (A Katixo channel)
**Character:** Kinnu = a cute GIRL stickman character (big round head, big happy eyes, a little hair bow), bright classroom/adventure scenes, thick outlines, flat bright colors, 3D-looking objects.
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK
- **Visual:** Night sky full of twinkling stars. A glowing golden bridge made of stars (Star Bridge) arcs across the sky. Kinnu stands at the start, pointing up, with a confused puzzled face and a big question mark "?" above her head. A few wrong numbers float crookedly near random stars (1, 2, 4, 7) to show miscounting.
- **On-screen text:** "Kinnu & the Star Bridge — Ginti 1 se 10!"
- **Style:** Magical deep-blue/purple night, sparkly glowing stars, warm golden bridge, big bold title.

## Slide 2 — STAR 1
- **Visual:** Kinnu standing on the FIRST glowing star of the bridge, arms up, super happy. The full bridge of 10 stars stretches ahead, faintly numbered. Star 1 is brightest and biggest with a giant "1" on it.
- **On-screen text:** "1 — Ek!"
- **Style:** Sparkles around star 1, joyful glow, clear single big number.

## Slide 3 — STARS 1-2-3
- **Visual:** Kinnu mid-hop between stars, motion lines under her feet. Stars 1, 2, 3 are lit up and labeled; the rest dim. Cheerful expression.
- **On-screen text:** "1, 2, 3"
- **Style:** Three bright stars in a row, soft motion trail, playful.

## Slide 4 — STARS 4-5 (halfway)
- **Visual:** Kinnu standing on star 5 in the middle of the bridge, looking back proudly at lit stars 1–5. A small "Halfway!" banner.
- **On-screen text:** "4, 5 — Aadha ho gaya!"
- **Style:** Bridge clearly half glowing (1-5 bright, 6-10 dim ahead), confident pose.

## Slide 5 — STARS 6-7-8
- **Visual:** Kinnu jumping bravely across stars 6, 7, 8, slightly nervous but determined face, little sweat drop but smiling. Stars 6,7,8 lighting up as she lands.
- **On-screen text:** "6, 7, 8"
- **Style:** A bit higher in the sky, dramatic but cute, brave hero vibe.

## Slide 6 — STARS 9-10 (finish)
- **Visual:** Kinnu leaping onto the LAST star (10) at the far end, arms wide in victory, confetti sparkles. ALL ten stars now glowing in a full arc with numbers 1–10 visible along the bridge.
- **On-screen text:** "9, 10 — Kinnu jeet gayi!"
- **Style:** Triumphant, full glowing rainbow-gold bridge, celebratory sparkles.

## Slide 7 — QUIZ / TUM KARO
- **Visual:** Wide friendly sky scene with 10 clearly separated stars in a gentle arc, each ready to be counted. Kinnu on the side pointing at them, inviting the viewer. Big finger-counting hand graphic.
- **On-screen text:** "Tum karo! Count 1 → 10"
- **Style:** Clean, evenly spaced ten countable stars, warm invite.

## Slide 8 — RECAP + SUBSCRIBE
- **Visual:** Kinnu happily sitting on top of the full glowing Star Bridge with numbers 1 to 10 shown in a neat row. Big like and subscribe buttons floating, a small "Kinnu" logo and "A Katixo channel" tag.
- **On-screen text:** "1-10 done! Like & Subscribe — A Katixo channel"
- **Style:** Warm celebratory wrap-up, friendly, clear branding.

# Kinnu's Good Habit Mission | Moral Story for Kids — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Brand:** Kinnu (A Katixo channel)
**Character:** Kinnu = a cute GIRL stickman (big round head, big happy eyes, little hair bow), bright friendly scenes, thick outlines, flat bright colors.
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK
- **Visual:** Kinnu (girl stickman with hair bow) standing in a colorful playroom, mid-play, about to toss a toy block onto an already untidy floor. A few toys scattered near her feet. Big happy careless grin.
- **On-screen text:** "Kinnu's Good Habit Mission!"
- **Style:** Bright cheerful playroom, thick black outlines, flat bold colors (sky blue walls, yellow floor), playful mood.

## Slide 2 — THE MESS GROWS
- **Visual:** Kinnu yawning happily, walking toward a small bed. The whole floor is a big mess: blocks, teddy, books, socks scattered everywhere like a mini jungle. Toys piled high.
- **On-screen text:** "Ab so jaati hun!"
- **Style:** Exaggerated messy room, lots of colorful scattered objects, comic clutter, thick outlines, flat colors.

## Slide 3 — THE PROBLEM
- **Visual:** Next morning. Kinnu looking sad and confused, searching through the messy pile, one foot tripping on a block (small motion lines / "oops" star). Teddy and crayons hidden somewhere in the clutter. Worried face.
- **On-screen text:** "Teddy kahan hai?"
- **Style:** Same messy room, sad blue mood lighting on Kinnu, motion/trip lines, thick outlines, flat colors.

## Slide 4 — MUMMY'S LESSON
- **Visual:** Kind Mummy stickman (taller, with a dupatta/saree hint) kneeling beside Kinnu, gently talking, finger raised in a friendly "tip" gesture. Kinnu looking up, thinking (small thought bubble with a sparkle/idea).
- **On-screen text:** "Cleanliness — ek achhi aadat!"
- **Style:** Warm cozy tone, soft yellow glow, caring mood, thick outlines, flat bright colors.

## Slide 5 — KINNU CLEANS UP
- **Visual:** Kinnu actively cleaning: putting blocks into a labeled toy box, books onto a shelf, socks into a basket. Show her in mid-action carrying toys, big determined smile. Arrows pointing toys to their right places.
- **On-screen text:** "Sab apni jagah par!"
- **Style:** Energetic helpful mood, organized props (box, shelf, basket), thick outlines, flat bright colors.

## Slide 6 — CLEAN & HAPPY
- **Visual:** Sparkling clean tidy room — floor empty and shiny (sparkle stars), everything in its place. Kinnu hugging her teddy, beaming with joy. Crayons neatly in a cup nearby.
- **On-screen text:** "Wow! Sab kuch mil gaya!"
- **Style:** Bright, clean, sparkly happy scene, sparkle effects, thick outlines, flat bright colors.

## Slide 7 — THE LESSON + TUM BHI KARO
- **Visual:** Kinnu smiling and pointing at the viewer (friendly "you do it too" gesture), with a small clean toy box beside her. A checkmark/star badge for "good habit."
- **On-screen text:** "Tum bhi apne toys uthao!"
- **Style:** Encouraging, direct-to-camera, bright and warm, thick outlines, flat colors.

## Slide 8 — RECAP + SUBSCRIBE
- **Visual:** Kinnu waving goodbye in her clean tidy room, big happy smile. Subscribe + Like buttons floating beside her. Small "KINNU" logo and "A Katixo channel" text.
- **On-screen text:** "Like & Subscribe! A Katixo channel"
- **Style:** Celebratory end-card, confetti/sparkles, bright friendly colors, thick outlines.

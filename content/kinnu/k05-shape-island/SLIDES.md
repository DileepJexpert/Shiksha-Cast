# Kinnu's Shape Island | Circle, Square, Triangle — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Brand:** Kinnu (A Katixo channel)
**Character:** Kinnu = a cute GIRL stickman (big round head, big happy eyes, little hair bow), bright classroom/adventure scenes, thick outlines, flat bright colors.
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK
- **Visual:** Kinnu holding an old treasure map labeled "SHAPE ISLAND" with a big X and a sparkling treasure chest icon. She points at a triangle on the map looking confused, a question mark above her head.
- **On-screen text:** "Treasure on Shape Island!"
- **Style:** Adventure-poster feel, blue ocean border, dotted treasure path, sparkles, thick outlines, bright colors.

## Slide 2 — Circle
- **Visual:** Kinnu standing beside a giant circle. Around it, real round objects: sun, ball, pizza, moon — each clearly a circle. Kinnu traces the round edge with her finger (motion arc).
- **On-screen text:** "Circle = round, no corners (ball, sun)"
- **Style:** One big bold circle, real-object icons in circles, "CIRCLE" banner, flat bright colors.

## Slide 3 — Square
- **Visual:** Kinnu next to a big square with all four sides marked equal (little tick marks) and four corner dots. Real objects: chocolate piece, window, biscuit. Kinnu counts sides 1-2-3-4.
- **On-screen text:** "Square = 4 equal sides, 4 corners"
- **Style:** Bold square outline, numbered sides, corner dots highlighted, "SQUARE" banner.

## Slide 4 — Triangle
- **Visual:** Kinnu beside a big triangle with three sides and three corners labeled. Real objects: samosa, pizza slice, mountain peak. Kinnu counts 1-2-3.
- **On-screen text:** "Triangle = 3 sides, 3 corners"
- **Style:** Bold triangle outline, three numbered corners, "TRIANGLE" banner, bright flat colors.

## Slide 5 — Oops, Corrected
- **Visual:** Split scene: on the left a round circle (no corners), on the right a triangle (three corners) with a big arrow between them and a friendly "X" over Kinnu's earlier wrong idea. Kinnu laughs, hand on head.
- **On-screen text:** "Circle is round. Triangle has 3 corners!"
- **Style:** Comparison layout, green check on correct, red light "X" on mistake, playful tone.

## Slide 6 — Recap of 3 Shapes
- **Visual:** Three big shapes in a row — circle, square, triangle — each with its label and key fact. Kinnu in the middle wearing a little detective hat, pointing along the row.
- **On-screen text:** "Circle • Square • Triangle"
- **Style:** Clean three-column recap, each shape a different bright color, detective theme.

## Slide 7 — Quiz Time (pizza / window)
- **Visual:** Two flashcards: a round pizza plate, and a square window. Kinnu holds a pointer between them with empty answer boxes. Question marks float above each card.
- **On-screen text:** "Tum karo! pizza = ? window = ?"
- **Style:** Quiz-show layout, spotlight, answer boxes, bright cheerful colors.

## Slide 8 — Quiz Time (samosa)
- **Visual:** A big hot samosa with steam lines, three sides and corners highlighted. Kinnu points and claps. A glowing "TRIANGLE" answer appears.
- **On-screen text:** "samosa = triangle!"
- **Style:** Close-up of samosa, three corners marked, confetti, green checkmark.

## Slide 9 — Treasure & Subscribe
- **Visual:** Open treasure chest glowing with circle, square, and triangle gems spilling out. Kinnu waves goodbye, holding the map. Subscribe button and Like thumb glow at the bottom.
- **On-screen text:** "Treasure = Shapes! Like & Subscribe! A Katixo channel"
- **Style:** Triumphant closing scene, golden glow, big Subscribe button, Kinnu logo corner, thick outlines.

# Kinnu Finds the Missing Word | Noun and Verb — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Brand:** Kinnu (A Katixo channel)
**Character:** Kinnu = a cute GIRL stickman (big round head, big happy eyes, little hair bow), bright classroom/adventure scenes, thick outlines, flat bright colors.
---
## Slide 1 — HOOK
- **Visual:** Kinnu standing in a colorful classroom holding a big paper card that reads "Kinnu ___ " with a giant glowing question mark floating where the missing word should be. Kinnu has a puzzled, curious face.
- **On-screen text:** "The Missing Word Mystery!"
- **Style:** Bright yellow/blue background, sparkles around the question mark, big bold thick outlines, fun adventure-poster feel.

## Slide 2 — What is a Noun
- **Visual:** Kinnu pointing happily to a row of labeled objects: a dog, a ball, a mango, a small house, each with its name tag. A speech bubble says "Naming word!"
- **On-screen text:** "Noun = Naming Word (dog, ball, school)"
- **Style:** Flat bright colors, each object in its own bubble, green NOUN banner at top.

## Slide 3 — Noun Park
- **Visual:** Kinnu walking through a cheerful "NOUN PARK" gate. Inside: a tree, a bird, a river, a house — each with a little signboard showing its name. Kinnu points with her finger.
- **On-screen text:** "Noun Park: tree, bird, river, house"
- **Style:** Park scene, blue sky, big welcome arch reading NOUN PARK, thick outlines.

## Slide 4 — What is a Verb
- **Visual:** Kinnu shown in mid-action: jumping with motion lines, plus small icons of run, eat, sing, dance around her. A speech bubble says "Action word!"
- **On-screen text:** "Verb = Action Word (run, jump, sing)"
- **Style:** Dynamic motion lines, orange VERB banner at top, energetic flat colors.

## Slide 5 — Verb Valley
- **Visual:** A green valley labeled "VERB VALLEY" full of action: a monkey jumping, a fish swimming in a stream, a baby crying. Kinnu watches with a delighted smile.
- **On-screen text:** "Verb Valley: jump, swim, cry"
- **Style:** Rolling hills, bright sun, motion lines on each animal, VERB VALLEY signpost.

## Slide 6 — Mystery Solved
- **Visual:** Back in classroom. The card now reads "Kinnu runs!" with the word "runs" glowing and a little VERB tag pointing to it. Kinnu is running with a big proud smile, confetti around.
- **On-screen text:** "Kinnu runs! (runs = verb)"
- **Style:** Celebration colors, confetti, glowing answer word, thick outlines.

## Slide 7 — Quiz Time (apple / dance)
- **Visual:** Two big flashcards side by side: an apple, and a dancing figure. Kinnu stands between them holding a pointer, inviting the viewer. Two empty boxes labeled NOUN and VERB.
- **On-screen text:** "Tum karo! apple = ? dance = ?"
- **Style:** Quiz-show layout, bright spotlight, NOUN box green, VERB box orange.

## Slide 8 — Quiz Time (school / sing)
- **Visual:** Two flashcards: a school building, and Kinnu singing with music notes. NOUN and VERB boxes again, with happy checkmarks appearing.
- **On-screen text:** "school = noun, sing = verb!"
- **Style:** Same quiz-show layout, green checkmarks, cheerful confetti.

## Slide 9 — Recap & Subscribe
- **Visual:** Kinnu waving goodbye beside two big banners: a green NOUN banner (naming word) and an orange VERB banner (action word). A Subscribe button and Like thumb glow at the bottom.
- **On-screen text:** "Noun = name, Verb = action. Like & Subscribe! A Katixo channel"
- **Style:** Warm closing scene, big friendly Subscribe button, Kinnu logo corner, thick outlines.

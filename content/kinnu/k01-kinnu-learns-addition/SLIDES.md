# Kinnu Learns Addition — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_010.png`
**Brand:** Kinnu (A Katixo channel) | **Style:** Bright, playful, funny stickman cartoon, big rounded text, primary colors (yellow/red/blue), white or soft pastel background

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A cute smiling stickman "Kinnu" waving, holding a laddoo, big googly eyes
- **Text overlay:** "Kinnu Learns ADDITION!"
- **Style:** Big fun cartoon title, confetti, very colorful

## Slide 2 — Kinnu has 2 laddoos
- **Visual:** Stickman Kinnu holding 2 yellow laddoos, happy face
- **Text overlay:** "Kinnu ke paas 2 laddoo | Yummy!"
- **Style:** Show 2 clear laddoos with a big "2"

## Slide 3 — Friend gives 3 more
- **Visual:** A second stickman handing 3 laddoos to Kinnu
- **Text overlay:** "Dost deta hai 3 aur laddoo!"
- **Style:** Show the 3 new laddoos with a big "+3"

## Slide 4 — The funny mistake
- **Visual:** Kinnu with a goofy proud face, thought bubble showing "23"
- **Text overlay:** "Kinnu: 23 laddoos?! | He he... GALAT!"
- **Style:** Funny, big crossed-out "23", laughing emoji vibe

## Slide 5 — Teacher corrects
- **Visual:** A friendly stickman teacher with a pointer, gentle smile
- **Text overlay:** "Ye ADDITION hai — jodna! | Joining nahi"
- **Style:** Teacher in front of a small board

## Slide 6 — What is addition
- **Visual:** Two groups of laddoos (2 and 3) with a big plus sign between them
- **Text overlay:** "Addition = saath milaakar ginna"
- **Style:** Clear 2 + 3 layout, arrows pulling groups together

## Slide 7 — Count together
- **Visual:** All 5 laddoos in a row, each numbered 1,2,3,4,5
- **Text overlay:** "Gino: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 = 5 laddoo!"
- **Style:** Bouncy counting, each laddoo highlighted in turn

## Slide 8 — The rule
- **Visual:** Big happy equation with laddoos: 2 + 3 = 5, Kinnu cheering
- **Text overlay:** "2 + 3 = 5 | Yeh hai ADDITION!"
- **Style:** Celebration, stars, very bold equation

## Slide 9 — Your turn (1 + 1)
- **Visual:** 1 ball + 1 ball = 2 balls, Kinnu pointing at the viewer
- **Text overlay:** "Tum karo: 1 + 1 = ?"
- **Style:** Interactive, question mark, inviting kids to answer

## Slide 10 — Recap / CTA
- **Visual:** Kinnu waving bye, holding a "SUBSCRIBE" sign, balloons
- **Text overlay:** "Addition = jodna! | Like & Subscribe to Kinnu"
- **Style:** Warm, cheerful goodbye, "A Katixo channel" small at bottom

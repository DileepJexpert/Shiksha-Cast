# S06 — Why Is Yawning Contagious — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A teen mid-yawn in close-up, with three smaller silhouettes around them also starting to yawn, connected by glowing neon arcs.
- **Text overlay:** "YAWNING — Itna Contagious Kyun?"
- **Style:** Big bold title in neon cyan, subtitle in soft yellow. Dark navy background with subtle brain texture.

## Slide 2 — What Is a Yawn
- **Visual:** Anatomical side profile of a head mid-yawn, arrows showing deep air intake, open jaw, tightening eardrums. Small icons of dog, fish, snake, fetus.
- **Text overlay:** "Ek yawn = ~6 seconds | Fetus se snake tak sabko aata hai"
- **Style:** Diagram style with neon green labels, clean line art on dark.

## Slide 3 — Why We Yawn (Myth Busted)
- **Visual:** A crossed-out oxygen molecule (O2) on the left, a glowing thermometer-brain on the right. Split panel.
- **Text overlay:** "Oxygen theory = GALAT (1987) | Brain Cooling = sabse strong"
- **Style:** Left side dimmed red X, right side bright cyan. Bold contrast.

## Slide 4 — Brain Cooling Mechanism
- **Visual:** Brain shown as a glowing CPU with a fan icon; cool blue air arrows entering nose/mouth, warm red blood arrows leaving.
- **Text overlay:** "Brain = 2% weight, 20% energy | Yawn = body ka cooling fan"
- **Style:** Tech-meets-biology, blue and red gradient arrows on dark.

## Slide 5 — The Contagion Effect
- **Visual:** A grid of faces; one yawns and a ripple of neon spreads outward to others. Sound-wave icon for the audio trigger.
- **Text overlay:** "~50% adults catch a yawn | Dekhkar, sunkar, padhkar bhi!"
- **Style:** Ripple/spread motif, magenta accents.

## Slide 6 — Mirror Neurons & Empathy
- **Visual:** Two brain outlines facing each other with matching neon neurons lighting up in sync.
- **Text overlay:** "Mirror Neurons — dusron ko copy karne wale cells"
- **Style:** Symmetrical, glowing synaptic lines in cyan/purple.

## Slide 7 — Closeness Boosts Contagion
- **Visual:** Concentric rings — center: family/close friends (bright), middle: acquaintances, outer: strangers (dim). Yawn icons fade outward.
- **Text overlay:** "Jitna close rishta, utna strong contagion"
- **Style:** Radial gradient, warm-to-cool fade.

## Slide 8 — Age & Development
- **Visual:** Timeline from newborn to child, with contagious-yawn icon appearing only after age ~4–5.
- **Text overlay:** "Choti age me contagion kam | Empathy ke saath develop hota hai"
- **Style:** Horizontal timeline, soft pastel-on-dark milestones.

## Slide 9 — Animals Too
- **Visual:** Chimpanzee, dog with owner, wolf, elephant — each with a small yawn icon and neon link to a companion.
- **Text overlay:** "Dogs apne owner ka yawn pakad lete hain!"
- **Style:** Wildlife silhouettes, green/teal accents.

## Slide 10 — Evolution & Survival
- **Visual:** A herd/group of animals at dusk, all yawning in sync, with an alertness meter rising.
- **Text overlay:** "Vigilance + Synchronization = jhund ka survival tool"
- **Style:** Sunset-on-dark scene, amber glow with neon alert meter.

## Slide 11 — Fun Facts
- **Visual:** Four quick icon cards — a viewer yawning at screen, a stifled awkward yawn, a bored brain, an athlete before a match.
- **Text overlay:** "Boredom, stress, focus — sab yawn trigger karte hain"
- **Style:** 2x2 card grid, playful neon outlines.

## Slide 12 — Quick Brain Check
- **Visual:** Big pause icon, three numbered question bubbles, a glowing brain in the center.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! Video PAUSE karo — 3 sawaal"
- **Style:** Quiz-show vibe, bold yellow numbers on dark.

## Slide 13 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three checkmark cards matching the questions: brain cooling, mirror neurons/empathy, close friends.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: Brain Cooling | Mirror Neurons | Close rishte"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, clean answer cards.

## Slide 14 — Recap & Next Episode
- **Visual:** Mini-montage of yawn, brain, mirror neurons icons; right side teaser card with a dreaming face and stars for next episode.
- **Text overlay:** "Next: Why Do We Dream? | Subscribe karna mat bhoolna!"
- **Style:** Recap strip on left, glowing teaser on right. Katixo Shiksha logo bottom-right.

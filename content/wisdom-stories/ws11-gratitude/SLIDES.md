# WS11 — Gratitude Yaani Kritagyata Ki Shakti — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_010.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person standing among many possessions yet looking longingly into the distance
- **Text overlay:** "GRATITUDE — Kritagyata Ki Shakti"
- **Style:** Dark calm background, soft golden glow, gentle and inviting

## Slide 2 — The Rich Man's Emptiness
- **Visual:** A wealthy man in a big house, head bowed, talking to a calm sage
- **Text overlay:** "Sab kuch hai | Par shaanti nahi"
- **Style:** Warm dim interior, soft melancholy tones

## Slide 3 — The Joyful Hut
- **Visual:** A small hut, children playing, a mother singing, warm laughter inside
- **Text overlay:** "Farq cheezon mein nahi | Nazar mein hai"
- **Style:** Bright, warm, glowing rural happiness

## Slide 4 — One Mind, One Feeling
- **Visual:** A balance scale: gratitude on one side glowing, complaint on the other fading
- **Text overlay:** "Shukr aur shikayat | Saath nahi reh sakte"
- **Style:** Symbolic, soft contrast, calming blues and gold

## Slide 5 — Gratitude Journal
- **Visual:** A hand writing three lines in a notebook by candlelight at night
- **Text overlay:** "Roz raat 3 cheezein likho | Soch ki disha badlo"
- **Style:** Cozy, intimate, warm lamp glow

## Slide 6 — Abundance, Not Lack
- **Visual:** Open hands holding simple gifts: bread, eyes, feet, a roof glowing softly
- **Text overlay:** "Jo paas hai use dekho | Wo kisi ka sapna hai"
- **Style:** Tender, golden, full and warm

## Slide 7 — Say Thank You
- **Visual:** A young person warmly thanking a parent/teacher, a glowing connection between them
- **Text overlay:** "Apno ka shukriya kaho | Jitna baantoge, utna badhega"
- **Style:** Heartfelt, soft warm tones

## Slide 8 — Gratitude in Hard Times
- **Visual:** A small light glowing through dark storm clouds; a figure standing firm
- **Text overlay:** "Har andhere mein subah ka vaada | Tum abhi bhi khade ho"
- **Style:** Dramatic yet hopeful, warm light piercing dark

## Slide 9 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: a balance scale, a journal, two grateful hands
- **Text overlay:** "1) Shukr chuno  2) 3 cheezein likho  3) Jo hai use dekho"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, three-panel layout on dark calm bg

## Slide 10 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** A peaceful face smiling at sunrise, eyes soft with contentment
- **Text overlay:** "Khushi mehsoos karne mein hai | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful sunrise, uplifting

# WS07 — Dusron Se Comparison Kaise Chhodein? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_010.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person smiling at their phone, then face falling as they scroll through others' shiny lives
- **Text overlay:** "Dusron Se COMPARISON Kaise Chhodein?"
- **Style:** Soft dark background, warm glow from the phone, relatable mood

## Slide 2 — The Small Plant
- **Visual:** A tiny sapling looking up sadly at a huge mango tree towering above it
- **Text overlay:** "Main to kuch bhi nahi… | Bada ped kitna ooncha"
- **Style:** Earthy greens, soft melancholic light

## Slide 3 — You Are a Rose
- **Visual:** An old gardener kneeling beside the sapling, the sapling now blooming into a rose
- **Text overlay:** "Tu aam nahi, gulaab hai | Apni khushboo pehchaan"
- **Style:** Warm golden hour, hopeful blossoming

## Slide 4 — Inside vs Outside
- **Visual:** Split image: one side a calm posed photo, other side the real tired person behind it
- **Text overlay:** "Apna andar vs unka bahar | Yeh muqabla barabar nahi"
- **Style:** Contrast split, honest and gentle tones

## Slide 5 — Fish and the Tree
- **Visual:** A fish in water beside a monkey on a tree, each thriving in its own element
- **Text overlay:** "Har koi unique hai | Apni taqat pehchaan"
- **Style:** Playful yet calm, balanced two-scene composition

## Slide 6 — Compare With Your Past Self
- **Visual:** A person looking at a faded "past me" silhouette behind them, an arrow of growth between
- **Text overlay:** "Apne pichle version se | Aaj kitna behtar?"
- **Style:** Soft blue tones, reflective and forward-moving

## Slide 7 — Gratitude List
- **Visual:** A hand writing three things in a journal, small glowing hearts rising
- **Text overlay:** "Roz 3 cheezein likho | Jo paas hai, uski qadar"
- **Style:** Warm, cozy, thankful golden light

## Slide 8 — Jealousy to Curiosity
- **Visual:** A dark "jealousy" cloud transforming into a bright lightbulb of inspiration
- **Text overlay:** "Jalan ko seekh mein badlo | Agar woh kar sakta hai…"
- **Style:** Symbolic transformation, warm uplifting glow

## Slide 9 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: split mirror, a past-self arrow, a gratitude journal
- **Text overlay:** "1) Andar vs bahar  2) Apne aap se  3) Gratitude"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, three-panel layout on dark calm bg

## Slide 10 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** A single beautiful rose glowing in a garden at sunrise, fully bloomed
- **Text overlay:** "Tum original ho, copy nahi | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful sunrise, uplifting and cinematic

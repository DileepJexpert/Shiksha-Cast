- **Text overlay:** "Pehle chai peete hain"
- **Style:** Calm cinematic close-up, steam rising, muted earthy tones.

## Slide 3 — Empty to Learn
- **Visual:** Same cup now empty, held in open palms, soft light entering it.
- **Text overlay:** "Khaali pyaali me hi naya samaata hai"
- **Style:** Minimal, symbolic, soft top light, text bottom-center.

- **Visual:** A fruit-laden tree bending low at golden hour, contrasted with a stiff bare tree standing tall.
- **Text overlay:** "Jo jhukta hai, wahi uthta hai"
- **Style:** Nature scene, warm golden light, gentle depth.

- **Visual:** Split composition — calm steady figure (self-respect) vs tense defensive figure (ego).
- **Text overlay:** "Ego aur self-respect alag hain"
- **Style:** Balanced two-panel, contrasting cool vs warm tones.

## Slide 6 — Technique: Sorry & Thank You

## Slide 7 — Technique: Learn From Everyone
- **Visual:** A person genuinely listening to a child/elder, leaning in, warm attentive expression.

- **Visual:** A strong figure kneeling to lift a fallen object, light shining on them — strength in humility.

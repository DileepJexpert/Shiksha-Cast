# WS12 — Chhodna Seekho — Acceptance Aur Let Go Ki Kala — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_010.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person clutching a heavy glowing bundle close to the chest, looking weary
- **Text overlay:** "CHHODNA Seekho — Acceptance Aur Let Go"
- **Style:** Dark calm background, soft golden glow, gentle and inviting

## Slide 2 — Two Monks at the River
- **Visual:** Two monks by a flowing river; a woman unable to cross the strong current
- **Text overlay:** "Do saadhu | Ek nadi | Ek pareshani"
- **Style:** Soft natural tones, flowing water, serene

## Slide 3 — Who Is Still Carrying Her?
- **Visual:** The elder monk calm; the younger monk's head clouded with a lingering thought
- **Text overlay:** "Maine to use kab ka utaar diya | Tum abhi tak uthaye ho"
- **Style:** Warm, reflective, gentle contrast

## Slide 4 — Pain vs Suffering
- **Visual:** A single wound being reopened repeatedly by one's own hand
- **Text overlay:** "Dard zaroori | Dukh ek chunaav"
- **Style:** Symbolic, muted soft blues

## Slide 5 — Acceptance
- **Visual:** A person standing calmly, arms relaxed, finally stopping a tug against the past
- **Text overlay:** "Jo ho gaya, ho gaya | Sachchai ko gale lagao"
- **Style:** Peaceful, soft light, releasing tension

## Slide 6 — Control vs No-Control
- **Visual:** A paper split in two columns: one side glowing (my effort), one side fading (others)
- **Text overlay:** "Control mein hai? Wahan lago | Nahi hai? Chhod do"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, calm grid

## Slide 7 — Open Hand
- **Visual:** Two hands holding sand: a clenched fist losing it, an open palm keeping it
- **Text overlay:** "Jitna kasoge, utna khooge | Khule haath mein asli pakad"
- **Style:** Intimate close-up, warm golden sand

## Slide 8 — Forgiveness
- **Visual:** A chain breaking and dissolving into light around a peaceful figure
- **Text overlay:** "Maafi tumhare liye hai | Khud ko azaad karo"
- **Style:** Liberating, soft glow, hopeful

## Slide 9 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: a bundle set down, a split paper, an open hand
- **Text overlay:** "1) Bojh neeche rakho  2) Control pe lago  3) Khule haath jiyo"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, three-panel layout on dark calm bg

## Slide 10 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** A person standing lighter by a flowing river at sunrise, arms open, free
- **Text overlay:** "Zindagi ek nadi hai, behna seekho | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful sunrise, uplifting

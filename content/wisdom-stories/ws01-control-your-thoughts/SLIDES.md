# WS01 — Apne Thoughts Ko Control Kaise Karein? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_010.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person sitting alone at night, head in hands, glowing thought-bubbles swirling around the head
- **Text overlay:** "Apne THOUGHTS Ko Control Kaise Karein?"
- **Style:** Dark calm background, soft golden glow, gentle and inviting

## Slide 2 — The Restless Mind
- **Visual:** A young man talking to a calm old sage under a tree
- **Text overlay:** "Mann ek talaab hai | Hilta paani = kuch saaf nahi dikhta"
- **Style:** Warm earthy tones, peaceful

## Slide 3 — The Muddy Water
- **Visual:** A glass of muddy water slowly turning clear as the mud settles
- **Text overlay:** "Ruko. Kuch mat karo. | Mitti khud baith jaayegi"
- **Style:** Close-up, soft light through the glass

## Slide 4 — Don't Fight Thoughts
- **Visual:** A person tugging a rope against a giant thought-cloud (tug of war)
- **Text overlay:** "Thought se lado mat | Jitna ladoge, utna strong"
- **Style:** Symbolic, soft blue tones

## Slide 5 — You Are the Sky
- **Visual:** Vast open sky with small clouds drifting; a tiny calm silhouette below
- **Text overlay:** "Tum aasmaan ho | Thoughts sirf baadal hain"
- **Style:** Wide, airy, light blue and white, spacious

## Slide 6 — Breathe
- **Visual:** Close-up of a calm face, eyes closed, soft breath visualized as flowing light
- **Text overlay:** "3 gehri saanson se | Nervous system shaant"
- **Style:** Serene, warm glow, slow mood

## Slide 7 — The Garden of the Mind
- **Visual:** A garden where some seeds grow into bright flowers, others into thorns
- **Text overlay:** "Mann ek bagicha hai | Jo feed karoge, wahi ugega"
- **Style:** Lush, hopeful greens and golden light

## Slide 8 — Daily Practice
- **Visual:** A person meditating with a small "10 minutes" clock glowing beside them
- **Text overlay:** "Mind control = habit | Roz 10 minute"
- **Style:** Calm dawn colors, steady and grounded

## Slide 9 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: a drifting cloud, lungs breathing, a growing plant
- **Text overlay:** "1) Lado mat  2) Saans lo  3) Focus chuno"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, three-panel layout on dark calm bg

## Slide 10 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** Sunrise over calm mountains, a small figure standing peacefully
- **Text overlay:** "Shaanti bahar nahi, andar hai | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful sunrise, uplifting

# WS06 — Sabr Ki Taqat: Patience Se Kaise Jeetein? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_010.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A restless person tapping their foot, staring impatiently at a slow-loading clock/hourglass
- **Text overlay:** "Sabr Ki TAQAT — Patience Se Kaise Jeetein?"
- **Style:** Warm dark background, soft golden glow, inviting and calm

## Slide 2 — Two Farmers, One Seed
- **Visual:** Two farmers planting seeds in adjacent fields at sunrise
- **Text overlay:** "Do kisaan, ek hi din boya | Result kab aayega?"
- **Style:** Earthy warm tones, peaceful morning light

## Slide 3 — The Bamboo Miracle
- **Visual:** A towering bamboo shooting up tall, a stunned village looking up
- **Text overlay:** "6 hafton mein 90 feet | 5 saal ki jadein"
- **Style:** Lush green, dramatic upward perspective, hopeful

## Slide 4 — Roots Below the Ground
- **Visual:** Cross-section of soil showing strong roots spreading underground beneath a tiny sprout
- **Text overlay:** "Result na dikhe to bhi | Andar bahut kuch ban raha hai"
- **Style:** Split view, earthy browns, soft golden roots glowing

## Slide 5 — Don't Compare Timings
- **Visual:** A rose and a bamboo side by side, each with its own small clock
- **Text overlay:** "Har beej ka apna time | Apni raftaar mat naapo"
- **Style:** Soft pastel, balanced two-object composition

## Slide 6 — The 10-Minute Rule
- **Visual:** A calm hand resting, a small "10 min" timer glowing gently beside it
- **Text overlay:** "Jaldbaazi se pehle | 10 minute ruko"
- **Style:** Minimal, serene blue tones, steady mood

## Slide 7 — Love the Process
- **Visual:** A person smiling while doing daily work — reading, practicing — small upward arrow of progress
- **Text overlay:** "Process se pyaar karo | Roz 1% behtar"
- **Style:** Warm, encouraging golden light, motion of growth

## Slide 8 — Stay Steady in Pain
- **Visual:** A steady figure standing firm while soft storm clouds and taunts swirl around but don't move them
- **Text overlay:** "Takleef mein bhi | Apne raaste pe tike raho"
- **Style:** Symbolic, calm blue-grey, grounded and strong

## Slide 9 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: roots growing, two clocks, a calm hand with timer
- **Text overlay:** "1) Karte raho  2) Apna time  3) 10 min ruko"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, three-panel layout on dark calm bg

## Slide 10 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** A tall bamboo touching the sky at sunrise, a small peaceful figure below
- **Text overlay:** "Tike rehne wale jeette hain | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"

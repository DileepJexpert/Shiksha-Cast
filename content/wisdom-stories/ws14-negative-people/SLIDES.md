# WS14 — Negative Logon Se Kaise Deal Karein? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_011.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person standing bright and hopeful while dark, heavy figures around them lean in with grey clouds over their heads
- **Text overlay:** "Negative Logon Se Kaise DEAL Karein?"
- **Style:** Dark calm background, one warm golden spotlight on the central figure

## Slide 2 — The Calm Farmer
- **Visual:** A peaceful farmer listening quietly while an angry man shouts and waves his arms
- **Text overlay:** "Ek shaant kisaan | Ek gusse mein bhara aadmi"
- **Style:** Warm earthy village tones, soft and grounded

## Slide 3 — The Unaccepted Gift
- **Visual:** A wrapped gift box being held out, the other person calmly not taking it; box stays with the giver
- **Text overlay:** "Gift na lo, to wo kiska? | Negativity unhi ki reh jaati hai"
- **Style:** Symbolic, soft golden glow, gentle smile

## Slide 4 — Hurt People Hurt People
- **Visual:** A cracked, sad figure throwing dark stones outward; the stones are made of their own pain
- **Text overlay:** "Negative log = dukhi log | Gusse ki jagah compassion"
- **Style:** Muted blues, empathetic and soft, not harsh

## Slide 5 — The Grey Rock
- **Visual:** A calm grey boulder by a stream while waves of drama splash around it harmlessly
- **Text overlay:** "Grey Rock Method | Reaction mat do"
- **Style:** Cool stone tones, steady, unbothered mood

## Slide 6 — Boundaries
- **Visual:** A person drawing a soft glowing circle of light around themselves, calm and protected
- **Text overlay:** "Boundaries banao | Sabko khush rakhna kaam nahi"
- **Style:** Warm protective glow, confident and serene

## Slide 7 — Don't Try to Change Them
- **Visual:** A person walking forward on a sunlit path, gently letting go of a heavy chain attached to a sleeping figure
- **Text overlay:** "Badalne ki koshish chhodo | Sahi logon ke saath chalo"
- **Style:** Hopeful dawn light, sense of release and freedom

## Slide 8 — The Filter
- **Visual:** Words flowing through a funnel/filter — useful drops pass through, dark junk is caught and discarded
- **Text overlay:** "Har baat ko filter karo | Kaam ki rakho, baaki phenk do"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, soft amber light through the funnel

## Slide 9 — Recharge Your Energy
- **Visual:** A person sitting peacefully in nature, a soft glowing battery/heart filling up inside them
- **Text overlay:** "Apni energy roz bharo | Bhara cup koi khaali nahi karta"
- **Style:** Lush calm greens and golden hour light, restorative

## Slide 10 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: a returned gift, a glowing boundary circle, a recharging heart
- **Text overlay:** "1) React mat karo  2) Boundaries banao  3) Energy sahi jagah lagao"
- **Style:** Clean three-panel layout on dark calm background

## Slide 11 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** A vast calm ocean at sunrise with tiny pebbles on the shore, a peaceful figure looking out
- **Text overlay:** "Tum samandar ho, wo sirf kankad | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful sunrise over water, uplifting and spacious

# WS09 — Gussa Kaise Control Karein? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_010.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person mid-outburst, then a quiet regretful moment afterward, head lowered
- **Text overlay:** "GUSSA Kaise Control Karein?"
- **Style:** Warm dark background with a soft ember glow, calm yet intense

## Slide 2 — The Bag of Nails
- **Visual:** An angry boy hammering a nail into a wooden fence, a father watching calmly
- **Text overlay:** "Jab gussa aaye | Ek keel thok dena"
- **Style:** Earthy warm tones, rustic fence, teaching mood

## Slide 3 — Pulling the Nails Out
- **Visual:** The boy gently pulling nails out of the fence, fewer remaining each day
- **Text overlay:** "Shaant raho to | Ek keel nikaal dena"
- **Style:** Soft hopeful light, sense of slow progress

## Slide 4 — The Holes Remain
- **Visual:** Close-up of the fence with holes left behind even after all nails removed
- **Text overlay:** "Keel nikal gayi | Par chhed reh gaya"
- **Style:** Quiet, reflective, gentle shadows on wood

## Slide 5 — Anger Hides Something
- **Visual:** A flame on the surface with hidden roots below — fear, pain, tiredness
- **Text overlay:** "Gusse ke neeche chhupa hai | Dar, dard, thakaan"
- **Style:** Symbolic split, warm ember above, cool depth below

## Slide 6 — The 90-Second Pause
- **Visual:** A calm figure with eyes closed, a soft "90 sec" counter glowing, lips sealed
- **Text overlay:** "Pehle 90 second ruko | 10 tak gino"
- **Style:** Serene, minimal, cooling blue-warm balance

## Slide 7 — Breathe & Step Away
- **Visual:** A person walking outside, sipping water, taking deep calming breaths
- **Text overlay:** "Doori banao, paani piyo | 4 gehri saansein"
- **Style:** Open, airy, refreshing calm tones

## Slide 8 — Express, Don't Explode
- **Visual:** Two contrasting halves: one shouting (explode), one calmly speaking (express)
- **Text overlay:** "Dabao mat, samjho | Express karo, explode nahi"
- **Style:** Balanced contrast, warm honest light

## Slide 9 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: a nail hole, a 90-sec timer, a calm speaking figure
- **Text overlay:** "1) Shabd nishaan  2) 90 sec ruko  3) Wajah samjho"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, three-panel layout on dark calm bg

## Slide 10 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** A person standing calm and strong at sunrise, the ember now a warm steady light
- **Text overlay:** "Apne gusse ke maalik bano | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful sunrise, uplifting and cinematic

## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A student at a desk, books open, but a glowing phone pulling their attention away
- **Text overlay:** "FOCUS Aur Concentration Kaise Badhayein?"
- **Style:** Dark calm background, soft golden glow, gentle and inviting

## Slide 2 — Three Students, One Target
- **Visual:** A guru with three students, a wooden bird on a distant tree branch
- **Text overlay:** "Tumhe kya dikh raha hai? | Ped, aasmaan, patte, panchhi"
- **Style:** Warm outdoor light, classic and serene

## Slide 3 — Only the Eye of the Bird
- **Visual:** Tight zoom on a single bird's eye, everything else blurred away
- **Text overlay:** "Sirf panchhi ki aankh | Ab teer chalao"
- **Style:** Sharp focal point, dramatic soft blur around

## Slide 4 — One Thing, Fully
- **Visual:** A brain switching between many open tasks, energy leaking at each switch
- **Text overlay:** "Multitasking nahi | Ek kaam, poore mann se"
- **Style:** Symbolic, soft blue tones

## Slide 5 — Pomodoro
- **Visual:** A timer set to 25:00 beside a focused person, phone placed far away
- **Text overlay:** "25 min focus + 5 min break | Bina rukawat"
- **Style:** Clean, energizing, warm spotlight

## Slide 6 — Remove Distractions First
- **Visual:** A clean desk, phone face-down in another room, notifications off
- **Text overlay:** "Willpower se mat lado | Distraction door rakho"
- **Style:** Minimal, calm, grounded tones

## Slide 7 — Train the Mind
- **Visual:** A flexing muscle morphing into a calm meditating figure, attention beam steady
- **Text overlay:** "Concentration ek muscle hai | Roz 10 minute"
- **Style:** Warm, steady, focused glow

## Slide 8 — Sleep and Body
- **Visual:** A well-rested person, water glass, a short walk at dawn, a bright sharp mind
- **Text overlay:** "Thaka dimaag focus nahi karta | Sharir theek, dimaag tez"

## Slide 9 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: a single target, a 25-min timer, a steady beam of light
- **Text overlay:** "1) Ek kaam  2) Focused session  3) Roz practice + neend"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, three-panel layout on dark calm bg

## Slide 10 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** An archer at sunrise, perfectly still, eyes locked on a single distant target

# WS16 — Consistency Vs Motivation: Asli Safalta Ka Raaz — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_011.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A burst of fireworks fading into the dark while a single steady candle keeps glowing beside it
- **Text overlay:** "Consistency vs Motivation — Asli Safalta Ka Raaz"
- **Style:** Dark calm background, contrast between fading sparks and one steady warm flame

## Slide 2 — Two Friends
- **Visual:** Two young writers at desks — one wildly energetic with papers flying, one calm writing a single page
- **Text overlay:** "Aarav: mood par likhta | Kabir: roz ek panna"
- **Style:** Warm cozy study tones, split composition

## Slide 3 — One Year Later
- **Visual:** Scattered loose pages on one side, a complete finished book on the other
- **Text overlay:** "Saal baad: bikhre panne vs ek poori kitaab"
- **Style:** Soft golden light, clear before/after contrast

## Slide 4 — Motivation vs Consistency
- **Visual:** Ocean waves (changing, restless) beside a steady lighthouse standing firm
- **Text overlay:** "Motivation = mausam | Consistency = decision"
- **Style:** Calm sea-blue tones, steady and reassuring

## Slide 5 — The Two-Minute Rule
- **Visual:** A small glowing 2-minute timer beside an open book showing just one page
- **Text overlay:** "Aadat itni chhoti banao | ki na karna mushkil ho"
- **Style:** Minimal, soft amber glow, approachable

## Slide 6 — Don't Break the Chain
- **Visual:** A wall calendar with a chain of warm checkmarks across the days
- **Text overlay:** "Streak banao | Kabhi 2 din lagataar mat chhodo"
- **Style:** Clean, warm, satisfying chain of marks

## Slide 7 — The Power of Compounding
- **Visual:** A bamboo plant with deep underground roots, then shooting up tall in year five
- **Text overlay:** "1% roz behtar = 37x saal mein | Jadein andar ban rahi hain"
- **Style:** Lush green and earth tones, hopeful upward growth

## Slide 8 — Design Your Environment
- **Visual:** Running shoes and clothes laid out ready at night; a guitar resting in plain sight
- **Text overlay:** "Environment > willpower | Kaam ko aasaan banao"
- **Style:** Cozy, practical, warm evening light

## Slide 9 — Be Patient, Not Perfect
- **Visual:** A runner who has stumbled getting back up and continuing down a sunlit track
- **Text overlay:** "Girna haar nahi | Chhod dena haar hai"
- **Style:** Determined, warm dawn light, motivating

## Slide 10 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: a small action arrow, a 2-minute timer, a chain of checkmarks
- **Text overlay:** "1) Action lo  2) 2-minute rule  3) 2 din mat chhodo"

## Slide 11 — Closing / CTA

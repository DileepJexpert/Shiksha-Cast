# WS18 — Time Ki Asli Value — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_011.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A glowing hourglass on a wooden table, golden sand falling, soft sunrise light behind. Warm amber-to-cream gradient background.
- **Text overlay:** "Time Ki Value"
- **Style:** Large elegant serif title, centered, gentle glow. Katixo KhojLab logo bottom-right.

---
## Slide 2 — The Rich Seth
- **Visual:** A wealthy man sitting alone in a big empty mansion, a clock on the wall, dim warm lamp light, slightly melancholic mood.
- **Text overlay:** "Sab kuch tha, par time nahi"
- **Style:** Cinematic shadows, muted gold tones, small overlay text top-left.

---
## Slide 3 — The Realisation
- **Visual:** Same man near a window looking at a sunrise he never noticed before, single tear, soft orange light on his face.
- **Text overlay:** "Kaash kisi ne pehle bataya hota"
- **Style:** Close-up, emotional, warm rim light. Minimal text bottom.

---
## Slide 4 — Lesson 1: Equal 24 Hours
- **Visual:** Two silhouettes — one rich, one simple — standing under the same large clock showing 24, balanced scale feel.
- **Text overlay:** "Sabko milte hain 24 ghante"
- **Style:** Symmetrical composition, soft teal-gold gradient, clean type.

---
## Slide 5 — Lesson 2: Every Moment a Choice
- **Visual:** A fork-in-the-road path at dawn, one path glowing toward a mountain (dreams), one fading into a phone glow.
- **Text overlay:** "Har pal ek choice hai"
- **Style:** Wide cinematic landscape, depth of field, subtle text center-bottom.

---
## Slide 6 — Technique: Time-Blocking
- **Visual:** A clean planner/notebook with three colored time blocks for the day, warm desk light, pen resting.
- **Text overlay:** "Din ko blocks me baanto"
- **Style:** Flat-lay top view, cozy warm palette, neat handwriting feel.

---
## Slide 7 — Technique: 2-Minute & 5-Minute Rule
- **Visual:** A small stopwatch at "2:00" glowing, a person taking the first step up a staircase of light.
- **Text overlay:** "Bas 5 minute shuru karo"
- **Style:** Motion energy, upward composition, bright hopeful gold.

---
## Slide 8 — Time Thieves
- **Visual:** Shadowy hands subtly pulling sand from an hourglass — phone, gossip, comparison icons faint in the shadows.
- **Text overlay:** "Time chor pehchaano"
- **Style:** Darker tone, contrast, single hourglass focal point.

---
## Slide 9 — Balance
- **Visual:** A family laughing together at golden hour, warm and relaxed, soft bokeh background.
- **Text overlay:** "Aaram bhi time ka best use hai"
- **Style:** Warm, human, soft focus, gentle joy.

---
## Slide 10 — Recap: 3 Lessons
- **Visual:** Three glowing icons in a row — hourglass, fork-path, planner — on a calm gradient.
- **Text overlay:** "3 Sabak: Respect • Choice • Plan"
- **Style:** Clean infographic feel, balanced spacing, soft warm background.

---
## Slide 11 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** Sunrise over open horizon, an open hand holding a small glowing light (today = a gift).
- **Text overlay:** "Aaj ek tohfa hai — ise jiyo"
- **Style:** Uplifting, expansive, warm. Katixo KhojLab logo + "Subscribe" prompt bottom-center.

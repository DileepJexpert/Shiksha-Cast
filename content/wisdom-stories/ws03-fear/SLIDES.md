# WS03 — Dar Ko Kaise Jeetein? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_011.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A silhouette standing at the edge of a cliff/dark doorway, hesitant, soft light beyond
- **Text overlay:** "Dar Ko Kaise Jeetein?"
- **Style:** Dark calm background, soft golden glow beyond the threshold

## Slide 2 — The Boy by the River
- **Visual:** A young boy standing at a riverbank, staring at the water, an old fisherman watching
- **Text overlay:** "Roz aata hai, par paani mein nahi utarta"
- **Style:** Warm earthy tones, soft morning river light

## Slide 3 — Courage Isn't No Fear
- **Visual:** The fisherman speaking gently to the boy, calm and wise
- **Text overlay:** "Himmat = darte hue bhi kadam uthaana"
- **Style:** Peaceful, golden hour, intimate

## Slide 4 — Fear Is a Signal
- **Visual:** A glowing arrow pointing through a fear-cloud toward a bright path
- **Text overlay:** "Dar ek signal hai, deewar nahi"
- **Style:** Symbolic, soft blue with a warm path of light

## Slide 5 — Break It Into Steps
- **Visual:** The boy stepping in slowly — feet, knees, waist — a gentle progression
- **Text overlay:** "Bada dar = chhote kadam"
- **Style:** Hopeful, gradual, soft water tones

## Slide 6 — Name the Worst Case
- **Visual:** Hands writing on paper: "Sabse bura kya?" with a plan beside it
- **Text overlay:** "Worst case likho | Phir plan banao"
- **Style:** Cozy desk light, calming and grounded

## Slide 7 — Regret Is the Bigger Fear
- **Visual:** An old hand reaching back toward a faded, un-taken path
- **Text overlay:** "Dar ek pal ka, pachhtaava saalon ka"
- **Style:** Soft, emotional, muted warm tones

## Slide 8 — Change Your Body
- **Visual:** A figure straightening up — shoulders back, deep breath, light filling chest
- **Text overlay:** "Posture badlo | Saans dheemi karo"
- **Style:** Serene, warm glow, empowering

## Slide 9 — The First Step
- **Visual:** The boy taking a real step into the river, fear present but no longer in charge
- **Text overlay:** "Dar tha, par ab woh maalik nahi"
- **Style:** Triumphant soft light, movement and relief

## Slide 10 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: an arrow-signal, a small stair-step, a written plan
- **Text overlay:** "1) Signal hai  2) Chhote kadam  3) Plan banao"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, three-panel layout on dark calm bg

## Slide 11 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** The boy now swimming confidently, sunrise over the river
- **Text overlay:** "Khoobsurti dar ke us paar hai | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful sunrise, uplifting

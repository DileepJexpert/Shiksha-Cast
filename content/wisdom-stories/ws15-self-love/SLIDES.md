# WS15 — Khud Se Pyaar: Self-Love Aur Self-Respect — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_011.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person gently placing a hand over their own heart, soft warm light glowing from within
- **Text overlay:** "Khud Se Pyaar — Self-Love & Self-Respect"
- **Style:** Dark calm background, tender golden glow centered on the chest

## Slide 2 — The Forgotten Plant
- **Visual:** A girl watering a row of blooming flowers, one wilting plant in the middle left dry
- **Text overlay:** "Sabko paani, par ek paudha sookh raha"
- **Style:** Warm garden tones, soft morning light

## Slide 3 — That Plant Is You
- **Visual:** Grandmother gently pointing at the wilting middle plant; the girl realizing with soft tears
- **Text overlay:** "Wo beech wala paudha tum ho | Khaali bartan se kuch nahi dhalta"
- **Style:** Gentle, emotional, warm earthy palette

## Slide 4 — Self-Love Is Not Ego
- **Visual:** A person hugging themselves kindly, with a small reflection accepting their flaws
- **Text overlay:** "Self-love = ego nahi | Khud ko accept karo"
- **Style:** Soft, warm, reassuring tones

## Slide 5 — Watch Your Self-Talk
- **Visual:** Two speech bubbles over a head — one harsh and dark crossed out, one kind and glowing
- **Text overlay:** "Khud se aise baat karo | jaise apne best friend se"
- **Style:** Minimal, clean, soft contrast between dark and warm bubble

## Slide 6 — Self-Care in Action
- **Visual:** Small calm icons of healthy food, sleep, a cup of tea, and a gentle 'No' sign
- **Text overlay:** "Self-love sirf feeling nahi, action hai"
- **Style:** Cozy, warm, gentle daily-life mood

## Slide 7 — Self-Respect
- **Visual:** A person standing tall and calm, gently declining a hand that wants to pull them down
- **Text overlay:** "Apni value jaano | Kam par settle mat karo"
- **Style:** Confident, dignified, warm steady light

## Slide 8 — Stop Comparing
- **Visual:** A rose and a lotus blooming side by side at different moments, both beautiful
- **Text overlay:** "Har phool apne waqt par khilta hai"
- **Style:** Serene, two soft blooms, airy and spacious

## Slide 9 — Three Good Things
- **Visual:** A person at night writing three small glowing stars in a journal
- **Text overlay:** "Roz raat apni 3 achhi baatein yaad karo"
- **Style:** Calm night tones, warm lamp glow, peaceful

## Slide 10 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: a watered plant, a kind speech bubble, a single unique blooming flower
- **Text overlay:** "1) Khud ko paani do  2) Self-talk badlo  3) Tulna mat karo"
- **Style:** Clean three-panel layout on warm dark background

## Slide 11 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** A person standing in soft sunrise light, fully at peace with themselves, the once-wilting plant now blooming beside them
- **Text overlay:** "Tum jaise ho, kaafi ho | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful sunrise, gentle and uplifting

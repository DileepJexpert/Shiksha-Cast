# WS08 — Self-Confidence Kaise Badhayein? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_010.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person about to speak up, then hesitating with a worried face as others look on
- **Text overlay:** "Self-CONFIDENCE Kaise Badhayein?"
- **Style:** Warm dark background, soft golden glow, encouraging mood

## Slide 2 — The Shy Boy Arjun
- **Visual:** A nervous student sitting in class, hand half-raised then pulled back
- **Text overlay:** "Jawab aata tha | Par haath nahi uthaata"
- **Style:** Soft classroom tones, gentle and relatable

## Slide 3 — The Sky Didn't Fall
- **Visual:** The boy raising his hand, teacher smiling warmly, no one laughing
- **Text overlay:** "Jawab galat… par aasmaan nahi gira | Koi nahi hansa"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful light, a moment of relief

## Slide 4 — Action Comes First
- **Visual:** A person taking a step forward; courage glowing AFTER the step, not before
- **Text overlay:** "Pehle action, phir himmat | Confidence inaam hai"
- **Style:** Symbolic, soft motion, uplifting golden trail

## Slide 5 — Talk to Yourself Kindly
- **Visual:** A person looking in a mirror, the reflection gently encouraging them
- **Text overlay:** "Inner voice ko dost banao | Main seekh raha hoon"
- **Style:** Soft warm reflection, compassionate tone

## Slide 6 — Small Wins, Brick by Brick
- **Visual:** A wall being built brick by brick, each brick labeled as a tiny win
- **Text overlay:** "Choti jeetein jodo | Eent eent se deewar"
- **Style:** Steady, grounded earthy tones, sense of building

## Slide 7 — Body Language
- **Visual:** A figure standing tall, shoulders back, head high, calm confident smile
- **Text overlay:** "Seedhi posture, gehri saans | Bahar se andar tak"
- **Style:** Clean, strong, warm light, empowering

## Slide 8 — Accept Yourself Fully
- **Visual:** A person calmly standing whole, embracing both their strengths and flaws as one glow
- **Text overlay:** "Apni kamiyon ko apnao | Yahi asli confidence"
- **Style:** Serene, warm, self-accepting and grounded

## Slide 9 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: a forward step, a kind mirror, a brick wall rising
- **Text overlay:** "1) Action lo  2) Khud se pyaar  3) Choti jeetein"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, three-panel layout on dark calm bg

## Slide 10 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** The once-shy boy now standing confidently at sunrise, head held high
- **Text overlay:** "Tum zyada capable ho | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful sunrise, uplifting and cinematic

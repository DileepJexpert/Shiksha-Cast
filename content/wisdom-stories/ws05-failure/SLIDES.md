# WS05 — Failure Se Kaise Seekhein? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_011.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A broken clay pot on the ground with a single new pot glowing whole beside it
- **Text overlay:** "Failure Se Kaise Seekhein?"
- **Style:** Dark calm background, soft golden glow, gentle and inviting

## Slide 2 — The Discouraged Apprentice
- **Visual:** A young apprentice slumped beside a wobbly broken pot, an old potter watching kindly
- **Text overlay:** "Shayad yeh kaam mere bas ka nahi"
- **Style:** Warm earthy tones, soft workshop light

## Slide 3 — The Pile of Broken Pots
- **Visual:** A large heap of broken clay pots behind a hut, the potter gesturing to it
- **Text overlay:** "Har toota bartan mera guru tha"
- **Style:** Soft golden dusk, meaningful and warm

## Slide 4 — Result, Not Identity
- **Visual:** Two contrasting labels: "Main fail hua" (an event) vs "Main failure hoon" (crossed out)
- **Text overlay:** "Failure ek nateeja hai, pehchaan nahi"
- **Style:** Clean, symbolic, soft contrast

## Slide 5 — Three Questions
- **Visual:** Three glowing question marks rising from a broken pot, turning into a map
- **Text overlay:** "Kya hua? | Mere control mein kya? | Agli baar kya alag?"
- **Style:** Reflective, soft blue-gold, thoughtful

## Slide 6 — Falling Isn't Failing
- **Visual:** A toddler falling and getting back up, repeated steps of rising
- **Text overlay:** "Girna nahi, girkar na uthna = failure"
- **Style:** Tender, hopeful, warm light

## Slide 7 — Fail Small, Fail Fast
- **Visual:** Many tiny experiments/pots, small cracks, quick learning — not one giant gamble
- **Text overlay:** "Chhote experiments | Jaldi seekho"
- **Style:** Energetic but calm, light tones

## Slide 8 — Don't Hide Your Scars
- **Visual:** A repaired pot with golden seams (kintsugi style), proudly displayed
- **Text overlay:** "Tumhare scars hi tumhari kahani hain"
- **Style:** Beautiful golden-seam imagery, dignified

## Slide 9 — The Master Potter
- **Visual:** The apprentice, now a master, smiling at his own pile of broken pots behind him
- **Text overlay:** "Har toota bartan ek behtar kumhaar banata hai"
- **Style:** Triumphant soft sunrise, fulfilling

## Slide 10 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: an event tag, three question marks, a small fast arrow
- **Text overlay:** "1) Nateeja hai  2) 3 sawaal  3) Fail fast"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, three-panel layout on dark calm bg

## Slide 11 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** Sunrise over a workshop, a row of beautiful finished pots on a shelf
- **Text overlay:** "Kamyabi toote bartano ke upar khadi hai | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful sunrise, uplifting

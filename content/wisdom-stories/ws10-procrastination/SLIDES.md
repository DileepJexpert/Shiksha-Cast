# WS10 — Aalas Aur Procrastination Kaise Khatam Karein? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_011.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A young person staring at a laptop and a to-do list, clock ticking, time blurring in the background
- **Text overlay:** "Aalas Aur PROCRASTINATION Kaise Khatam Karein?"
- **Style:** Dark calm background, soft golden glow, gentle and inviting

## Slide 2 — The Big Stone
- **Visual:** A boy looking at a huge stone blocking a village path, an old farmer approaching
- **Text overlay:** "Kaam bada nahi | Bhaari hai shuru se pehle ki soch"
- **Style:** Warm earthy tones, rural morning light

## Slide 3 — One Push
- **Visual:** The boy giving small pushes to the stone; it slowly rolls aside
- **Text overlay:** "Bas ek dhakka | Phir ek aur"
- **Style:** Dynamic, hopeful, dust and warm sun

## Slide 4 — It's Fear, Not Laziness
- **Visual:** A small person standing before a giant shadowy mountain-shaped task
- **Text overlay:** "Aalas nahi, DAR hai | Kaam bada dikhta hai"
- **Style:** Symbolic, soft blue-grey tones

## Slide 5 — The Two Minute Rule
- **Visual:** A stopwatch showing 2:00, a hand opening just one page of a book
- **Text overlay:** "Sirf 2 minute karo | Shuru karna sabse mushkil"
- **Style:** Clean, focused, warm spotlight

## Slide 6 — Break It Down
- **Visual:** A large boulder breaking into small, easy-to-carry pebbles forming steps
- **Text overlay:** "Bada kaam tukdo mein todo | Chhoti jeet = motivation"
- **Style:** Illustrative, soft greens and gold

## Slide 7 — Done Beats Perfect
- **Visual:** A half-finished sketch glowing brighter than a blank perfect blueprint
- **Text overlay:** "Done is better than perfect | Pehle raaste pe aao"
- **Style:** Encouraging, warm creative tones

## Slide 8 — Design Your Environment
- **Visual:** A phone placed in another room; running shoes ready by the bed
- **Text overlay:** "Setup tumhe badalta hai | Acche kaam ko aasaan banao"
- **Style:** Cozy, grounded, soft dawn light

## Slide 9 — Action Before Mood
- **Visual:** The boy and farmer talking; an arrow showing Action leading to Motivation
- **Text overlay:** "Mood ka intezaar mat karo | Pehle action, phir motivation"
- **Style:** Warm, wise, gentle contrast

## Slide 10 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: a 2-minute timer, a broken-into-steps stairway, a forward arrow
- **Text overlay:** "1) 2 min shuru  2) Tukdo mein todo  3) Pehle action"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, three-panel layout on dark calm bg

## Slide 11 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** The cleared path leading toward a sunrise; the boy walking forward confidently
- **Text overlay:** "Tum aalsi nahi ho | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful sunrise, uplifting

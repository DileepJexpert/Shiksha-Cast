# WS20 — Asli Khushi Kahan Milti Hai — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_011.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person holding a shiny new object yet looking empty, soft glow fading around them, warm dusk gradient.
- **Text overlay:** "Asli Khushi Kahan Milti Hai?"
- **Style:** Elegant serif title centered, gentle glow. Katixo KhojLab logo bottom-right.

---
## Slide 2 — The Sad King
- **Visual:** A richly dressed king alone on a throne in a vast palace, head in hand, cold blue shadows despite gold.
- **Text overlay:** "Sab kuch tha, par sukoon nahi"
- **Style:** Grand but lonely, contrast of gold and cool shadow.

---
## Slide 3 — The Happy Farmer
- **Visual:** A bare-chested farmer singing joyfully in a sunlit green field, arms open, genuine wide smile.
- **Text overlay:** "Sabse khush — par kameez hi nahi"
- **Style:** Bright daylight, vivid greens and warm gold, pure joy.

---
## Slide 4 — Lesson 1: Happiness is a State
- **Visual:** A glowing heart-shaped light inside a simple silhouette, not in the objects around them.
- **Text overlay:** "Khushi cheezon me nahi, mann me hai"
- **Style:** Symbolic, soft inner glow, minimal warm background.

---
## Slide 5 — Lesson 2: Hedonic Treadmill
- **Visual:** A person running on an endless treadmill chasing a floating carrot of "more", never reaching it.
- **Text overlay:** "Ye daud kabhi khatm nahi hoti"
- **Style:** Slightly surreal, muted tones, sense of motion and futility.

---
## Slide 6 — Technique: Gratitude
- **Visual:** Hands writing three lines in a journal by warm window light, a steaming cup beside.
- **Text overlay:** "Roz 3 cheezon ka shukriya"
- **Style:** Cozy, warm morning light, intimate close-up.

---
## Slide 7 — Technique: Give Happiness
- **Visual:** A person sharing food/helping a stranger, both smiling, soft golden glow connecting them.
- **Text overlay:** "Dene wale zyada khush rehte hain"
- **Style:** Human, heartwarming, soft bokeh background.

---
## Slide 8 — Comparison Steals Joy
- **Visual:** A person looking at glowing phone screens of others' "perfect" lives, their own light dimming.
- **Text overlay:** "Comparison khushi ka chor hai"
- **Style:** Darker contrast, blue screen glow vs warm self-light.

---
## Slide 9 — Live in the Present
- **Visual:** A calm person savoring a simple moment — tea, sunlight, eyes gently closed, fully present.
- **Text overlay:** "Isi pal me jeena seekho"
- **Style:** Serene, soft warm light, shallow focus, peaceful.

---
## Slide 10 — Recap: 3 Lessons
- **Visual:** Three glowing icons — heart, gratitude journal, present-moment sun — on a calm gradient.
- **Text overlay:** "3 Sabak: Santosh • Gratitude/Give • Present"
- **Style:** Clean infographic feel, balanced spacing.

---
## Slide 11 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** A small glowing flower blooming inside an open palm at sunrise, warm and hopeful.
- **Text overlay:** "Khushi ki chaabi tumhare haath me"
- **Style:** Uplifting, gentle sunrise. Katixo KhojLab logo + "Subscribe" prompt bottom-center.

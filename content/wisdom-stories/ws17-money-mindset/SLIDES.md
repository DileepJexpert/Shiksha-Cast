# WS17 — Paisa Aur Mindset: Money Ke Baare Mein Soch — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_011.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A coin being planted in soil like a seed, with a tiny golden sprout rising from it
- **Text overlay:** "Paisa Aur Mindset — Money Ke Baare Mein Soch"
- **Style:** Dark calm background, warm golden glow on the sprouting coin

## Slide 2 — Two Brothers
- **Visual:** Two brothers — one buying flashy items, the other quietly placing coins into a small growing plant pot
- **Text overlay:** "Rohan: paisa udao | Mohan: paisa ek beej hai"
- **Style:** Warm village tones, split composition

## Slide 3 — Ten Years Later
- **Visual:** One brother stressed under a pile of bills, the other calm beside a tree grown from coins
- **Text overlay:** "Income nahi, habits ameer banati hain"
- **Style:** Soft golden light, clear contrast of stress vs peace

## Slide 4 — Pay Yourself First
- **Visual:** Money flowing — a portion diverted into a savings jar before the rest goes to expenses
- **Text overlay:** "Paisa naukar banao, maalik nahi | Pehle khud ko pay karo"
- **Style:** Clean, warm, calm financial flow imagery

## Slide 5 — Needs vs Wants
- **Visual:** A balance scale: bread, shelter on one side; a shiny gadget on the other
- **Text overlay:** "Need vs Want | 24 ghante ruko, phir kharido"
- **Style:** Minimal, soft amber, thoughtful mood

## Slide 6 — Make a Budget (50-30-20)
- **Visual:** A pie chart glowing softly — 50% needs, 30% wants, 20% savings
- **Text overlay:** "Har paise ko ek kaam do | 50-30-20 rule"
- **Style:** Clean, warm, simple infographic feel

## Slide 7 — Invest in Yourself
- **Visual:** A person learning a new skill, a small glowing brain/lightbulb growing into a money tree
- **Text overlay:** "Sabse bada asset = teri skill | Khud par invest karo"
- **Style:** Hopeful, warm light, upward growth

## Slide 8 — Avoid the Show-Off Trap
- **Visual:** A flashy car on the outside cracking to reveal an empty wallet; a simple calm person with a full jar
- **Text overlay:** "Jo dikhta hai wo kharcha | Jo nahi dikhta wo daulat"
- **Style:** Subtle, warm, quietly wise contrast

## Slide 9 — Gratitude and Giving
- **Visual:** Open hands gently passing a coin onward, water-like flow, warm and abundant
- **Text overlay:** "Paisa behta paani hai | Gratitude se kami ka dar mitta hai"
- **Style:** Warm, generous, peaceful golden tones

## Slide 10 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: a savings jar, a balance scale, a person with a lightbulb
- **Text overlay:** "1) Khud ko pay karo  2) Need vs want  3) Khud par invest"
- **Style:** Clean three-panel layout on warm dark background

## Slide 11 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** A calm person at sunrise beside a healthy money tree, peaceful and secure
- **Text overlay:** "Paisa bhi ho, sukoon bhi | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful sunrise, balanced and uplifting

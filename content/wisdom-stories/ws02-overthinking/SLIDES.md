# WS02 — Overthinking Se Kaise Bachein? — Slide Design Brief

**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_011.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A person lying awake at night, tangled glowing thought-threads looping around their head
- **Text overlay:** "Overthinking Se Kaise Bachein?"
- **Style:** Dark calm background, soft golden glow, gentle and inviting

## Slide 2 — The Worried Farmer
- **Visual:** A tired farmer sitting at the edge of his field at dusk, looking anxious
- **Text overlay:** "Khet mein kam, dimaag mein zyada hal chala raha hai"
- **Style:** Warm earthy tones, soft evening light

## Slide 3 — 90% Never Happens
- **Visual:** A wall of worry-clouds, most fading away; only one or two turning real
- **Text overlay:** "90% chintaayein kabhi sach nahi hotin"
- **Style:** Soft blue-grey, fading clouds, airy

## Slide 4 — Thinking vs Overthinking
- **Visual:** Split screen — one side a straight arrow (solution), other side a spinning circle (loop)
- **Text overlay:** "Solve kar rahe ho ya bas ghoom rahe ho?"
- **Style:** Clean, symbolic, two-tone contrast

## Slide 5 — Action Beats Worry
- **Visual:** The farmer standing up, taking one small step toward his field
- **Text overlay:** "Kuch kar sakte ho? Karo. Nahi? Jaane do"
- **Style:** Hopeful golden light, forward motion

## Slide 6 — Worry Time
- **Visual:** A calm clock showing 5 o'clock, a small notebook beside it
- **Text overlay:** "Sirf 15 minute | Tera time 5 baje hai"
- **Style:** Warm, grounded, soft focus

## Slide 7 — Write It Down
- **Visual:** Hands writing in a diary, tangled thoughts flowing out onto the page and untangling
- **Text overlay:** "Dimaag se nikaalo, kaagaz par"
- **Style:** Cozy desk light, calming

## Slide 8 — Done Beats Perfect
- **Visual:** A half-built bridge being walked on vs a perfect blueprint sitting unused
- **Text overlay:** "Done is better than perfect"
- **Style:** Soft motivational tones, sense of movement

## Slide 9 — Just Notice It
- **Visual:** A person gently catching a thought-bubble, smiling, aware
- **Text overlay:** "Pehchaan lo — yeh fir shuru ho gaya"
- **Style:** Serene, warm glow, slow mood

## Slide 10 — Recap (3 Lessons)
- **Visual:** Three soft icons: a straight arrow, a hand taking action, a diary
- **Text overlay:** "1) Solve ya ghoom?  2) Action lo  3) Likh do"
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, three-panel layout on dark calm bg

## Slide 11 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** Sunrise over a calm field, the farmer standing relaxed, light-hearted
- **Text overlay:** "Sirf is pal mein jiyo | Subscribe — Katixo KhojLab"
- **Style:** Warm hopeful sunrise, uplifting

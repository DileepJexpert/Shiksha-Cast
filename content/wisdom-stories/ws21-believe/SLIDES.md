# WS21 — Apne Aap Par Vishwas — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_011.png`
**Brand:** Katixo KhojLab | **Style:** Calm, cinematic, soft warm gradients, minimal text

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A silhouette standing at the edge of a cliff at sunrise, ready to leap, faint inner glow rising.
- **Text overlay:** "Apne Aap Par Vishwas"
- **Style:** Elegant serif title centered, gentle glow. Katixo KhojLab logo bottom-right.

---
## Slide 2 — The Tied Elephant
- **Visual:** A huge elephant tied by a thin rope to a small stake, standing calmly, circus tent warm light.
- **Text overlay:** "Itni patli rassi, phir bhi bandha"
- **Style:** Cinematic, warm dusk, gentle irony in the framing.

---
## Slide 3 — The Old Belief
- **Visual:** Flashback split — a small baby elephant struggling against the rope vs the grown one not even trying.
- **Text overlay:** "Mann me purana vishwas baitha hai"
- **Style:** Two-tone memory feel, faded sepia for past, warm for present.

---
## Slide 4 — Lesson 1: The Rope is in Your Mind
- **Visual:** A glowing rope wrapped around a person's head/heart rather than their body.
- **Text overlay:** "Asli rassi mann me hai"
- **Style:** Symbolic, soft glow, minimal warm background.

---
## Slide 5 — Lesson 2: Action Builds Confidence
- **Visual:** A person taking the first step up a glowing staircase, each step lighting up after they climb.
- **Text overlay:** "Confidence action se aata hai"
- **Style:** Upward energy, hopeful gold light, sense of momentum.

---
## Slide 6 — Technique: Small Wins
- **Visual:** A jar slowly filling with small glowing stars/marbles, each a tiny victory.
- **Text overlay:** "Chhoti jeetein ikatthi karo"
- **Style:** Cozy, warm, satisfying visual of accumulation.

---
## Slide 7 — Technique: Change Self-Talk
- **Visual:** A person looking in a mirror, the reflection smiling encouragingly back at them.
- **Text overlay:** "Khud se dost jaisi baat karo"
- **Style:** Intimate, warm mirror light, gentle and kind.

---
## Slide 8 — Don't Let Others Limit You
- **Visual:** A figure walking forward through a wall of doubting shadow-voices, light ahead.
- **Text overlay:** "Doosron ki raay teri seema nahi"
- **Style:** Contrast of dark voices vs bright path forward.

---
## Slide 9 — Remember Past Wins
- **Visual:** A montage glow behind a person — learning to walk, cycling, passing an exam — warm memories.
- **Text overlay:** "Tum pehle bhi jeet chuke ho"
- **Style:** Warm nostalgic bokeh, uplifting.

---
## Slide 10 — Recap: 3 Lessons
- **Visual:** Three glowing icons — broken rope, climbing steps, mirror smile — on a calm gradient.
- **Text overlay:** "3 Sabak: Beliefs todo • Action • Self-talk"
- **Style:** Clean infographic feel, balanced spacing.

---
## Slide 11 — Closing / CTA
- **Visual:** The elephant snapping the rope effortlessly and walking free toward a bright sunrise.
- **Text overlay:** "Rassi todo, aage badho"
- **Style:** Triumphant, expansive sunrise. Katixo KhojLab logo + "Subscribe" prompt bottom-center.

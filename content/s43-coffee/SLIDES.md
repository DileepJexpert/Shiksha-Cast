# S43 — Why Does Coffee Wake You Up? — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_013.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A sleepy student slumped over books, then a steaming coffee cup and the brain lighting up bright.
- **Text overlay:** "Why Does Coffee WAKE You Up?"
- **Style:** Big neon-cyan title, dark backdrop, sleepy-to-alert glow transition.

## Slide 2 — Adenosine = Sleep Chemical
- **Visual:** A brain with adenosine molecules accumulating over a day-clock; molecules docking into receptor slots.
- **Text overlay:** "Adenosine = your sleep timer"
- **Style:** Glowing brain, rising molecule counter, neon receptor docks.

## Slide 3 — Caffeine the Fake Key
- **Visual:** Caffeine molecule shaped like a key fitting into the same receptor lock as adenosine; adenosine left out.
- **Text overlay:** "Caffeine = fake key, blocks adenosine"
- **Style:** Lock-and-key metaphor, neon-green caffeine vs blue adenosine.

## Slide 4 — Signal Hidden
- **Visual:** Receptors all filled with caffeine; the "sleepy" signal arrow crossed out; floating leftover adenosine.
- **Text overlay:** "Tiredness hidden, NOT removed"
- **Style:** Red X over sleep signal, neon highlight on blocked receptors.

## Slide 5 — Adrenaline & Dopamine Surge
- **Visual:** Brain releasing dopamine and adrenaline; heart icon beating faster, focus rays sharpening.
- **Text overlay:** "Adrenaline up = alert + faster heartbeat"
- **Style:** Energetic neon-orange surge, pulsing heart, focus burst lines.

## Slide 6 — How Fast It Works
- **Visual:** Coffee cup → stomach absorption → bloodstream → brain crossing the blood-brain barrier; 15-45 min timer.
- **Text overlay:** "Effect starts in 15-45 minutes"
- **Style:** Glowing journey path, neon timer, body-cross-section style.

## Slide 7 — Half-Life
- **Visual:** A bar of 100mg caffeine halving to 50mg after 5-6 hours; a moon icon warning against evening coffee.
- **Text overlay:** "Half-life ~5-6 hrs → no evening coffee"
- **Style:** Decay bar graph, neon clock, night-sky warning panel.

## Slide 8 — The Caffeine Crash
- **Visual:** Caffeine leaving receptors; a flood of stored adenosine rushing in; sudden sleepy face. "Energy loan" tag.
- **Text overlay:** "Crash = energy on loan, paid back later"
- **Style:** Wave of blue molecules, neon crash effect, loan metaphor icon.

## Slide 9 — Tolerance & Withdrawal
- **Visual:** Brain growing extra receptors over days; a headache icon for quitting. Up-arrow on cups needed.
- **Text overlay:** "Daily use → tolerance + withdrawal"
- **Style:** Multiplying receptor slots, neon headache flash, dark theme.

## Slide 10 — Caffeine Numbers
- **Visual:** Comparison chart — coffee ~80-100mg, tea ~30-50mg, cola ~30-40mg; teen limit gauge under 100mg.
- **Text overlay:** "Teens: keep under ~100mg/day"
- **Style:** Clean neon bar chart, drink icons, healthy-energy reminder.

## Slide 11 — QUIZ
- **Visual:** Glowing brain icon with three numbered coffee-themed question cards. Pause symbol.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! | 1·2·3 → PAUSE"
- **Style:** Neon-yellow energetic accents, countdown vibe, dark backdrop.

## Slide 12 — ANSWERS
- **Visual:** Three answer cards with green ticks, mini-icons echoing Slides 2, 3, 8.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: Adenosine · Fake Key · Adenosine Flood"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, clean reveal layout, dark theme.

## Slide 13 — RECAP / Next Episode
- **Visual:** Recap montage of brain + caffeine-key icons; teaser of a tilted Earth orbiting the Sun for next episode; glowing Subscribe button.
- **Text overlay:** "Next: Why Do We Have Seasons? | SUBSCRIBE"
- **Style:** Bold Katixo Shiksha logo, neon Subscribe CTA, upbeat dark finish.

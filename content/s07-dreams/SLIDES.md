# S07 — Why Do We Dream — Slide Design Brief
**Format:** 1920×1080 PNG | **Export:** `slides/slide_001.png` to `slide_014.png`
**Brand:** Katixo Shiksha | **Style:** Dark science theme, neon accents, bold text overlays

---
## Slide 1 — HOOK / Title
- **Visual:** A sleeping teen with a glowing dream-cloud above their head showing surreal floating images (flying, stairs, a clock melting).
- **Text overlay:** "DREAMS — Hum Sapne Kyun Dekhte Hain?"
- **Style:** Dreamy neon purple/blue gradient, soft glow title in cyan.

## Slide 2 — Sleep Stages
- **Visual:** A circular 90-minute sleep-cycle wheel split into non-REM and REM segments, with 4–6 cycles shown as a wavy night timeline.
- **Text overlay:** "1 cycle = ~90 min | Raat me 4–6 cycles"
- **Style:** Infographic wheel, neon teal for REM, deep blue for non-REM.

## Slide 3 — REM Sleep
- **Visual:** Close-up of closed eyes with darting motion arrows; a brain glowing brightly beside a paralyzed body outline.
- **Text overlay:** "REM = Rapid Eye Movement | Body paralyzed = REM atonia"
- **Style:** High-contrast, electric cyan brain waves on dark.

## Slide 4 — How Dreams Form
- **Visual:** Brain diagram with three regions highlighted: visual cortex (bright), amygdala (red glow), prefrontal cortex (dimmed).
- **Text overlay:** "Logic OFF, Imagination ON — isliye sapne weird lagte hain"
- **Style:** Labeled brain map, color-coded glow per region.

## Slide 5 — Memory Consolidation
- **Visual:** Brain shown as a filing cabinet/hard-drive; daytime memories sorted into KEEP and DELETE folders at night.
- **Text overlay:** "Dreams = dimaag ki raat ki safai aur filing"
- **Style:** Tech-storage metaphor, green KEEP / red DELETE.

## Slide 6 — Emotional Processing
- **Visual:** A stormy emotion cloud (stress, fear) over a head, calming into soft colors after REM. A therapy-couch icon subtle in corner.
- **Text overlay:** "REM = overnight therapy | Bhavnaon ko process karta hai"
- **Style:** Warm-to-calm gradient, soothing neon.

## Slide 7 — Threat Simulation Theory
- **Visual:** A VR-headset style scene of a person being chased/running, framed as a safe simulation grid.
- **Text overlay:** "Dreams = khatron ki safe practice (Revonsuo)"
- **Style:** VR/grid aesthetic, neon green wireframe.

## Slide 8 — Activation-Synthesis
- **Visual:** Random electrical sparks from the brainstem being woven by the brain into a story ribbon.
- **Text overlay:** "Random signals → brain banata hai kahani (1977)"
- **Style:** Spark-to-narrative flow, magenta and gold.

## Slide 9 — Fun Dream Facts
- **Visual:** Four icon cards — forgetting a dream, color vs B/W TV, a blind person's sensory dream, a lucid-dream control dial.
- **Text overlay:** "Roz sapne aate hain | Lucid dreaming = control!"
- **Style:** 2x2 card grid, playful neon outlines.

## Slide 10 — Nightmares & Night Terrors
- **Visual:** Split panel — left: REM nightmare (remembered story), right: non-REM night terror in a child (no memory).
- **Text overlay:** "Nightmare (REM) vs Night Terror (deep sleep)"
- **Style:** Dark split design, red accents on left, indigo on right.

## Slide 11 — Why Dreams Matter
- **Visual:** A glowing brain with rays labeled memory, emotion, creativity, practice; a lightbulb inspired by a dream.
- **Text overlay:** "Dreams = smarter + emotionally strong brain"
- **Style:** Radiant brain hub, multi-color neon rays.

## Slide 12 — Quick Brain Check
- **Visual:** Big pause icon, three numbered question bubbles, a dreaming brain in the center.
- **Text overlay:** "Quick Brain Check! Video PAUSE karo — 3 sawaal"
- **Style:** Quiz-show vibe, bold yellow numbers on dark.

## Slide 13 — Answers
- **Visual:** Three checkmark cards: REM sleep, REM atonia, two theories.
- **Text overlay:** "Answers: REM | REM Atonia | Memory + Emotion theories"
- **Style:** Green checkmarks, clean answer cards.

## Slide 14 — Recap & Next Episode
- **Visual:** Recap strip of sleep cycle, REM brain, filing icons; teaser card with a swirling black hole for next episode.
- **Text overlay:** "Next: What's Inside a Black Hole? | Subscribe karna mat bhoolna!"
- **Style:** Recap on left, glowing black-hole teaser on right. Katixo Shiksha logo bottom-right.

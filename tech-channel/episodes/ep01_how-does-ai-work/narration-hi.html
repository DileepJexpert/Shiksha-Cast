EP01 — How does AI like ChatGPT actually work?

HINGLISH (Roman)
01Title

ChatGPT jaisa AI actually kaam kaise karta hai? Welcome to Katixo KhojLab. Aap ise almost roz use karte ho — par andar ho kya raha hai? Aaj is box ko kholte hain. No hype, no jargon.

[CUE] Title pe ~2s ruko, calm confident tone, phir aage badho.
02The Hook

Aap type karte ho "I am going ho…" aur aapka phone guess karta hai "home". Yeh chhota sa guess? Yahi hai ChatGPT ka pura raaz — bas ek billion times bada. Paanch minute ruko, ye "magic" poori tarah samajh aa jayega.

[CUE] Yahan energy high — ye hook hai. "guess… home" pe phone-type ka mime karo.
03Aaj ka sawaal

Toh aaj ka ek sawaal: ye decide kaise karta hai ki aage kya bolna hai — aur kya use sach mein kuch pata hota hai, ya bas guess kar raha hai? Chalo pata karte hain.

[CUE] Thoda slow. Sawaal ko land hone do, phir aage.
04Idea 1 — Prediction machine

Idea number one: ChatGPT ek prediction machine hai. Use do "The cat sat on the blank", aur ye kisi database mein answer dhoondhta nahi. Ye bas predict karta hai ki sabse likely next word kya hai. "Mat"? Bahut likely. "Banana"? Almost kabhi nahi. Top wala uthata hai, add karta hai, aur phir se predict karta hai.

[CUE] mat / floor / sofa / banana bolte waqt bars ek-ek karke reveal karo.
05Idea 2 — Trained on text

Par use kaise pata "mat" likely hai aur "banana" nahi? Kyunki usne bahut saara text padha hai — billions of sentences, books, websites, conversations, aur code se. Usne facts ko list ki tarah ratta nahi maara. Usne language ke patterns absorb kiye — jaise koi student jo millions of pages padhke samajh gaya ki sentences kaise flow karte hain.

[CUE] chips (books, websites, code) ko list karte waqt reveal karo.
06Idea 3 — One token at a time

Ab ye part logon ko surprise karta hai: ye answer ek-ek piece karke banata hai. Har chhote piece ko token kehte hain. Ek token predict karo, sentence mein add karo, phir poora wapas daalke agla token predict karo. "The… sky… looks… blue… because…" — ek token at a time, bahut tezi se.

[CUE] token chain left-to-right reveal; steady rhythm mein padho.
07Idea 4 — Context / attention

Ab ye topic pe tika kaise rehta hai? Ye aapke likhe har word ko weigh karta hai. Agar aap kaho "I left my phone in the car, can you help me find it?" — toh ye samajh jaata hai ki "phone" aur "car" sabse zyada matter karte hain, na ki "the" ya "my". Yahi idea — context pe dhyaan dena — wahi hai jisne modern AI ko kaam karwaaya.

[CUE] "phone" aur "car" highlights ko point karo jab bolo.
08Why it matters

Aur yahi sabse khoobsurat baat hai. Ye ek simple idea — next token predict karo — almost kisi bhi cheez pe scale karta hai. Wahi trick essay likhti hai, code debug karti hai, email draft karti hai, sawaal ka jawab deti hai. Par yaad rakho: ye predict kar raha hai, samajh nahi raha.

[CUE] "predict kar raha hai, samajh nahi raha" pe zor — ye myth set karta hai.
09Common myth

Aur isse aata hai sabse bada myth. Log sochte hain ChatGPT ko sach pata hota hai aur wo use "look up" karta hai. Aisa nahi hai. Ye sabse likely-sounding words predict karta hai. Toh ye poore confidence ke saath — bilkul galat bhi ho sakta hai. Ise hallucination kehte hain. Toh koi bhi important cheez hamesha verify karo.

[CUE] Myth pe ek beat ke baad green "Reality" card reveal karo.
10Recap

Quick recap. Ek: ChatGPT jaisa AI bas next-word prediction hai, massive scale pe. Do: ye sochta ya jaanta nahi — patterns se guess karta hai. Toh use karo, par jo facts matter karte hain unhe check karo.

[CUE] Crisp aur clear — ye do lines log yaad rakhein.
11CTA
